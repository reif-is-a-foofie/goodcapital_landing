Statistical Report, 1998 Presented by F. Michael Watson Secretary to the First Presidency Brothers and sisters, for the information of the members of the Church, the First Presidency has issued the following statistical report concerning the membership growth and status of the Church as of 31 December 1998. These statistics are based on 1998 reports available prior to this conference.
