When we come to the sacred page, and review the history of God’s dealings with His own peculiar people, we find there is no subject upon which the Lord has been more particular than this. Why so ? Because Noah was endowed with authority, and clothed with the Priesthood ; ho stood in the place of God unto them, and his words would either prove a savour of life unto life, or of death unto death.
