1 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily, thus saith the Lord, it is expedient in me that they should continue preaching the gospel, and in exhortation to the churches in the regions round about, until conference;
2 And then, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">behold, it shall be made known unto them, by the voice of the conference, their several missions.

6 Now I give no more unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you at this time. Gird up your loins and be sober. Even so. Amen.
