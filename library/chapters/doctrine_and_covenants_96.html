2 Therefore, let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">servant Newel K. Whitney take charge of the place which is named among you, upon which I design to build mine holy house.
3 And again, let it be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">divided into lots, according to wisdom, for the benefit of those who seek inheritances, as it shall be determined in council among you.
4 Therefore, take <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">heed that ye see to this matter, and that portion that is necessary to benefit mine order, for the purpose of bringing forth my word to the children of men.
5 For behold, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">verily I say unto you, this is the most expedient in me, that my word should go forth unto the children of men, for the purpose of subduing the hearts of the children of men for your good. Even so. Amen.
6 And again, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily I say unto you, it is wisdom and expedient in me, that my servant John Johnson whose offering I have accepted, and whose prayers I have heard, unto whom I give a promise of eternal life inasmuch as he keepeth my commandments from henceforth—
7 For he is a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">descendant of Joseph and a partaker of the blessings of the promise made unto his fathers—
8 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Verily I say unto you, it is expedient in me that he should become a member of the order, that he may assist in bringing forth my word unto the children of men.
9 Therefore ye shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">ordain him unto this blessing, and he shall seek diligently to take away incumbrances that are upon the house named among you, that he may dwell therein. Even so. Amen.
