Psalm 86 is a prayer for God's help in the face of trouble and dangerous enemies. Its chiastic configuration accents the themes of God's love, the psalmist's need for God's help, and the praiseworthiness of God. The structurally highlighted central unit is a prayer for guidance and help (David A. Dorsey, The Literary Structure of the Old Testament)
