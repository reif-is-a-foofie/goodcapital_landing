Mosiah 25-28: The youth of Alma2. Mosiah 25:1-13: Mosiah2 addresses his people. Mosiah 25:14-24: Alma1 organizes the church in the land of Zarahemla. Upon his recovery, he testifies of the power of redemption. Mosiah 27:32-37: The great mission of Alma2 and the sons of Mosiah2 begins from this time. Mosiah 28:1-9: The sons of Mosiah2 begin their mission to the Lamanites. Mosiah 28:10-20: Mosiah2 translates the plates in his possession and hands them over to Alma2. Mosiah 29-Alma 4: The judgeship of Alma2. Mosiah 29: Having difficulty appointing a new king, Mosiah2 proposes (and then enacts) a change in government, to a system of judges elected by "the voice of the people." Alma2 becomes the first chief judge. (He is also the chief high priest of the church.) Alma1 and Mosiah2 die. (Mike Tueller)
