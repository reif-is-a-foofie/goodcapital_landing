THE ANNALS OF MOSIAH (CHAPS. 25-29).

Mosiah 25-28: The youth of Alma2. Mosiah 25:1-13: Mosiah2 addresses his people. Mosiah 25:14-24: Alma1 organizes the church in the land of Zarahemla. Mosiah 26:1-36: In response to widespread iniquity, especially among the youth, Alma1, in response to revelation, establishes a judicial function among the priesthood. Only those who repent retain their membership in the church. Mosiah 26:37-27:7: Assessment of the church and people generally. They prosper, though there are outbreaks of persecution of church members, which Mosiah2 rules unlawful. Mosiah 27:8-31: Alma2, son of Alma1, and the sons of Mosiah2 go about encouraging spiritual rebellion. They are rebuked by an angel, and Alma2 is incapacitated. Upon his recovery, he testifies of the power of redemption.
