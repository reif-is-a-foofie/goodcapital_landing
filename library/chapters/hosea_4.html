4

1 HEAR the word of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.898">LORD, ye children of Israel: for the LORD hath a controversy with the inhabitants of the land, because [there is] no truth, nor mercy, nor knowledge of God in the land.
2 By <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">swearing, and lying, and killing, and stealing, and committing adultery, they break out, and blood toucheth blood.
3 Therefore shall the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.965">land mourn, and every one that dwelleth therein shall languish, with the beasts of the field, and with the fowls of heaven; yea, the fishes of the sea also shall be taken away.
4 Yet let no man strive, nor <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">reprove another: for thy people [are] as they that strive with the priest.
5 Therefore shalt thou <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.916">fall in the day, and the prophet also shall fall with thee in the night, and I will destroy thy mother.
6 My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge: because thou hast rejected knowledge, I will also reject thee, that thou shalt be no priest to me: seeing thou hast <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.956">forgotten the law of thy God, I will also forget thy children.

8 They <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.852">eat up the sin of my people, and they set their heart on their iniquity.
9 And there shall be, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">like people, like priest: and I will punish them for their ways, and reward them their doings.
10 For they shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.962">eat, and not have enough: they shall commit whoredom, and shall not increase: because they have left off to take heed to the LORD.
11 Whoredom and <span class="cw" data-tgt="psefariacommentary__kli_yakar_on_genesis_9_20_10.325">wine and new wine take away the heart.
12 My people <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.944">ask counsel at their stocks, and their staff declareth unto them: for the spirit of whoredoms hath caused [them] to err, and they have gone a whoring from under their God.
13 They sacrifice upon the tops of the mountains, and burn incense upon the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.891">hills, under oaks and poplars and elms, because the shadow thereof [is] good: therefore your daughters shall commit whoredom, and your spouses shall commit adultery.
14 I will not punish your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.970">daughters when they commit whoredom, nor your spouses when they commit adultery: for themselves are separated with whores, and they sacrifice with harlots: therefore the people [that] doth not understand shall fall.
