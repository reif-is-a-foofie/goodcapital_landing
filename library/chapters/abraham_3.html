1 AND I, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.260">Abraham, had the Urim and Thummim, which the Lord my God had given unto me, in Ur of the Chaldees;
2 And I saw the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_abraham_3_190.311">stars, that they were very great, and that one of them was nearest unto the throne of God; and there were many great ones which were near unto it;
3 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.336">Lord said unto me: These are the governing ones; and the name of the great one is Kolob, because it is near unto me, for I am the Lord thy God: I have set this one to govern all
4 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_peter_3_80.630">Lord said unto me, by the Urim and Thummim, that Kolob was after the manner of the Lord, according to its times and seasons in the revolutions thereof; that one revolution was a day unto the Lord, after his manner of reckoning, it being one thousand years according to the time appointed unto that whereon thou standest. This is the reckoning of the Lord's time, according to the reckoning of Kolob.
5 And the Lord said unto me: The <span class="cw" data-tgt="ptimes_and_seasonstimes___seasons__times_and_seasons_abbyy0.484">planet which is the lesser light, lesser than that which is to rule the day, even the night, is above or greater than that upon which thou standest in point of reckoning, for it moveth in order more slow; this is in order because it standeth above the earth upon which thou standest, therefore the reckoning of its time is not so many as to its number of days, and of months, and of years.
6 And the Lord said unto me: Now, Abraham, these two facts exist, behold thine eyes see it; it is given unto thee to know the times of reckoning, and the set time, yea, the set time of the earth upon which thou standest, and the set time of the greater <span class="cw" data-tgt="ptimes_and_seasonstimes___seasons__times_and_seasons_abbyy0.566">light which is set to rule the day, and the set time of the lesser light which is set to rule the night.
7 Now the set time of the lesser <span class="cw" data-tgt="ptimes_and_seasonstimes___seasons__times_and_seasons_abbyy0.510">light is a longer time as to its reckoning than the reckoning of the time of the earth upon which thou standest.
8 And where these <span class="cw" data-tgt="ptimes_and_seasonstimes___seasons__times_and_seasons_abbyy0.355">two facts exist, there shall be another fact above them, that is, there shall be another planet whose reckoning of time shall be longer still;
9 And thus there shall be the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.494">reckoning of the time of one planet above another, until thou come nigh unto Kolob, which Kolob is after the reckoning of the Lord's time; which Kolob is set nigh unto the throne of God, to govern all those planets which belong to the same order as that upon which thou standest.
10 And it is given unto thee to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_abraham_3_180.374">know the set time of all the stars that are set to give light, until thou come near unto the throne of God.
11 Thus I, Abraham, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moses_7_40.385">talked with the Lord, face to face, as one man talketh with another; and he told me of the works which his hands had made;
12 And he said unto me: My son, my son (and his <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moses_7_40.356">hand was stretched out), behold I will show you all these. And he put his hand upon mine eyes, and I saw those things which his hands had made, which were many; and they multiplied before mine eyes, and I could not see the end thereof.
13 And he said unto me: This is Shinehah, which is the sun. And he said unto me: Kokob, which is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.351">star. And he said unto me: Olea, which is the moon. And he said unto me: Kokaubeam, which signifies stars, or all the great lights, which were in the firmament of heaven.
14 And it was in the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_genesis_13_160.472">night time when the Lord spake these words unto me: I will multiply thee, and thy seed after thee, like unto these; and if thou canst count the number of sands, so shall be the number of thy seeds.

17 Now, if there be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pbh_robertsb_h__roberts__comprehensive_history_of_church0.398">two things, one above the other, and the moon be above the earth, then it may be that a planet or a star may exist above it; and there is nothing that the Lord thy God shall take in his heart to do but what he will do it.
18 Howbeit that he made the greater <span class="cw" data-tgt="pbh_robertsb_h__roberts__comprehensive_history_of_church0.654">star; as, also, if there be two spirits, and one shall be more intelligent that the other, yet these two spirits, notwithstanding one is more intelligent than the other, have no beginning; they existed before, they shall have no end, they shall exist after, for they are gnolaum, or eternal.
