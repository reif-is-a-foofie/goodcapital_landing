Seraphs are angels who reside in the presence of God, giving continual glory, honor, and adoration to him. "Praise ye him, all his angels: praise ye him, all his hosts." (Ps. 148:2.) It is clear that seraphs include the unembodied spirits of pre-existence, for our Lord "looked upon the wide expanse of eternity, and all the seraphic hosts of heaven, before the world was made."
