Doctrine and Covenants

97

1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Verily I say unto you my friends, I speak unto you with my voice, even the voice of my Spirit, that I may show unto you my will concerning your brethren in the land of Zion, many of whom are truly humble and are seeking diligently to learn wisdom and to find truth.
2 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Verily, verily I say unto you, blessed are such, for they shall obtain; for I, the Lord, show mercy unto all the meek, and upon all whomsoever I will, that I may be justified when I shall bring them unto judgment.
3 Behold, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">say unto you, concerning the school in Zion, I, the Lord, am well pleased that there should be a school in Zion, and also with my servant Parley P. Pratt, for he abideth in me.
4 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">inasmuch as he continueth to abide in me he shall continue to preside over the school in the land of Zion until I shall give unto him other commandments.
5 And I will <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">bless him with a multiplicity of blessings, in expounding all scriptures and mysteries to the edification of the school, and of the church in Zion.
6 And to the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">residue of the school, I, the Lord, am willing to show mercy; nevertheless, there are those that must needs be chastened, and their works shall be made known.
7 The ax is laid at the root of the trees; and every <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">tree that bringeth not forth good fruit shall be hewn down and cast into the fire. I, the Lord, have spoken it.
8 Verily I say unto you, all among them who know their hearts are honest, and are broken, and their spirits contrite, and are <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">willing to observe their covenants by sacrifice—yea, every sacrifice which I, the Lord, shall command—they are accepted of me.
9 For I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Lord, will cause them to bring forth as a very fruitful tree which is planted in a goodly land, by a pure stream, that yieldeth much precious fruit.
10 Verily I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.938">say unto you, that it is my will that a house should be built unto me in the land of Zion, like unto the pattern which I have given you.
11 Yea, let it be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.918">built speedily, by the tithing of my people.
12 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.956">Behold, this is the tithing and the sacrifice which I, the Lord, require at their hands, that there may be a house built unto me for the salvation of Zion—
13 For a place of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.951">thanksgiving for all saints, and for a place of instruction for all those who are called to the work of the ministry in all their several callings and offices;
14 That they may be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.950">perfected in the understanding of their ministry, in theory, in principle, and in doctrine, in all things pertaining to the kingdom of God on the earth, the keys of which kingdom have been conferred upon you.
15 And inasmuch as my people <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.949">build a house unto me in the name of the Lord, and do not suffer any unclean thing to come into it, that it be not defiled, my glory shall rest upon it;
16 Yea, and my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.920">presence shall be there, for I will come into it, and all the pure in heart that shall come into it shall see God.
17 But if it be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.899">defiled I will not come into it, and my glory shall not be there; for I will not come into unholy temples.
18 And, now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.947">behold, if Zion do these things she shall prosper, and spread herself and become very glorious, very great, and very terrible.
19 And the nations of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.960">earth shall honor her, and shall say: Surely Zion is the city of our God, and surely Zion cannot fall, neither be moved out of her place, for God is there, and the hand of the Lord is there;
20 And he hath <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.907">sworn by the power of his might to be her salvation and her high tower.
21 Therefore, verily, thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.962">Lord, let Zion rejoice, for this is Zion—THE PURE IN HEART; therefore, let Zion rejoice, while all the wicked shall mourn.
22 For behold, and lo, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.951">vengeance cometh speedily upon the ungodly as the whirlwind; and who shall escape it?
23 The Lord's <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.971">scourge shall pass over by night and by day, and the report thereof shall vex all people; yea, it shall not be stayed until the Lord come;
24 For the indignation of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.901">Lord is kindled against their abominations and all their wicked works.
25 Nevertheless, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.900">Zion shall escape if she observe to do all things whatsoever I have commanded her.
26 But if she observe not to do whatsoever I have commanded her, I will <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.955">visit her according to all her works, with sore affliction, with pestilence, with plague, with sword, with vengeance, with devouring fire.
27 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.936">Nevertheless, let it be read this once to her ears, that I, the Lord, have accepted of her offering; and if she sin no more none of these things shall come upon her;
28 And I will <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.944">bless her with blessings, and multiply a multiplicity of blessings upon her, and upon her generations forever and ever, saith the Lord your God. Amen.
