2 Nephi

16

1 IN the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.903">year that king Uzziah died, I saw also the Lord sitting upon a throne, high and lifted up, and his train filled the temple.
2 Above it <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">stood the seraphim; each one had six wings; with twain he covered his face, and with twain he covered his feet, and with twain he did fly.
3 And one cried unto another, and said: <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">Holy, holy, holy, is the Lord of Hosts; the whole earth is full of his glory.
4 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">posts of the door moved at the voice of him that cried, and the house was filled with smoke.
5 Then said I: Wo is unto me! for I am undone; because I am a man of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.834">unclean lips; and I dwell in the midst of a people of unclean lips; for mine eyes have seen the King, the Lord of Hosts.
6 Then <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">flew one of the seraphim unto me, having a live coal in his hand, which he had taken with the tongs from off the altar;
7 And he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">laid it upon my mouth, and said: Lo, this has touched thy lips; and thine iniquity is taken away, and thy sin purged.
8 Also I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">heard the voice of the Lord, saying: Whom shall I send, and who will go for us? Then I said: Here am I; send me.
9 And he said: Go and tell this people--<span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">Hear ye indeed, but they understood not; and see ye indeed, but they perceived not.
10 Make the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_helaman_13_50.821">heart of this people fat, and make their ears heavy, and shut their eyes--lest they see with their eyes, and hear with their ears, and understand with their heart, and be converted and be healed.
11 Then said I: <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.968">Lord, how long? And he said: Until the cities be wasted without inhabitant, and the houses without man, and the land be utterly desolate;
12 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.961">Lord have removed men far away, for there shall be a great forsaking in the midst of the land.
13 But yet there shall be a tenth, and they shall return, and shall be eaten, as a teil-tree, and as an <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.979">oak whose substance is in them when they cast their leaves; so the holy seed shall be the substance thereof.
