Doctrine and Covenants

32

1 And now concerning my servant Parley P. Pratt, behold, I say unto him that as I live I will that he shall declare my gospel and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">learn of me, and be meek and lowly of heart.
2 And that which I have appointed unto him is that he shall go with my servants, Oliver Cowdery and Peter Whitmer, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Jun., into the wilderness among the Lamanites.
3 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Ziba Peterson also shall go with them; and I myself will go with them and be in their midst; and I am their advocate with the Father, and nothing shall prevail against them.
4 And they shall give <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">heed to that which is written, and pretend to no other revelation; and they shall pray always that I may unfold the same to their understanding.
5 And they shall give <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">heed unto these words and trifle not, and I will bless them. Amen.
