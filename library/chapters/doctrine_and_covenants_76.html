Doctrine and Covenants

76

1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pbh_robertsb_h__roberts__comprehensive_history_of_church1.000">Hear, O ye heavens, and give ear, O earth, and rejoice ye inhabitants thereof, for the Lord is God, and beside him there is no Savior.
2 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Great is his wisdom, marvelous are his ways, and the extent of his doings none can find out.
3 His <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">purposes fail not, neither are there any who can stay his hand.
4 From <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">eternity to eternity he is the same, and his years never fail.
5 For thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Lord—I, the Lord, am merciful and gracious unto those who fear me, and delight to honor those who serve me in righteousness and in truth unto the end.
6 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Great shall be their reward and eternal shall be their glory.
7 And to them will I reveal all mysteries, yea, all the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">hidden mysteries of my kingdom from days of old, and for ages to come, will I make known unto them the good pleasure of my will concerning all things pertaining to my kingdom.
8 Yea, even the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pbh_robertsb_h__roberts__comprehensive_history_of_church1.000">wonders of eternity shall they know, and things to come will I show them, even the things of many generations.
9 And their <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">wisdom shall be great, and their understanding reach to heaven; and before them the wisdom of the wise shall perish, and the understanding of the prudent shall come to naught.
10 For by my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.963">Spirit will I enlighten them, and by my power will I make known unto them the secrets of my will—yea, even those things which eye has not seen, nor ear heard, nor yet entered into the heart of man.
11 We, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.967">Joseph Smith, Jun., and Sidney Rigdon, being in the Spirit on the sixteenth day of February, in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and thirty-two—
12 By the power of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.928">Spirit our eyes were opened and our understandings were enlightened, so as to see and understand the things of God—
13 Even those things which were from the beginning before the world was, which were ordained of the Father, through his Only <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.945">Begotten Son, who was in the bosom of the Father, even from the beginning;
14 Of whom we <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.947">bear record; and the record which we bear is the fulness of the gospel of Jesus Christ, who is the Son, whom we saw and with whom we conversed in the heavenly vision.
15 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.952">while we were doing the work of translation, which the Lord had appointed unto us, we came to the twenty-ninth verse of the fifth chapter of John, which was given unto us as follows—
16 Speaking of the resurrection of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.917">dead, concerning those who shall hear the voice of the Son of Man:
17 And shall come forth; they who have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.943">done good, in the resurrection of the just; and they who have done evil, in the resurrection of the unjust.
18 Now this <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.862">caused us to marvel, for it was given unto us of the Spirit.
19 And while we meditated upon these things, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.942">Lord touched the eyes of our understandings and they were opened, and the glory of the Lord shone round about.
20 And we <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.903">beheld the glory of the Son, on the right hand of the Father, and received of his fulness;
21 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.944">saw the holy angels, and them who are sanctified before his throne, worshiping God, and the Lamb, who worship him forever and ever.
22 And now, after the many testimonies which have been given of him, this is the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.840">testimony, last of all, which we give of him: That he lives!
23 For we <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.924">saw him, even on the right hand of God; and we heard the voice bearing record that he is the Only Begotten of the Father—
24 That by him, and through him, and of him, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.912">worlds are and were created, and the inhabitants thereof are begotten sons and daughters unto God.
25 And this we <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.967">saw also, and bear record, that an angel of God who was in authority in the presence of God, who rebelled against the Only Begotten Son whom the Father loved and who was in the bosom of the Father, was thrust down from the presence of God and the Son,
26 And was called Perdition, for the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.918">heavens wept over him—he was Lucifer, a son of the morning.
27 And we <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.985">beheld, and lo, he is fallen! is fallen, even a son of the morning!
28 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.955">while we were yet in the Spirit, the Lord commanded us that we should write the vision; for we beheld Satan, that old serpent, even the devil, who rebelled against God, and sought to take the kingdom of our God and his Christ—
29 Wherefore, he <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.930">maketh war with the saints of God, and encompasseth them round about.
30 And we <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.934">saw a vision of the sufferings of those with whom he made war and overcame, for thus came the voice of the Lord unto us:
31 Thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.955">Lord concerning all those who know my power, and have been made partakers thereof, and suffered themselves through the power of the devil to be overcome, and to deny the truth and defy my power—
32 They are they who are the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.869">sons of perdition, of whom I say that it had been better for them never to have been born;
33 For they are vessels of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.925">wrath, doomed to suffer the wrath of God, with the devil and his angels in eternity;
34 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.867">Concerning whom I have said there is no forgiveness in this world nor in the world to come—
35 Having <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.960">denied the Holy Spirit after having received it, and having denied the Only Begotten Son of the Father, having crucified him unto themselves and put him to an open shame.
36 These are they who shall go away into the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.901">lake of fire and brimstone, with the devil and his angels—
37 And the only <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.965">ones on whom the second death shall have any power;
38 Yea, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.917">verily, the only ones who shall not be redeemed in the due time of the Lord, after the sufferings of his wrath.
39 For all the rest shall be brought forth by the resurrection of the dead, through the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.942">triumph and the glory of the Lamb, who was slain, who was in the bosom of the Father before the worlds were made.
40 And this is the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.913">gospel, the glad tidings, which the voice out of the heavens bore record unto us—
41 That he came into the world, even <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.952">Jesus, to be crucified for the world, and to bear the sins of the world, and to sanctify the world, and to cleanse it from all unrighteousness;
42 That through him all might be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.760">saved whom the Father had put into his power and made by him;
43 Who glorifies the Father, and saves all the works of his hands, except those sons of perdition who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.940">deny the Son after the Father has revealed him.
44 Wherefore, he saves all except them—they shall go away into everlasting punishment, which is endless punishment, which is eternal punishment, to reign with the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.970">devil and his angels in eternity, where their worm dieth not, and the fire is not quenched, which is their torment—
45 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.908">end thereof, neither the place thereof, nor their torment, no man knows;
46 Neither was it <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.873">revealed, neither is, neither will be revealed unto man, except to them who are made partakers thereof;
47 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.884">Nevertheless, I, the Lord, show it by vision unto many, but straightway shut it up again;
48 Wherefore, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.946">end, the width, the height, the depth, and the misery thereof, they understand not, neither any man except those who are ordained unto this condemnation.
49 And we heard the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.934">voice, saying: Write the vision, for lo, this is the end of the vision of the sufferings of the ungodly.
50 And again we bear <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.927">record—for we saw and heard, and this is the testimony of the gospel of Christ concerning them who shall come forth in the resurrection of the just—
51 They are they who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.941">received the testimony of Jesus, and believed on his name and were baptized after the manner of his burial, being buried in the water in his name, and this according to the commandment which he has given—
52 That by <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.939">keeping the commandments they might be washed and cleansed from all their sins, and receive the Holy Spirit by the laying on of the hands of him who is ordained and sealed unto this power;
53 And who overcome by <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.927">faith, and are sealed by the Holy Spirit of promise, which the Father sheds forth upon all those who are just and true.
54 They are they who are the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pgutenberg_ldslds_historical__brigham_young_discourses0.431">church of the Firstborn.
55 They are they into whose <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.940">hands the Father has given all things—
56 They are they who are <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.844">priests and kings, who have received of his fulness, and of his glory;
57 And are priests of the Most High, after the order of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.915">Melchizedek, which was after the order of Enoch, which was after the order of the Only Begotten Son.
58 Wherefore, as it is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.959">written, they are gods, even the sons of God—
59 Wherefore, all things are theirs, whether <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.935">life or death, or things present, or things to come, all are theirs and they are Christ's, and Christ is God's.
60 And they shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.552">overcome all things.
61 Wherefore, let no man <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.929">glory in man, but rather let him glory in God, who shall subdue all enemies under his feet.
62 These shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.847">dwell in the presence of God and his Christ forever and ever.
63 These are they whom he shall bring with him, when he shall come in the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.903">clouds of heaven to reign on the earth over his people.
64 These are they who shall have part in the first resurrection.
65 These are they who shall come forth in the resurrection of the just.
66 These are they who are come unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.919">Mount Zion, and unto the city of the living God, the heavenly place, the holiest of all.
67 These are they who have come to an innumerable company of angels, to the general <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.919">assembly and church of Enoch, and of the Firstborn.
68 These are they whose <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.850">names are written in heaven, where God and Christ are the judge of all.
69 These are they who are just men made <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.937">perfect through Jesus the mediator of the new covenant, who wrought out this perfect atonement through the shedding of his own blood.
70 These are they whose bodies are celestial, whose <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.940">glory is that of the sun, even the glory of God, the highest of all, whose glory the sun of the firmament is written of as being typical.
71 And again, we saw the terrestrial world, and behold and lo, these are they who are of the terrestrial, whose <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.959">glory differs from that of the church of the Firstborn who have received the fulness of the Father, even as that of the moon differs from the sun in the firmament.
72 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.380">Behold, these are they who died without law;
73 And also they who are the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.934">spirits of men kept in prison, whom the Son visited, and preached the gospel unto them, that they might be judged according to men in the flesh;
74 Who received not the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.832">testimony of Jesus in the flesh, but afterwards received it.
75 These are they who are <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.879">honorable men of the earth, who were blinded by the craftiness of men.
76 These are they who receive of his <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.756">glory, but not of his fulness.
77 These are they who receive of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.857">presence of the Son, but not of the fulness of the Father.
78 Wherefore, they are bodies terrestrial, and not bodies celestial, and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.935">differ in glory as the moon differs from the sun.
79 These are they who are not <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.901">valiant in the testimony of Jesus; wherefore, they obtain not the crown over the kingdom of our God.
80 And now this is the end of the vision which we saw of the terrestrial, that the Lord <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.904">commanded us to write while we were yet in the Spirit.
81 And again, we saw the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.939">glory of the telestial, which glory is that of the lesser, even as the glory of the stars differs from that of the glory of the moon in the firmament.
82 These are they who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.796">received not the gospel of Christ, neither the testimony of Jesus.
83 These are they who deny not the Holy Spirit.
84 These are they who are <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjournal_of_discoursesjd_vol_110.314">thrust down to hell.
85 These are they who shall not be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.924">redeemed from the devil until the last resurrection, until the Lord, even Christ the Lamb, shall have finished his work.
86 These are they who receive not of his fulness in the eternal world, but of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.902">Holy Spirit through the ministration of the terrestrial;
87 And the terrestrial through the ministration of the celestial.
88 And also the telestial receive it of the administering of angels who are appointed to <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.945">minister for them, or who are appointed to be ministering spirits for them; for they shall be heirs of salvation.
89 And thus we <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.905">saw, in the heavenly vision, the glory of the telestial, which surpasses all understanding;
90 And no man <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.764">knows it except him to whom God has revealed it.
91 And thus we <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.925">saw the glory of the terrestrial which excels in all things the glory of the telestial, even in glory, and in power, and in might, and in dominion.
92 And thus we <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.920">saw the glory of the celestial, which excels in all things—where God, even the Father, reigns upon his throne forever and ever;
93 Before whose <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.904">throne all things bow in humble reverence, and give him glory forever and ever.
94 They who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.923">dwell in his presence are the church of the Firstborn; and they see as they are seen, and know as they are known, having received of his fulness and of his grace;
95 And he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.942">makes them equal in power, and in might, and in dominion.
96 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.930">glory of the celestial is one, even as the glory of the sun is one.
97 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.827">glory of the terrestrial is one, even as the glory of the moon is one.
98 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.947">glory of the telestial is one, even as the glory of the stars is one; for as one star differs from another star in glory, even so differs one from another in glory in the telestial world;
99 For these are they who are of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.941">Paul, and of Apollos, and of Cephas.
100 These are they who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.920">say they are some of one and some of another—some of Christ and some of John, and some of Moses, and some of Elias, and some of Esaias, and some of Isaiah, and some of Enoch;
101 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.872">received not the gospel, neither the testimony of Jesus, neither the prophets, neither the everlasting covenant.
102 Last of all, these all are they who will not be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.900">gathered with the saints, to be caught up unto the church of the Firstborn, and received into the cloud.
103 These are they who are <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.926">liars, and sorcerers, and adulterers, and whoremongers, and whosoever loves and makes a lie.
104 These are they who suffer the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.423">wrath of God on earth.
105 These are they who suffer the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.922">vengeance of eternal fire.
106 These are they who are <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.944">cast down to hell and suffer the wrath of Almighty God, until the fulness of times, when Christ shall have subdued all enemies under his feet, and shall have perfected his work;
107 When he shall deliver up the kingdom, and present it unto the Father, spotless, saying: I have overcome and have trodden the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.964">wine-press alone, even the wine-press of the fierceness of the wrath of Almighty God.
108 Then shall he be crowned with the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.918">crown of his glory, to sit on the throne of his power to reign forever and ever.
109 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.946">behold, and lo, we saw the glory and the inhabitants of the telestial world, that they were as innumerable as the stars in the firmament of heaven, or as the sand upon the seashore;
110 And heard the voice of the Lord saying: These all shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.934">bow the knee, and every tongue shall confess to him who sits upon the throne forever and ever;
111 For they shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.931">judged according to their works, and every man shall receive according to his own works, his own dominion, in the mansions which are prepared;
112 And they shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.897">servants of the Most High; but where God and Christ dwell they cannot come, worlds without end.
113 This is the end of the vision which we saw, which we were <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.864">commanded to write while we were yet in the Spirit.
114 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.935">great and marvelous are the works of the Lord, and the mysteries of his kingdom which he showed unto us, which surpass all understanding in glory, and in might, and in dominion;
115 Which he <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.875">commanded us we should not write while we were yet in the Spirit, and are not lawful for man to utter;
116 Neither is man capable to make them known, for they are only to be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.931">seen and understood by the power of the Holy Spirit, which God bestows on those who love him, and purify themselves before him;
117 To whom he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.944">grants this privilege of seeing and knowing for themselves;
118 That through the power and manifestation of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.906">Spirit, while in the flesh, they may be able to bear his presence in the world of glory.
119 And to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.890">God and the Lamb be glory, and honor, and dominion forever and ever. Amen.
