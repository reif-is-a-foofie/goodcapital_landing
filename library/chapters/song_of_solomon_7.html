“I am my beloved's, and his desire is for me” (Song of Songs 7:11).“I am my beloved's, and his desire is for me.” There are three desires. The desire of Israel is only for their Father in Heaven, as it is stated: “I am my beloved's, and his desire is for me.” The desire of a woman in only for her husband, as it is stated: “Your desire shall be for your husband” (Genesis 3:16). The desire of the evil inclination is only for Cain and his ilk, as it is stated: “Its desire is for you” (Genesis 4:7).
