Donaldson Commentary●●●○○Cross-ref · Ezekiel 44:15●●●○○
Zadok was high priest in the time of King David, and it was he who remained faithful to the Lord's anointed while others were deposed. The sons of Zadok were to attend to the temple ordinances and to teach the people (Ezekiel 40:46; 43:19; 44:15, 23; 48:11), but as we have seen above, for quite a long period of time the position of high priest passed out of this family (beginning with the Maccabean revolt, when it was retained by the Hasmonaeans).
