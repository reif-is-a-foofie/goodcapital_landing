There are numerous gifts of the Spirit, all different in type and in manner of ministration, but they all come from the one and same God. God is the same, but his children are different-their weaknesses and needs and hopes require different gifts and different manifestations of the power of God. Paul wrote to the Corinthian Saints: "Now there are diversities of gifts, but the same Spirit. And there are differences of administrations, but the same Lord. And there are diversities of operations, but it is the same God which worketh all in all.
