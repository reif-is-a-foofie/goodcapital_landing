1 Nephi

11

1 FOR it came to pass after I had <span class="w" data-st="desir" data-v="1" style="--ws:0.645">desired to know the things that my father had seen, and believing that the Lord was able to make them known unto me, as I sat pondering in mine heart I was caught away in the Spirit of the Lord, yea, into an exceedingly high mountain, which I never had before seen, and upon which I never had before set my foot.
2 And the <span class="w" data-st="spirit" data-v="2" style="--ws:0.145">Spirit said unto me: Behold, what desirest thou?
3 And I said: I <span class="w" data-st="desire" data-v="3" style="--ws:0.625">desire to behold the things which my father saw.
4 And the <span class="w" data-st="spirit" data-v="4" style="--ws:0.352">Spirit said unto me: Believest thou that thy father saw the tree of which he hath spoken?
5 And I said: Yea, thou <span class="w" data-st="know" data-v="5" style="--ws:1.0">knowest that I believe all the words of my father.
6 And when I had <span class="w" data-st="spoken" data-v="6" style="--ws:0.192">spoken these words, the Spirit cried with a loud voice, saying: Hosanna to the Lord, the most high God; for he is God over all the earth, yea, even above all. And blessed art thou, Nephi, because thou believest in the Son of the most high God; wherefore, thou shalt behold the things which thou hast desired.
7 And behold this thing shall be given unto thee for a sign, that after thou hast beheld the tree which bore the fruit which thy father tasted, thou shalt also behold a man descending out of heaven, and him shall ye witness; and after ye have witnessed him ye shall bear record that it is the Son of God.
8 And it came to <span class="w" data-st="pass" data-v="8" style="--ws:0.46">pass that the Spirit said unto me: Look! And I looked and beheld a tree; and it was like unto the tree which my father had seen; and the beauty thereof was far beyond, yea, exceeding of all beauty; and the whiteness thereof did exceed the whiteness of the driven snow.
9 And it came to <span class="w" data-st="pass" data-v="9" style="--ws:0.31">pass after I had seen the tree, I said unto the Spirit: I behold thou hast shown unto me the tree which is precious above all.
10 And he said unto me: What <span class="w" data-st="desir" data-v="10" style="--ws:1.0">desirest thou?
11 And I said unto him: To <span class="w" data-st="know" data-v="11" style="--ws:0.165">know the interpretation thereof--for I spake unto him as a man speaketh; for I beheld that he was in the form of a man; yet nevertheless, I knew that it was the Spirit of the Lord; and he spake unto me as a man speaketh with another.
12 And it came to <span class="w" data-st="pass" data-v="12" style="--ws:0.358">pass that he said unto me: Look! And I looked as if to look upon him, and I saw him not; for he had gone from before my presence.
13 And it came to <span class="w" data-st="pass" data-v="13" style="--ws:0.997">pass that I looked and beheld the great city of Jerusalem, and also other cities. And I beheld the city of Nazareth; and in the city of Nazareth I beheld a virgin, and she was exceedingly fair and white.
14 And it came to <span class="w" data-st="pass" data-v="14" style="--ws:1.0">pass that I saw the heavens open; and an angel came down and stood before me; and he said unto me: Nephi, what beholdest thou?
15 And I said unto him: A virgin, most <span class="w" data-st="beauti" data-v="15" style="--ws:1.0">beautiful and fair above all other virgins.
16 And he said unto me: <span class="w" data-st="know" data-v="16" style="--ws:1.0">Knowest thou the condescension of God?
17 And I said unto him: I <span class="w" data-st="know" data-v="17" style="--ws:1.0">know that he loveth his children; nevertheless, I do not know the meaning of all things.
18 And he said unto me: <span class="w" data-st="behold" data-v="18" style="--ws:0.536">Behold, the virgin whom thou seest is the mother of the Son of God, after the manner of the flesh.
19 And it came to <span class="w" data-st="pass" data-v="19" style="--ws:0.236">pass that I beheld that she was carried away in the Spirit; and after she had been carried away in the Spirit for the space of a time the angel spake unto me, saying: Look!
20 And I <span class="w" data-st="look" data-v="20" style="--ws:0.511">looked and beheld the virgin again, bearing a child in her arms.
21 And the <span class="w" data-st="angel" data-v="21" style="--ws:0.999">angel said unto me: Behold the Lamb of God, yea, even the Son of the Eternal Father! Knowest thou the meaning of the tree which thy father saw?
22 And I <span class="w" data-st="answer" data-v="22" style="--ws:0.426">answered him, saying: Yea, it is the love of God, which sheddeth itself abroad in the hearts of the children of men; wherefore, it is the most desirable above all things.
23 And he <span class="w" data-st="spake" data-v="23" style="--ws:1.0">spake unto me, saying: Yea, and the most joyous to the soul.
24 And after he had said these words, he said unto me: Look! And I <span class="w" data-st="look" data-v="24" style="--ws:1.0">looked, and I beheld the Son of God going forth among the children of men; and I saw many fall down at his feet and worship him.
25 And it came to <span class="w" data-st="pass" data-v="25" style="--ws:0.388">pass that I beheld that the rod of iron, which my father had seen, was the word of God, which led to the fountain of living waters, or to the tree of life; which waters are a representation of the love of God; and I also beheld that the tree of life was a representation of the love of God.
26 And the <span class="w" data-st="angel" data-v="26" style="--ws:0.801">angel said unto me again: Look and behold the condescension of God!
27 And I looked and <span class="w" data-st="beheld" data-v="27" style="--ws:1.0">beheld the Redeemer of the world, of whom my father had spoken; and I also beheld the prophet who should prepare the way before him. And the Lamb of God went forth and was baptized of him; and after he was baptized, I beheld the heavens open, and the Holy Ghost come down out of heaven and abide upon him in the form of a dove.
28 And I <span class="w" data-st="beheld" data-v="28" style="--ws:1.0">beheld that he went forth ministering unto the people, in power and great glory; and the multitudes were gathered together to hear him; and I beheld that they cast him out from among them.
29 And I also beheld twelve others following him. And it came to pass that they were <span class="w" data-st="carri" data-v="29" style="--ws:0.587">carried away in the Spirit from before my face, and I saw them not.
30 And it came to <span class="w" data-st="pass" data-v="30" style="--ws:0.711">pass that the angel spake unto me again, saying: Look! And I looked, and I beheld the heavens open again, and I saw angels descending upon the children of men; and they
31 And he spake unto me again, saying: Look! And I looked, and I <span class="w" data-st="beheld" data-v="31" style="--ws:0.527">beheld the Lamb of God going forth among the children of men. And I beheld multitudes of people who were sick, and who were afflicted with all manner of diseases, and with devils and unclean spirits; and the angel spake and showed all these things unto me. And they were healed by the power of the Lamb of God; and the devils and the unclean spirits were cast out.
32 And it came to <span class="w" data-st="pass" data-v="32" style="--ws:0.489">pass that the angel spake unto me again, saying: Look! And I looked and beheld the Lamb of God, that he was taken by the people; yea, the Son of the everlasting God was judged of the world; and I saw and bear record.
33 And I, <span class="w" data-st="nephi" data-v="33" style="--ws:1.0">Nephi, saw that he was lifted up upon the cross and slain for the sins of the world.
34 And after he was <span class="w" data-st="slain" data-v="34" style="--ws:0.443">slain I saw the multitudes of the earth, that they were gathered together to fight against the apostles of the Lamb; for thus were the twelve called by the angel of the Lord.
35 And the <span class="w" data-st="multitude" data-v="35" style="--ws:0.537">multitude of the earth was gathered together; and I beheld that they were in a large and spacious building, like unto the building which my father saw. And the angel of the Lord spake unto me again, saying: Behold the world and the wisdom thereof; yea, behold the house of Israel hath gathered together to fight against the twelve apostles of the Lamb.
36 And it came to <span class="w" data-st="pass" data-v="36" style="--ws:0.643">pass that I saw and bear record, that the great and spacious building was the pride of the world; and it fell, and the fall thereof was exceedingly great. And the angel of the Lord spake unto me again, saying: Thus shall be the destruction of all nations, kindreds, tongues, and people, that shall fight against the twelve apostles of the Lamb.
