Doctrine and Covenants

33

1 Behold, I say unto you, my servants Ezra and Northrop, open ye your ears and hearken to the voice of the Lord your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">God, whose word is quick and powerful, sharper than a two-edged sword, to the dividing asunder of the joints and marrow, soul and spirit; and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart.
2 For verily, verily, I say unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you that ye are called to lift up your voices as with the sound of a trump, to declare my gospel unto a crooked and perverse generation.
3 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">behold, the field is white already to harvest; and it is the eleventh hour, and the last time that I shall call laborers into my vineyard.
4 And my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">vineyard has become corrupted every whit; and there is none which doeth good save it be a few; and they err in many instances because of priestcrafts, all having corrupt minds.
5 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">verily, verily, I say unto you, that this church have I established and called forth out of the wilderness.
6 And even so will I gather <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">mine elect from the four quarters of the earth, even as many as will believe in me, and hearken unto my voice.
7 Yea, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">verily, verily, I say unto you, that the field is white already to harvest; wherefore, thrust in your sickles, and reap with all your might, mind, and strength.
8 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Open your mouths and they shall be filled, and you shall become even as Nephi of old, who journeyed from Jerusalem in the wilderness.
9 Yea, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">open your mouths and spare not, and you shall be laden with sheaves upon your backs, for lo, I am with you.
10 Yea, open your mouths and they shall be filled, saying: Repent, repent, and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.968">prepare ye the way of the Lord, and make his paths straight; for the kingdom of heaven is at hand;
11 Yea, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.953">repent and be baptized, every one of you, for a remission of your sins; yea, be baptized even by water, and then cometh the baptism of fire and of the Holy Ghost.
12 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.956">Behold, verily, verily, I say unto you, this is my gospel; and remember that they shall have faith in me or they can in nowise be saved;
13 And upon this <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.952">rock I will build my church; yea, upon this rock ye are built, and if ye continue, the gates of hell shall not prevail against you.
14 And ye shall remember the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.912">church articles and covenants to keep them.
15 And whoso having faith you shall confirm in my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.938">church, by the laying on of the hands, and I will bestow the gift of the Holy Ghost upon them.
16 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.946">Book of Mormon and the holy scriptures are given of me for your instruction; and the power of my Spirit quickeneth all things.
17 Wherefore, be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.962">faithful, praying always, having your lamps trimmed and burning, and oil with you, that you may be ready at the coming of the Bridegroom—
18 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.921">behold, verily, verily, I say unto you, that I come quickly. Even so. Amen.
