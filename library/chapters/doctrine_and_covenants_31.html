Donaldson CommentaryCross-ref · James 1:13●●○○○
"The saints should pray always lest they enter into temptation. (3 Ne. 18:18; D&C 61:39.) The meaning of the petition, 'Lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil' (Matt. 6:13; Luke 11:4; 3 Ne. 13:12), is: Suffer us not to be led into greater temptation than we can bear, but deliver us from evil. (Bruce R. Mc Conkie, Doctrinal New Testament Commentary, 3 vols., 3:, p.250)
