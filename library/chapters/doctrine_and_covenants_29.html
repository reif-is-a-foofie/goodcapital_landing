Doctrine and Covenants

29

1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Listen to the voice of Jesus Christ, your Redeemer, the Great I AM, whose arm of mercy hath atoned for your sins;
2 Who will gather his people even as a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">hen gathereth her chickens under her wings, even as many as will hearken to my voice and humble themselves before me, and call upon me in mighty prayer.
3 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Behold, verily, verily, I say unto you, that at this time your sins are forgiven you, therefore ye receive these things; but remember to sin no more, lest perils shall come upon you.
4 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Verily, I say unto you that ye are chosen out of the world to declare my gospel with the sound of rejoicing, as with the voice of a trump.
5 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Lift up your hearts and be glad, for I am in your midst, and am your advocate with the Father; and it is his good will to give you the kingdom.
6 And, as it is written—Whatsoever ye shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">ask in faith, being united in prayer according to my command, ye shall receive.
7 And ye are <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">called to bring to pass the gathering of mine elect; for mine elect hear my voice and harden not their hearts;
8 Wherefore the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">decree hath gone forth from the Father that they shall be gathered in unto one place upon the face of this land, to prepare their hearts and be prepared in all things against the day when tribulation and desolation are sent forth upon the wicked.
9 For the hour is nigh and the day soon at hand when the earth is ripe; and all the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">proud and they that do wickedly shall be as stubble; and I will burn them up, saith the Lord of Hosts, that wickedness shall not be upon the earth;
10 For the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.929">hour is nigh, and that which was spoken by mine apostles must be fulfilled; for as they spoke so shall it come to pass;
11 For I will reveal <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.953">myself from heaven with power and great glory, with all the hosts thereof, and dwell in righteousness with men on earth a thousand years, and the wicked shall not stand.
12 And again, verily, verily, I say unto you, and it hath gone forth in a firm decree, by the will of the Father, that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.983">mine apostles, the Twelve which were with me in my ministry at Jerusalem, shall stand at my right hand at the day of my coming in a pillar of fire, being clothed with robes of righteousness, with crowns upon their heads, in glory even as I am, to judge the whole house of Israel, even as many as have loved me and kept my commandments, and none else.
13 For a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.967">trump shall sound both long and loud, even as upon Mount Sinai, and all the earth shall quake, and they shall come forth—yea, even the dead which died in me, to receive a crown of righteousness, and to be clothed upon, even as I am, to be with me, that we may be one.
14 But, behold, I say unto you that before this great day shall come the sun shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.975">darkened, and the moon shall be turned into blood, and the stars shall fall from heaven, and there shall be greater signs in heaven above and in the earth beneath;
15 And there shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.923">weeping and wailing among the hosts of men;
16 And there shall be a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.947">great hailstorm sent forth to destroy the crops of the earth.
17 And it shall come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.971">pass, because of the wickedness of the world, that I will take vengeance upon the wicked, for they will not repent; for the cup of mine indignation is full; for behold, my blood shall not cleanse them if they hear me not.
18 Wherefore, I the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.962">Lord God will send forth flies upon the face of the earth, which shall take hold of the inhabitants thereof, and shall eat their flesh, and shall cause maggots to come in upon them;
19 And their <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.943">tongues shall be stayed that they shall not utter against me; and their flesh shall fall from off their bones, and their eyes from their sockets;
20 And it shall come to pass that the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.948">beasts of the forest and the fowls of the air shall devour them up.
21 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.973">great and abominable church, which is the whore of all the earth, shall be cast down by devouring fire, according as it is spoken by the mouth of Ezekiel the prophet, who spoke of these things, which have not come to pass but surely must, as I live, for abominations shall not reign.
22 And again, verily, verily, I say unto you that when the thousand <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.959">years are ended, and men again begin to deny their God, then will I spare the earth but for a little season;
23 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.942">end shall come, and the heaven and the earth shall be consumed and pass away, and there shall be a new heaven and a new earth.
24 For all old things shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.962">pass away, and all things shall become new, even the heaven and the earth, and all the fulness thereof, both men and beasts, the fowls of the air, and the fishes of the sea;
25 And not <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.911">one hair, neither mote, shall be lost, for it is the workmanship of mine hand.
26 But, behold, verily I say unto you, before the earth shall pass away, Michael, mine archangel, shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.973">sound his trump, and then shall all the dead awake, for their graves shall be opened, and they shall come forth—yea, even all.
27 And the righteous shall be gathered on my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.940">right hand unto eternal life; and the wicked on my left hand will I be ashamed to own before the Father;
28 Wherefore I will <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.937">say unto them—Depart from me, ye cursed, into everlasting fire, prepared for the devil and his angels.
29 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.927">behold, I say unto you, never at any time have I declared from mine own mouth that they should return, for where I am they cannot come, for they have no power.
30 But remember that all my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.958">judgments are not given unto men; and as the words have gone forth out of my mouth even so shall they be fulfilled, that the first shall be last, and that the last shall be first in all things whatsoever I have created by the word of my power, which is the power of my Spirit.
31 For by the power of my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.897">Spirit created I them; yea, all things both spiritual and temporal—
32 First spiritual, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.939">secondly temporal, which is the beginning of my work; and again, first temporal, and secondly spiritual, which is the last of my work—
33 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.951">Speaking unto you that you may naturally understand; but unto myself my works have no end, neither beginning; but it is given unto you that ye may understand, because ye have asked it of me and are agreed.
34 Wherefore, verily I say unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.953">you that all things unto me are spiritual, and not at any time have I given unto you a law which was temporal; neither any man, nor the children of men; neither Adam, your father, whom I created.
35 Behold, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.969">gave unto him that he should be an agent unto himself; and I gave unto him commandment, but no temporal commandment gave I unto him, for my commandments are spiritual; they are not natural nor temporal, neither carnal nor sensual.
36 And it came to pass that Adam, being tempted of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.969">devil—for, behold, the devil was before Adam, for he rebelled against me, saying, Give me thine honor, which is my power; and also a third part of the hosts of heaven turned he away from me because of their agency;
37 And they were <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.967">thrust down, and thus came the devil and his angels;
38 And, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.900">behold, there is a place prepared for them from the beginning, which place is hell.
39 And it must <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.935">needs be that the devil should tempt the children of men, or they could not be agents unto themselves; for if they never should have bitter they could not know the sweet—
40 Wherefore, it came to pass that the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.963">devil tempted Adam, and he partook of the forbidden fruit and transgressed the commandment, wherein he became subject to the will of the devil, because he yielded unto temptation.
41 Wherefore, I, the Lord God, caused that he should be cast out from the Garden of Eden, from my presence, because of his transgression, wherein he <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.968">became spiritually dead, which is the first death, even that same death which is the last death, which is spiritual, which shall be pronounced upon the wicked when I shall say: Depart, ye cursed.
42 But, behold, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.969">say unto you that I, the Lord God, gave unto Adam and unto his seed, that they should not die as to the temporal death, until I, the Lord God, should send forth angels to declare unto them repentance and redemption, through faith on the name of mine Only Begotten Son.
43 And thus did I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.951">Lord God, appoint unto man the days of his probation—that by his natural death he might be raised in immortality unto eternal life, even as many as would believe;
44 And they that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.925">believe not unto eternal damnation; for they cannot be redeemed from their spiritual fall, because they repent not;
45 For they <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.921">love darkness rather than light, and their deeds are evil, and they receive their wages of whom they list to obey.
46 But behold, I say unto you, that little <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.904">children are redeemed from the foundation of the world through mine Only Begotten;
47 Wherefore, they <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.930">cannot sin, for power is not given unto Satan to tempt little children, until they begin to become accountable before me;
48 For it is given unto them even as I will, according to mine own pleasure, that <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.907">great things may be required at the hand of their fathers.
49 And, again, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.905">say unto you, that whoso having knowledge, have I not commanded to repent?
50 And he that hath no <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.925">understanding, it remaineth in me to do according as it is written. And now I declare no more unto you at this time. Amen.
