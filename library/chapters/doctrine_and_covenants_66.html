Doctrine and Covenants

66

1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.867">Behold, thus saith the Lord unto my servant William E. McLellin—Blessed are you, inasmuch as you have turned away from your iniquities, and have received my truths, saith the Lord your Redeemer, the Savior of the world, even of as many as believe on my name.
2 Verily I say unto you, blessed are you for receiving mine <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">everlasting covenant, even the fulness of my gospel, sent forth unto the children of men, that they might have life and be made partakers of the glories which are to be revealed in the last days, as it was written by the prophets and apostles in days of old.
3 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Verily I say unto you, my servant William, that you are clean, but not all; repent, therefore, of those things which are not pleasing in my sight, saith the Lord, for the Lord will show them unto you.
4 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily, I, the Lord, will show unto you what I will concerning you, or what is my will concerning you.
5 Behold, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">verily I say unto you, that it is my will that you should proclaim my gospel from land to land, and from city to city, yea, in those regions round about where it has not been proclaimed.
6 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Tarry not many days in this place; go not up unto the land of Zion as yet; but inasmuch as you can send, send; otherwise, think not of thy property.
7 Go unto the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">eastern lands, bear testimony in every place, unto every people and in their synagogues, reasoning with the people.
8 Let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">servant Samuel H. Smith go with you, and forsake him not, and give him thine instructions; and he that is faithful shall be made strong in every place; and I, the Lord, will go with you.
9 Lay your hands upon the sick, and they shall recover. Return not till I, the Lord, shall send <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you. Be patient in affliction. Ask, and ye shall receive; knock, and it shall be opened unto you.
10 Seek not to be cumbered. Forsake all unrighteousness. <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.952">Commit not adultery—a temptation with which thou hast been troubled.
11 Keep these sayings, for they are true and faithful; and thou shalt magnify thine office, and push many people to <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.957">Zion with songs of everlasting joy upon their heads.
12 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.940">Continue in these things even unto the end, and you shall have a crown of eternal life at the right hand of my Father, who is full of grace and truth.
13 Verily, thus saith the Lord your God, your Redeemer, even <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.894">Jesus Christ. Amen.
