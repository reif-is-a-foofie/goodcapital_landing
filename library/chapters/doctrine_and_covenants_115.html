Doctrine and Covenants

115

1 Verily thus saith the Lord unto you, my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">servant Joseph Smith, Jun., and also my servant Sidney Rigdon, and also my servant Hyrum Smith, and your counselors who are and shall be appointed hereafter;
2 And also unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you, my servant Edward Partridge, and his counselors;
3 And also unto my faithful servants who are of the high council of my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">church in Zion, for thus it shall be called, and unto all the elders and people of my Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, scattered abroad in all the world;
4 For thus shall my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">church be called in the last days, even The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
5 Verily I say unto you all: <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Arise and shine forth, that thy light may be a standard for the nations;
6 And that the gathering together upon the land of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Zion, and upon her stakes, may be for a defense, and for a refuge from the storm, and from wrath when it shall be poured out without mixture upon the whole earth.
7 Let the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">city, Far West, be a holy and consecrated land unto me; and it shall be called most holy, for the ground upon which thou standest is holy.
8 Therefore, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">command you to build a house unto me, for the gathering together of my saints, that they may worship me.
9 And let there be a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">beginning of this work, and a foundation, and a preparatory work, this following summer;
10 And let the beginning be made on the fourth day of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.964">July next; and from that time forth let my people labor diligently to build a house unto my name;
11 And in <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.924">one year from this day let them re-commence laying the foundation of my house.
12 Thus let them from that time forth <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.833">labor diligently until it shall be finished, from the cornerstone thereof unto the top thereof, until there shall not anything remain that is not finished.
13 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.956">Verily I say unto you, let not my servant Joseph, neither my servant Sidney, neither my servant Hyrum, get in debt any more for the building of a house unto my name;
14 But let a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.923">house be built unto my name according to the pattern which I will show unto them.
15 And if my people <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.945">build it not according to the pattern which I shall show unto their presidency, I will not accept it at their hands.
16 But if my people do <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.962">build it according to the pattern which I shall show unto their presidency, even my servant Joseph and his counselors, then I will accept it at the hands of my people.
17 And again, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.682">verily I say unto you, it is my will that the city of Far West should be built up speedily by the gathering of my saints;
18 And also that other places should be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.993">appointed for stakes in the regions round about, as they shall be manifested unto my servant Joseph, from time to time.
19 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.945">behold, I will be with him, and I will sanctify him before the people; for unto him have I given the keys of this kingdom and ministry. Even so. Amen.
