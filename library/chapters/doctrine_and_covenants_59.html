Doctrine and Covenants

59

1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Behold, blessed, saith the Lord, are they who have come up unto this land with an eye single to my glory, according to my commandments.
2 For those that live shall inherit the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">earth, and those that die shall rest from all their labors, and their works shall follow them; and they shall receive a crown in the mansions of my Father, which I have prepared for them.
3 Yea, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">blessed are they whose feet stand upon the land of Zion, who have obeyed my gospel; for they shall receive for their reward the good things of the earth, and it shall bring forth in its strength.
4 And they shall also be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">crowned with blessings from above, yea, and with commandments not a few, and with revelations in their time—they that are faithful and diligent before me.
5 Wherefore, I give unto them a commandment, saying thus: Thou shalt <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">love the Lord thy God with all thy heart, with all thy might, mind, and strength; and in the name of Jesus Christ thou shalt serve him.
6 Thou shalt <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">love thy neighbor as thyself. Thou shalt not steal; neither commit adultery, nor kill, nor do anything like unto it.
7 Thou shalt <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.396">thank the Lord thy God in all things.
8 Thou shalt offer a sacrifice unto the Lord thy God in righteousness, even that of a <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">broken heart and a contrite spirit.
9 And that thou mayest more <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">fully keep thyself unspotted from the world, thou shalt go to the house of prayer and offer up thy sacraments upon my holy day;
10 For verily this is a day appointed unto you to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.953">rest from your labors, and to pay thy devotions unto the Most High;
11 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.930">Nevertheless thy vows shall be offered up in righteousness on all days and at all times;
12 But remember that on this, the Lord's day, thou shalt offer <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.969">thine oblations and thy sacraments unto the Most High, confessing thy sins unto thy brethren, and before the Lord.
13 And on this day thou shalt do <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.967">none other thing, only let thy food be prepared with singleness of heart that thy fasting may be perfect, or, in other words, that thy joy may be full.
14 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.922">Verily, this is fasting and prayer, or in other words, rejoicing and prayer.
15 And inasmuch as ye do these things with thanksgiving, with <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.963">cheerful hearts and countenances, not with much laughter, for this is sin, but with a glad heart and a cheerful countenance—
16 Verily I say, that inasmuch as ye do this, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.966">fulness of the earth is yours, the beasts of the field and the fowls of the air, and that which climbeth upon the trees and walketh upon the earth;
17 Yea, and the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.958">herb, and the good things which come of the earth, whether for food or for raiment, or for houses, or for barns, or for orchards, or for gardens, or for vineyards;
18 Yea, all things which come of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.951">earth, in the season thereof, are made for the benefit and the use of man, both to please the eye and to gladden the heart;
19 Yea, for <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.947">food and for raiment, for taste and for smell, to strengthen the body and to enliven the soul.
20 And it <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.944">pleaseth God that he hath given all these things unto man; for unto this end were they made to be used, with judgment, not to excess, neither by extortion.
21 And in nothing doth man offend God, or against <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.947">none is his wrath kindled, save those who confess not his hand in all things, and obey not his commandments.
22 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.933">Behold, this is according to the law and the prophets; wherefore, trouble me no more concerning this matter.
23 But learn that he who <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.944">doeth the works of righteousness shall receive his reward, even peace in this world, and eternal life in the world to come.
24 I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.926">Lord, have spoken it, and the Spirit beareth record. Amen.
