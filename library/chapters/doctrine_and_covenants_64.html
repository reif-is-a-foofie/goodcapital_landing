Doctrine and Covenants

64

1 Behold, thus saith the Lord your God unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you, O ye elders of my church, hearken ye and hear, and receive my will concerning you.
2 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily I say unto you, I will that ye should overcome the world; wherefore I will have compassion upon you.
3 There are those among <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you who have sinned; but verily I say, for this once, for mine own glory, and for the salvation of souls, I have forgiven you your sins.
4 I will be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">merciful unto you, for I have given unto you the kingdom.
5 And the keys of the mysteries of the kingdom shall not be taken from my servant <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Joseph Smith, Jun., through the means I have appointed, while he liveth, inasmuch as he obeyeth mine ordinances.
6 There are those who have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">sought occasion against him without cause;
7 Nevertheless, he has sinned; but verily I say unto you, I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Lord, forgive sins unto those who confess their sins before me and ask forgiveness, who have not sinned unto death.
8 My <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">disciples, in days of old, sought occasion against one another and forgave not one another in their hearts; and for this evil they were afflicted and sorely chastened.
9 Wherefore, I say unto you, that ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">ought to forgive one another; for he that forgiveth not his brother his trespasses standeth condemned before the Lord; for there remaineth in him the greater sin.
10 I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.941">Lord, will forgive whom I will forgive, but of you it is required to forgive all men.
11 And ye ought to <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.958">say in your hearts—let God judge between me and thee, and reward thee according to thy deeds.
12 And him that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.942">repenteth not of his sins, and confesseth them not, ye shall bring before the church, and do with him as the scripture saith unto you, either by commandment or by revelation.
13 And this ye shall do that <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.970">God may be glorified—not because ye forgive not, having not compassion, but that ye may be justified in the eyes of the law, that ye may not offend him who is your lawgiver—
14 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_mosiah_26_320.316">Verily I say, for this cause ye shall do these things.
15 Behold, I, the Lord, was angry with him who was my servant <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.964">Ezra Booth, and also my servant Isaac Morley, for they kept not the law, neither the commandment;
16 They sought <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.961">evil in their hearts, and I, the Lord, withheld my Spirit. They condemned for evil that thing in which there was no evil; nevertheless I have forgiven my servant Isaac Morley.
17 And also my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.964">servant Edward Partridge, behold, he hath sinned, and Satan seeketh to destroy his soul; but when these things are made known unto them, and they repent of the evil, they shall be forgiven.
18 And now, verily I say that it is <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.960">expedient in me that my servant Sidney Gilbert, after a few weeks, shall return upon his business, and to his agency in the land of Zion;
19 And that which he hath <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.939">seen and heard may be made known unto my disciples, that they perish not. And for this cause have I spoken these things.
20 And again, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.969">say unto you, that my servant Isaac Morley may not be tempted above that which he is able to bear, and counsel wrongfully to your hurt, I gave commandment that his farm should be sold.
21 I will not that my servant Frederick G. Williams should sell his farm, for I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.967">Lord, will to retain a strong hold in the land of Kirtland, for the space of five years, in the which I will not overthrow the wicked, that thereby I may save some.
22 And after that day, I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.955">Lord, will not hold any guilty that shall go with an open heart up to the land of Zion; for I, the Lord, require the hearts of the children of men.
23 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.968">Behold, now it is called today until the coming of the Son of Man, and verily it is a day of sacrifice, and a day for the tithing of my people; for he that is tithed shall not be burned at his coming.
24 For after today cometh the burning—this is speaking after the manner of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.972">Lord—for verily I say, tomorrow all the proud and they that do wickedly shall be as stubble; and I will burn them up, for I am the Lord of Hosts; and I will not spare any that remain in Babylon.
25 Wherefore, if ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.915">believe me, ye will labor while it is called today.
26 And it is not meet that my servants, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.965">Newel K. Whitney and Sidney Gilbert, should sell their store and their possessions here; for this is not wisdom until the residue of the church, which remaineth in this place, shall go up unto the land of Zion.
27 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.930">Behold, it is said in my laws, or forbidden, to get in debt to thine enemies;
28 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.908">behold, it is not said at any time that the Lord should not take when he please, and pay as seemeth him good.
29 Wherefore, as ye are agents, ye are on the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.945">Lord's errand; and whatever ye do according to the will of the Lord is the Lord's business.
30 And he hath set <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.925">you to provide for his saints in these last days, that they may obtain an inheritance in the land of Zion.
31 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.938">behold, I, the Lord, declare unto you, and my words are sure and shall not fail, that they shall obtain it.
32 But all things must come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="ppseudepigraphapseudepigrapha__charles_apot_vol2_pseudepigrapha0.406">pass in their time.
33 Wherefore, be not weary in well-doing, for ye are laying the foundation of a <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.943">great work. And out of small things proceedeth that which is great.
34 Behold, the Lord requireth the heart and a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.955">willing mind; and the willing and obedient shall eat the good of the land of Zion in these last days.
35 And the rebellious shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.921">cut off out of the land of Zion, and shall be sent away, and shall not inherit the land.
36 For, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.929">verily I say that the rebellious are not of the blood of Ephraim, wherefore they shall be plucked out.
37 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.952">Behold, I, the Lord, have made my church in these last days like unto a judge sitting on a hill, or in a high place, to judge the nations.
38 For it shall come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.924">pass that the inhabitants of Zion shall judge all things pertaining to Zion.
39 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.914">liars and hypocrites shall be proved by them, and they who are not apostles and prophets shall be known.
40 And even the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.943">bishop, who is a judge, and his counselors, if they are not faithful in their stewardships shall be condemned, and others shall be planted in their stead.
41 For, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.906">behold, I say unto you that Zion shall flourish, and the glory of the Lord shall be upon her;
42 And she shall be an <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.908">ensign unto the people, and there shall come unto her out of every nation under heaven.
43 And the day shall come when the nations of the earth shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.945">tremble because of her, and shall fear because of her terrible ones. The Lord hath spoken it. Amen.
