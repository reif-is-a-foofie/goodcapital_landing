1 Nephi

21

1 AND again: <span class="w" data-st="hearken" data-v="1" style="--ws:1.0">Hearken, O ye house of Israel, all ye that are broken off and are driven out because of the wickedness of the pastors of my people; yea, all ye that are broken off, that are scattered abroad, who are of my people, O house of Israel. Listen, O isles, unto me, and hearken ye people from far; the Lord hath called me from the womb; from the bowels of my mother hath he made mention of my name.
2 And he hath made my <span class="w" data-st="mouth" data-v="2" style="--ws:0.693">mouth like a sharp sword; in the shadow of his hand hath he hid me, and made me a polished shaft; in his quiver hath he hid me;
3 And said unto me: Thou art my <span class="w" data-st="servant" data-v="3" style="--ws:1.0">servant, O Israel, in whom I will be glorified.
4 Then I said, I have labored in <span class="w" data-st="vain" data-v="4" style="--ws:1.0">vain, I have spent my strength for naught and in vain; surely my judgment is with the Lord, and my work with my God.
5 And now, saith the <span class="w" data-st="lord" data-v="5" style="--ws:1.0">Lord--that formed me from the womb that I should be his servant, to bring Jacob again to him--though Israel be not gathered, yet shall I be glorious in the eyes of the Lord, and my God shall be my strength.
6 And he said: It is a <span class="w" data-st="light" data-v="6" style="--ws:1.0">light thing that thou shouldst be my servant to raise up the tribes of Jacob, and to restore the preserved of Israel. I will also give thee for a light to the Gentiles, that thou mayest be my salvation unto the ends of the earth.
7 Thus saith the <span class="w" data-st="lord" data-v="7" style="--ws:0.671">Lord, the Redeemer of Israel, his Holy One, to him whom man despiseth, to him whom the nations abhorreth, to servant of rulers: Kings shall see and arise, princes also shall worship, because of the Lord that is faithful.
8 Thus saith the <span class="w" data-st="lord" data-v="8" style="--ws:0.954">Lord: In an acceptable time have I heard thee, O isles of the sea, and in a day of salvation have I helped thee; and I will preserve thee, and give thee my servant for a covenant of the people, to establish the earth, to cause to inherit the desolate heritages;
9 That thou mayest <span class="w" data-st="say" data-v="9" style="--ws:0.605">say to the prisoners: Go forth; to them that sit in darkness: Show yourselves. They shall feed in the ways, and their pastures shall be in all high places.
10 They shall not <span class="w" data-st="hung" data-v="10" style="--ws:0.595">hunger nor thirst, neither shall the heat nor the sun smite them; for he that hath mercy on them shall lead them, even by the springs of water shall he guide them.
11 And I will make all my <span class="w" data-st="mountain" data-v="11" style="--ws:0.419">mountains a way, and my highways shall be exalted.
12 And then, O house of Israel, <span class="w" data-st="behold" data-v="12" style="--ws:1.0">behold, these shall come from far; and lo, these from the north and from the west; and these from the land of Sinim.
13 Sing, O <span class="w" data-st="heaven" data-v="13" style="--ws:1.0">heavens; and be joyful, O earth; for the feet of those who are in the east shall be established; and break forth into singing, O mountains; for they shall be smitten no more; for the Lord hath comforted his people, and will have mercy upon his afflicted.
14 But, <span class="w" data-st="behold" data-v="14" style="--ws:0.184">behold, Zion hath said: The Lord hath forsaken me, and my Lord hath forgotten me--but he will show that he hath not.
15 For can a <span class="w" data-st="woman" data-v="15" style="--ws:0.71">woman forget her sucking child, that she should not have compassion on the son of her womb? Yea, they may forget, yet will I not forget thee, O house of Israel.
16 <span class="w" data-st="behold" data-v="16" style="--ws:0.818">Behold, I have graven thee upon the palms of my hands; thy walls are continually before me.
17 Thy <span class="w" data-st="children" data-v="17" style="--ws:1.0">children shall make haste against thy destroyers; and they that made thee waste shall go forth of thee.
18 <span class="w" data-st="lift" data-v="18" style="--ws:1.0">Lift up thine eyes round about and behold; all these gather themselves together, and they shall come to thee. And as I live, saith the Lord, thou shalt surely clothe thee with them all, as with an ornament, and bind them on even as a bride.
19 For thy <span class="w" data-st="waste" data-v="19" style="--ws:1.0">waste and thy desolate places, and the land of thy destruction, shall even now be too narrow by reason of the inhabitants; and they that swallowed thee up shall be far away.
20 The <span class="w" data-st="children" data-v="20" style="--ws:0.956">children whom thou shalt have, after thou hast lost the first, shall again in thine ears say: The place is too strait for me; give place to me that I may dwell.
21 Then shalt thou <span class="w" data-st="say" data-v="21" style="--ws:1.0">say in thine heart: Who hath begotten me these, seeing I have lost my children, and am desolate, a captive, and removing to and fro? And who hath brought up these? Behold, I was left alone; these, where have they been?
22 Thus saith the <span class="w" data-st="lord" data-v="22" style="--ws:1.0">Lord God: Behold, I will lift up mine hand to the Gentiles, and set up my standard to the people; and they shall bring thy sons in their arms, and thy daughters shall be carried upon their shoulders.
23 And <span class="w" data-st="king" data-v="23" style="--ws:0.82">kings shall be thy nursing fathers, and their queens thy nursing mothers; they shall bow down to thee with their face towards the earth, and lick up the dust of thy feet; and thou shalt know that I am the Lord; for they shall not be ashamed that wait for me.
24 For shall the <span class="w" data-st="prey" data-v="24" style="--ws:0.995">prey be taken from the mighty, or the lawful captives delivered?
25 But thus saith the <span class="w" data-st="lord" data-v="25" style="--ws:0.873">Lord, even the captives of the mighty shall be taken away, and the prey of the terrible shall be delivered; for I will contend with him that contendeth with thee, and I will save thy children.
26 And I will <span class="w" data-st="feed" data-v="26" style="--ws:0.417">feed them that oppress thee with their own flesh; they shall be drunken with their own blood as with sweet wine; and all flesh shall know that I, the Lord, am thy Savior and thy Redeemer, the Mighty One of Jacob.
