3 Nephi

14

1 AND now it came to pass that when Jesus had spoken these words he turned again to the multitude, and did open his mouth unto them again, saying: Verily, verily, I say unto you, Judge not, that ye be not judged.
2 For with what judgment ye judge, ye shall be judged; and with what <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.931">measure ye mete, it shall be measured to you again.
3 And why beholdest thou the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.844">mote that is in thy brother's eye, but considerest not the beam that is in thine own eye?
4 Or how wilt thou say to thy brother: Let me pull the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.792">mote out of thine eye--and behold, a beam is in thine own eye?
5 Thou hypocrite, first cast the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.632">beam out of thine own eye; and then shalt thou see clearly to cast the mote out of thy brother's eye.
6 Give not that which is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.661">holy unto the dogs, neither cast ye your pearls before swine, lest they trample them under their feet, and turn again and rend you.
7 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.764">Ask, and it shall be given unto you; seek, and ye shall find; knock, and it shall be opened unto you.
8 For every <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.969">one that asketh, receiveth; and he that seeketh, findeth; and to him that knocketh, it shall be opened.
9 Or what man is there of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.901">you, who, if his son ask bread, will give him a stone?
10 Or if he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.437">ask a fish, will he give him a serpent?
11 If ye then, being <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.949">evil, know how to give good gifts unto your children, how much more shall your Father who is in heaven give good things to them that ask him?
12 Therefore, all things whatsoever ye would that men should do to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.576">you, do ye even so to them, for this is the law and the prophets.
13 Enter ye in at the strait <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_matthew_7_130.934">gate; for wide is the gate, and broad is the way, which leadeth to destruction, and many there be who go in thereat;
14 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.790">Because strait is the gate, and narrow is the way, which leadeth unto life, and few there be that find it.
15 Beware of false <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.665">prophets, who come to you in sheep's clothing, but inwardly they are ravening wolves.
16 Ye shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.949">know them by their fruits. Do men gather grapes of thorns, or figs of thistles?
17 Even so every <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.863">good tree bringeth forth good fruit; but a corrupt tree bringeth forth evil fruit.
18 A <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.948">good tree cannot bring forth evil fruit, neither a corrupt tree bring forth good fruit.
19 Every <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.985">tree that bringeth not forth good fruit is hewn down, and cast into the fire.
20 Wherefore, by their <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.314">fruits ye shall know them.
21 Not every <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.725">one that saith unto me, Lord, Lord, shall enter into the kingdom of heaven; but he that doeth the will of my Father who is in heaven.
22 Many will say to me in that day: <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.792">Lord, Lord, have we not prophesied in thy name, and in thy name have cast out devils, and in thy name done many wonderful works?
23 And then will I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.368">profess unto them: I never knew you; depart from me, ye that work iniquity.
24 Therefore, whoso heareth these <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjournal_of_discoursesjd_vol_210.591">sayings of mine and doeth them, I will liken him unto a wise man, who built his house upon a rock--
25 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjournal_of_discoursesjd_vol_210.605">rain descended, and the floods came, and the winds blew, and beat upon that house;
26 And every one that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.687">heareth these sayings of mine and doeth them not shall be likened unto a foolish man, who built his house upon the sand--
27 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.643">rain descended, and the floods came, and the winds blew, and beat upon that house; and it fell, and great was the fall of it.
