Donaldson Commentary●●●●○Cross-ref · 1 Kings 12:20●●●●○
Our present biblical text (1 Kgs. 12:20) indicates that only the tribe of Judah followed the house of David, but the Septuagint, the Greek Old Testament, includes Benjamin in the southern kingdom (see footnote b to 1 Kgs. 12:20; cf. 2 Chron. 11:12; 15:9). The tribe of Simeon had also been assimilated into Judah by this time (see Bible Map 9), and Levites and others from the various northern tribes had fled the apostate corruptions in the north, seeking spiritual refuge in Jerusalem.
