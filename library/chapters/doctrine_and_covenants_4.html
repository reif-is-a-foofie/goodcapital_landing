Doctrine and Covenants

4

1 Now <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">behold, a marvelous work is about to come forth among the children of men.
2 Therefore, O ye that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">embark in the service of God, see that ye serve him with all your heart, might, mind and strength, that ye may stand blameless before God at the last day.
3 Therefore, if ye have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">desires to serve God ye are called to the work;
4 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">behold the field is white already to harvest; and lo, he that thrusteth in his sickle with his might, the same layeth up in store that he perisheth not, but bringeth salvation to his soul;
5 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">faith, hope, charity and love, with an eye single to the glory of God, qualify him for the work.
6 Remember faith, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">virtue, knowledge, temperance, patience, brotherly kindness, godliness, charity, humility, diligence.
7 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Ask, and ye shall receive; knock, and it shall be opened unto you. Amen.
