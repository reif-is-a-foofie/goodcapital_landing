Hallowed be thy name] The name of God is hallowed, made holy, held in reverent esteem by any who aspire to goodness and happiness here and hereafter. It is interesting to note that the phrase "thy kingdom come," found in the Galilee sermon, is noticeably absent in the Bountiful address.
