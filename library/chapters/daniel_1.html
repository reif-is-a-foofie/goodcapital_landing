8 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">Daniel purposed in his heart that he would not defile himself with the portion of the king's meat, nor with the wine which he drank: therefore he requested of the prince of the eunuchs that he might not defile himself.

15 And at the end of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.698">ten days their countenances appeared fairer and fatter in flesh than all the children which did eat the portion of the king's meat.
