1 MOREOVER he made an altar of brass, twenty <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.965">cubits the length thereof, and twenty cubits the breadth thereof, and ten cubits the height thereof.
2 Also he made a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.968">molten sea of ten cubits from brim to brim, round in compass, and five cubits the height thereof; and a line of thirty cubits did compass it round about.
3 And under it [was] the similitude of oxen, which did <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">compass it round about: ten in a cubit, compassing the sea round about. Two rows of oxen [were] cast, when it was cast.
4 It <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">stood upon twelve oxen, three looking toward the north, and three looking toward the west, and three looking toward the south, and three looking toward the east: and the sea [was set] above upon them, and all their hinder parts [were] inward.
5 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.903">thickness of it [was] an handbreadth, and the brim of it like the work of the brim of a cup, with flowers of lilies; [and] it received and held three thousand baths.
6 He made also <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">ten lavers, and put five on the right hand, and five on the left, to wash in them: such things as they offered for the burnt offering they washed in them; but the sea [was] for the priests to wash in.
7 And he made ten candlesticks of gold according to their form, and set [them] in the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">temple, five on the right hand, and five on the left.
8 He made also <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.829">ten tables, and placed [them] in the temple, five on the right side, and five on the left. And he made an hundred basons of gold.
9 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">Furthermore he made the court of the priests, and the great court, and doors for the court, and overlaid the doors of them with brass.
10 And he set the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.351">sea on the right side of the east end, over against the south.
11 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.825">Huram made the pots, and the shovels, and the basons. And Huram finished the work that he was to make for king Solomon for the house of God;
12 [To wit], the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.986">two pillars, and the pommels, and the chapiters [which were] on the top of the two pillars, and the two wreaths to cover the two pommels of the chapiters which [were] on the top of the pillars;
13 And four hundred pomegranates on the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.996">two wreaths; two rows of pomegranates on each wreath, to cover the two pommels of the chapiters which [were] upon the pillars.
14 He made also bases, and lavers made he upon the bases;
15 One sea, and twelve oxen under it.
16 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.936">pots also, and the shovels, and the fleshhooks, and all their instruments, did Huram his father make to king Solomon for the house of the LORD of bright brass.
17 In the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.947">plain of Jordan did the king cast them, in the clay ground between Succoth and Zeredathah.
18 Thus <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.916">Solomon made all these vessels in great abundance: for the weight of the brass could not be found out.
19 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.800">Solomon made all the vessels that [were for] the house of God, the golden altar also, and the tables whereon the shewbread [was set];
20 Moreover the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.992">candlesticks with their lamps, that they should burn after the manner before the oracle, of pure gold;
21 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.991">flowers, and the lamps, and the tongs, [made he of] gold, [and] that perfect gold;
22 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.875">snuffers, and the basons, and the spoons, and the censers, [of] pure gold: and the entry of the house, the inner doors thereof for the most holy [place], and the doors of the house of the temple, [were of] gold.
