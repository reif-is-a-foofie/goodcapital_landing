Doctrine and Covenants

93

1 Verily, thus saith the Lord: It shall come to pass that every <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">soul who forsaketh his sins and cometh unto me, and calleth on my name, and obeyeth my voice, and keepeth my commandments, shall see my face and know that I am;
2 And that I am the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">true light that lighteth every man that cometh into the world;
3 And that I am in the Father, and the Father in me, and the Father and I are <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">one—
4 The Father <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">because he gave me of his fulness, and the Son because I was in the world and made flesh my tabernacle, and dwelt among the sons of men.
5 I was in the world and <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">received of my Father, and the works of him were plainly manifest.
6 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">John saw and bore record of the fulness of my glory, and the fulness of John's record is hereafter to be revealed.
7 And he <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">bore record, saying: I saw his glory, that he was in the beginning, before the world was;
8 Therefore, in the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">beginning the Word was, for he was the Word, even the messenger of salvation—
9 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">light and the Redeemer of the world; the Spirit of truth, who came into the world, because the world was made by him, and in him was the life of men and the light of men.
10 The worlds were made by him; men were made by him; all things were made by him, and through him, and of him.
11 And I, John, bear record that I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.972">beheld his glory, as the glory of the Only Begotten of the Father, full of grace and truth, even the Spirit of truth, which came and dwelt in the flesh, and dwelt among us.
12 And I, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.941">John, saw that he received not of the fulness at the first, but received grace for grace;
13 And he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.917">received not of the fulness at first, but continued from grace to grace, until he received a fulness;
14 And thus he was <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.850">called the Son of God, because he received not of the fulness at the first.
15 And I, John, bear record, and lo, the heavens were opened, and the Holy Ghost descended upon him in the form of a dove, and sat upon him, and there came a <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.975">voice out of heaven saying: This is my beloved Son.
16 And I, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.927">John, bear record that he received a fulness of the glory of the Father;
17 And he <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.891">received all power, both in heaven and on earth, and the glory of the Father was with him, for he dwelt in him.
18 And it shall come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.938">pass, that if you are faithful you shall receive the fulness of the record of John.
19 I give unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.956">you these sayings that you may understand and know how to worship, and know what you worship, that you may come unto the Father in my name, and in due time receive of his fulness.
20 For if <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.938">you keep my commandments you shall receive of his fulness, and be glorified in me as I am in the Father; therefore, I say unto you, you shall receive grace for grace.
21 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.884">verily I say unto you, I was in the beginning with the Father, and am the Firstborn;
22 And all those who are <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.885">begotten through me are partakers of the glory of the same, and are the church of the Firstborn.
23 Ye were also in the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.893">beginning with the Father; that which is Spirit, even the Spirit of truth;
24 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pbh_robertsb_h__roberts__comprehensive_history_of_church1.000">truth is knowledge of things as they are, and as they were, and as they are to come;
25 And whatsoever is more or <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.886">less than this is the spirit of that wicked one who was a liar from the beginning.
26 The Spirit of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.954">truth is of God. I am the Spirit of truth, and John bore record of me, saying: He received a fulness of truth, yea, even of all truth;
27 And no man <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.925">receiveth a fulness unless he keepeth his commandments.
28 He that <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.933">keepeth his commandments receiveth truth and light, until he is glorified in truth and knoweth all things.
29 Man was also in the beginning with <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.907">God. Intelligence, or the light of truth, was not created or made, neither indeed can be.
30 All <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.924">truth is independent in that sphere in which God has placed it, to act for itself, as all intelligence also; otherwise there is no existence.
31 Behold, here is the agency of man, and here is the condemnation of man; <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.952">because that which was from the beginning is plainly manifest unto them, and they receive not the light.
32 And every man whose <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.885">spirit receiveth not the light is under condemnation.
33 For man is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.950">spirit. The elements are eternal, and spirit and element, inseparably connected, receive a fulness of joy;
34 And when <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.527">separated, man cannot receive a fulness of joy.
35 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.956">elements are the tabernacle of God; yea, man is the tabernacle of God, even temples; and whatsoever temple is defiled, God shall destroy that temple.
36 The glory of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.859">God is intelligence, or, in other words, light and truth.
37 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.434">Light and truth forsake that evil one.
38 Every <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.953">spirit of man was innocent in the beginning; and God having redeemed man from the fall, men became again, in their infant state, innocent before God.
39 And that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.935">wicked one cometh and taketh away light and truth, through disobedience, from the children of men, and because of the tradition of their fathers.
40 But I have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.833">commanded you to bring up your children in light and truth.
41 But verily I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.904">say unto you, my servant Frederick G. Williams, you have continued under this condemnation;
42 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.932">You have not taught your children light and truth, according to the commandments; and that wicked one hath power, as yet, over you, and this is the cause of your affliction.
43 And now a commandment I give unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.917">you—if you will be delivered you shall set in order your own house, for there are many things that are not right in your house.
44 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.949">Verily, I say unto my servant Sidney Rigdon, that in some things he hath not kept the commandments concerning his children; therefore, first set in order thy house.
45 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.931">Verily, I say unto my servant Joseph Smith, Jun., or in other words, I will call you friends, for you are my friends, and ye shall have an inheritance with me—
46 I <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.909">called you servants for the world's sake, and ye are their servants for my sake—
47 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.935">verily I say unto Joseph Smith, Jun.—You have not kept the commandments, and must needs stand rebuked before the Lord;
48 Your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.941">family must needs repent and forsake some things, and give more earnest heed unto your sayings, or be removed out of their place.
49 What I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.928">say unto one I say unto all; pray always lest that wicked one have power in you, and remove you out of your place.
50 My <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.966">servant Newel K. Whitney also, a bishop of my church, hath need to be chastened, and set in order his family, and see that they are more diligent and concerned at home, and pray always, or they shall be removed out of their place.
51 Now, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.967">say unto you, my friends, let my servant Sidney Rigdon go on his journey, and make haste, and also proclaim the acceptable year of the Lord, and the gospel of salvation, as I shall give him utterance; and by your prayer of faith with one consent I will uphold him.
52 And let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.963">servants Joseph Smith, Jun., and Frederick G. Williams make haste also, and it shall be given them even according to the prayer of faith; and inasmuch as you keep my sayings you shall not be confounded in this world, nor in the world to come.
53 And, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.955">verily I say unto you, that it is my will that you should hasten to translate my scriptures, and to obtain a knowledge of history, and of countries, and of kingdoms, of laws of God and man, and all this for the salvation of Zion. Amen.
