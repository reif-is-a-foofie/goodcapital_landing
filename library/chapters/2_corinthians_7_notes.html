Godly sorrow is born of the Spirit; it is a gift of God that comes to those who have a broken heart and a contrite spirit. The sorrow of the world, in which there is no salvation, is the sorrow of the thief who is caught and the robber who is imprisoned. It is sorrow for lost status, lost wealth, and lost freedom.
