Doctrine and Covenants

41

1 Hearken and <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">hear, O ye my people, saith the Lord and your God, ye whom I delight to bless with the greatest of all blessings, ye that hear me; and ye that hear me not will I curse, that have professed my name, with the heaviest of all cursings.
2 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Hearken, O ye elders of my church whom I have called, behold I give unto you a commandment, that ye shall assemble yourselves together to agree upon my word;
3 And by the prayer of your <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">faith ye shall receive my law, that ye may know how to govern my church and have all things right before me.
4 And I will be your ruler when I come; and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">behold, I come quickly, and ye shall see that my law is kept.
5 He that receiveth my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">law and doeth it, the same is my disciple; and he that saith he receiveth it and doeth it not, the same is not my disciple, and shall be cast out from among you;
6 For it is not <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">meet that the things which belong to the children of the kingdom should be given to them that are not worthy, or to dogs, or the pearls to be cast before swine.
7 And again, it is <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">meet that my servant Joseph Smith, Jun., should have a house built, in which to live and translate.
8 And again, it is meet that my servant <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Sidney Rigdon should live as seemeth him good, inasmuch as he keepeth my commandments.
9 And again, I have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">called my servant Edward Partridge; and I give a commandment, that he should be appointed by the voice of the church, and ordained a bishop unto the church, to leave his merchandise and to spend all his time in the labors of the church;
10 To <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.896">see to all things as it shall be appointed unto him in my laws in the day that I shall give them.
11 And this <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.922">because his heart is pure before me, for he is like unto Nathanael of old, in whom there is no guile.
12 These words are given unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.959">you, and they are pure before me; wherefore, beware how you hold them, for they are to be answered upon your souls in the day of judgment. Even so. Amen.
