McConkie●●○○○
Men receive honors either from God or from their fellow men. As far as the recipients are concerned, in the eternal perspective, the honors of men count for nothing. They may result in some transitory glory in this world, but in eternity no honors will remain except those conferred by Deity. "I receive not honour from men," our Lord said. (John 5:41; Alma 60:36.) "If I honour myself, my honour is nothing: it is my Father that honoureth me; of whom ye say, that he is your God." (John 8:54.) SMcConkie, Mormon Doctrine
