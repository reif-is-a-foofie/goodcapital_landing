1 BUT it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.985">pass within a while after, in the time of wheat harvest, that Samson visited his wife with a kid; and he said, I will go in to my wife into the chamber. But her father would not suffer him to go in.

And they answered, Samson, the son in law of the Timnite, because he had taken his wife, and given her to his companion. And the Philistines came up, and burnt her and her father with fire.
7 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.919">Samson said unto them, Though ye have done this, yet will I be avenged of you, and after that I will cease.
8 And he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">smote them hip and thigh with a great slaughter: and he went down and dwelt in the top of the rock Etam.
9 Then the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">Philistines went up, and pitched in Judah, and spread themselves in Lehi.
10 And the men of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.951">Judah said, Why are ye come up against us? And they answered, To bind Samson are we come up, to do to him as he hath done to us.
11 Then <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.969">three thousand men of Judah went to the top of the rock Etam, and said to Samson, Knowest thou not that the Philistines [are] rulers over us? what [is] this [that] thou hast done unto us? And he said unto them, As they did unto me, so have I done unto them.
12 And they said unto him, We are come down to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.920">bind thee, that we may deliver thee into the hand of the Philistines. And Samson said unto them, Swear unto me, that ye will not fall upon me yourselves.

14 [And] when he came unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.910">Lehi, the Philistines shouted against him: and the Spirit of the LORD came mightily upon him, and the cords that [were] upon his arms became as flax that was burnt with fire, and his bands loosed from off his hands.
15 And he found a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.951">new jawbone of an ass, and put forth his hand, and took it, and slew a thousand men therewith.
16 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.964">Samson said, With the jawbone of an ass, heaps upon heaps, with the jaw of an ass have I slain a thousand men.
17 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.962">pass, when he had made an end of speaking, that he cast away the jawbone out of his hand, and called that place Ramathlehi.
18 And he was sore athirst, and called on the LORD, and said, Thou hast given this great deliverance into the hand of thy servant: and now shall I die for thirst, and fall into the hand of the uncircumcised?
19 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.848">God clave an hollow place that [was] in the jaw, and there came water thereout; and when he had drunk, his spirit came again, and he revived: wherefore he called the name thereof Enhakkore, which [is] in Lehi unto this day.
20 And he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.306">judged Israel in the days of the Philistines twenty years.
