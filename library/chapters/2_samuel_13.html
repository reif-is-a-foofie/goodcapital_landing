the four. Why? To answer, let me refer to a biblical passage, 2 Sam. 13:4: "And Amnon said unto him, I love Tamar, my brother Absalom's sister." Why not just say "my sister"? Because she was not a full sister--Amnon was the son of King David and "Ahinoam the Jezreelitess" (2 Sam. 3:2), while Tamar and Absalom were the children of King David and "Maacah the daughter of Talmai king of Geshur" (2 Sam. 3:3). Tamar and Amnon were the sons of different wives, and therefore only half-brothers, while Tamar and Absalom were full siblings, being from both the same father and mother.
