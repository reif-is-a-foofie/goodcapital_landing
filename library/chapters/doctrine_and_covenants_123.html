The Prophet Joseph wrote of the weight of the world's iniquity and said that it is as "an iron yoke, it is a strong band; they are the very handcuffs, and chains, and shackles, and fetters of hell" (D&C 123:8). This is the only use of this description in all of scripture. Fetters are things, such as chains and shackles, that restrain movement (Judg. 16:21; 2 Kgs. 25:7; Mark 5:4). The fetters of hell are those shackles of sin that bind one to Satan himself. (Hoyt W. Brewster, Jr., Doctrine and Covenants Encyclopedia, p.181)
