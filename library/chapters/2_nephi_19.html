12 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.568">Syrians before and the Philistines behind; and they shall devour Israel with open mouth.
13 For the people <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.629">turneth not unto him that smiteth them, neither do they seek the Lord of Hosts.

For all this his anger is not <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_19_170.803">turned away, but his hand is stretched out still.
