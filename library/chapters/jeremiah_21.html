8 And unto this people thou shalt <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.417">say, Thus saith the LORD; Behold, I set before you the way of life, and the way of death.

13 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.967">Behold, I [am] against thee, O inhabitant of the valley, [and] rock of the plain, saith the LORD; which say, Who shall come down against us? or who shall enter into our habitations?
