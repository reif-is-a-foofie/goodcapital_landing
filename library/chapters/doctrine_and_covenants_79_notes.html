Donaldson Commentary●●○○○Cross-ref · John 14:26●●○○○
"But the Comforter that I will send"-not the other Comforter-"shall teach you all things." Who? "He that loveth me." "This [Comforter] shall bring all things to remembrance, whatsoever things I have said unto you. He shall teach you until ye come to me and my Father." God is not a respecter of persons; we all have the same privilege. Come to God! Weary him until he blesses you. We are entitled to the same blessings: Jesus, revelations, just men, and angels. Not laying again the doctrine of Christ, go on unto perfection.
