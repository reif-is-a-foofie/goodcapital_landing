Doctrine and Covenants

54

1 Behold, thus saith the Lord, even <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end, even he who was crucified for the sins of the world—
2 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Behold, verily, verily, I say unto you, my servant Newel Knight, you shall stand fast in the office whereunto I have appointed you.
3 And if your <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">brethren desire to escape their enemies, let them repent of all their sins, and become truly humble before me and contrite.
4 And as the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">covenant which they made unto me has been broken, even so it has become void and of none effect.
5 And wo to him by whom this offense cometh, for it had been better for him that he had been <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">drowned in the depth of the sea.
6 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">blessed are they who have kept the covenant and observed the commandment, for they shall obtain mercy.
7 Wherefore, go to now and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">flee the land, lest your enemies come upon you; and take your journey, and appoint whom you will to be your leader, and to pay moneys for you.
8 And thus <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you shall take your journey into the regions westward, unto the land of Missouri, unto the borders of the Lamanites.
9 And after you have done journeying, behold, I say unto you, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">seek ye a living like unto men, until I prepare a place for you.
10 And again, be patient in tribulation <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.954">until I come; and, behold, I come quickly, and my reward is with me, and they who have sought me early shall find rest to their souls. Even so. Amen.
