Doctrine and Covenants

61

1 Behold, and hearken unto the voice of him who has all power, who is from everlasting to everlasting, even <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end.
2 Behold, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily thus saith the Lord unto you, O ye elders of my church, who are assembled upon this spot, whose sins are now forgiven you, for I, the Lord, forgive sins, and am merciful unto those who confess their sins with humble hearts;
3 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">verily I say unto you, that it is not needful for this whole company of mine elders to be moving swiftly upon the waters, whilst the inhabitants on either side are perishing in unbelief.
4 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Nevertheless, I suffered it that ye might bear record; behold, there are many dangers upon the waters, and more especially hereafter;
5 For I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Lord, have decreed in mine anger many destructions upon the waters; yea, and especially upon these waters.
6 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Nevertheless, all flesh is in mine hand, and he that is faithful among you shall not perish by the waters.
7 Wherefore, it is <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">expedient that my servant Sidney Gilbert and my servant William W. Phelps be in haste upon their errand and mission.
8 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Nevertheless, I would not suffer that ye should part until you were chastened for all your sins, that you might be one, that you might not perish in wickedness;
9 But now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily I say, it behooveth me that ye should part. Wherefore let my servants Sidney Gilbert and William W. Phelps take their former company, and let them take their journey in haste that they may fill their mission, and through faith they shall overcome;
10 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.872">inasmuch as they are faithful they shall be preserved, and I, the Lord, will be with them.
11 And let the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.913">residue take that which is needful for clothing.
12 Let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.939">servant Sidney Gilbert take that which is not needful with him, as you shall agree.
13 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.951">behold, for your good I gave unto you a commandment concerning these things; and I, the Lord, will reason with you as with men in days of old.
14 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.960">Behold, I, the Lord, in the beginning blessed the waters; but in the last days, by the mouth of my servant John, I cursed the waters.
15 Wherefore, the days will come that no <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.923">flesh shall be safe upon the waters.
16 And it shall be said in days to come that <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.954">none is able to go up to the land of Zion upon the waters, but he that is upright in heart.
17 And, as I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.968">Lord, in the beginning cursed the land, even so in the last days have I blessed it, in its time, for the use of my saints, that they may partake the fatness thereof.
18 And now I give unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.969">you a commandment that what I say unto one I say unto all, that you shall forewarn your brethren concerning these waters, that they come not in journeying upon them, lest their faith fail and they are caught in snares;
19 I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.959">Lord, have decreed, and the destroyer rideth upon the face thereof, and I revoke not the decree.
20 I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.941">Lord, was angry with you yesterday, but today mine anger is turned away.
21 Wherefore, let those <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.954">concerning whom I have spoken, that should take their journey in haste—again I say unto you, let them take their journey in haste.
22 And it <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.937">mattereth not unto me, after a little, if it so be that they fill their mission, whether they go by water or by land; let this be as it is made known unto them according to their judgments hereafter.
23 And now, concerning my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.977">servants, Sidney Rigdon, Joseph Smith, Jun., and Oliver Cowdery, let them come not again upon the waters, save it be upon the canal, while journeying unto their homes; or in other words they shall not come upon the waters to journey, save upon the canal.
24 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.975">Behold, I, the Lord, have appointed a way for the journeying of my saints; and behold, this is the way—that after they leave the canal they shall journey by land, inasmuch as they are commanded to journey and go up unto the land of Zion;
25 And they shall do <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.931">like unto the children of Israel, pitching their tents by the way.
26 And, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.869">behold, this commandment you shall give unto all your brethren.
27 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.939">Nevertheless, unto whom is given power to command the waters, unto him it is given by the Spirit to know all his ways;
28 Wherefore, let him do as the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.936">Spirit of the living God commandeth him, whether upon the land or upon the waters, as it remaineth with me to do hereafter.
29 And unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.935">you is given the course for the saints, or the way for the saints of the camp of the Lord, to journey.
30 And again, verily I say unto you, my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.964">servants, Sidney Rigdon, Joseph Smith, Jun., and Oliver Cowdery, shall not open their mouths in the congregations of the wicked until they arrive at Cincinnati;
31 And in that place they shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.960">lift up their voices unto God against that people, yea, unto him whose anger is kindled against their wickedness, a people who are well-nigh ripened for destruction.
32 And from thence let them <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.953">journey for the congregations of their brethren, for their labors even now are wanted more abundantly among them than among the congregations of the wicked.
33 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.944">concerning the residue, let them journey and declare the word among the congregations of the wicked, inasmuch as it is given;
34 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.898">inasmuch as they do this they shall rid their garments, and they shall be spotless before me.
35 And let them journey together, or two by two, as seemeth them good, only let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.967">servant Reynolds Cahoon, and my servant Samuel H. Smith, with whom I am well pleased, be not separated until they return to their homes, and this for a wise purpose in me.
36 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.945">verily I say unto you, and what I say unto one I say unto all, be of good cheer, little children; for I am in your midst, and I have not forsaken you;
37 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.858">inasmuch as you have humbled yourselves before me, the blessings of the kingdom are yours.
38 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.943">Gird up your loins and be watchful and be sober, looking forth for the coming of the Son of Man, for he cometh in an hour you think not.
39 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.923">Pray always that you enter not into temptation, that you may abide the day of his coming, whether in life or in death. Even so. Amen.
