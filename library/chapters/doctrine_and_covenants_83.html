Doctrine and Covenants

83

1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Verily, thus saith the Lord, in addition to the laws of the church concerning women and children, those who belong to the church, who have lost their husbands or fathers:
2 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Women have claim on their husbands for their maintenance, until their husbands are taken; and if they are not found transgressors they shall have fellowship in the church.
3 And if they are not faithful they shall not have fellowship in the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">church; yet they may remain upon their inheritances according to the laws of the land.
4 All <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">children have claim upon their parents for their maintenance until they are of age.
5 And after that, they have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">claim upon the church, or in other words upon the Lord's storehouse, if their parents have not wherewith to give them inheritances.
6 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">storehouse shall be kept by the consecrations of the church; and widows and orphans shall be provided for, as also the poor. Amen.
