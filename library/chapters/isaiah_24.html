Isaiah

24

1 BEHOLD, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.434">LORD maketh the earth empty, and maketh it waste, and turneth it upside down, and scattereth abroad the inhabitants thereof.
2 And it shall be, as with the people, so with the priest; as with the servant, so with his master; as with the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.827">maid, so with her mistress; as with the buyer, so with the seller; as with the lender, so with the borrower; as with the taker of usury, so with the giver of usury to him.
3 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.762">land shall be utterly emptied, and utterly spoiled: for the LORD hath spoken this word.
4 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.948">earth mourneth [and] fadeth away, the world languisheth [and] fadeth away, the haughty people of the earth do languish.
5 The earth also is defiled under the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.792">inhabitants thereof; because they have transgressed the laws, changed the ordinance, broken the everlasting covenant.
6 Therefore hath the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.858">curse devoured the earth, and they that dwell therein are desolate: therefore the inhabitants of the earth are burned, and few men left.
7 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_isaiah_24_70.478">new wine mourneth, the vine languisheth, all the merryhearted do sigh.
8 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.923">mirth of tabrets ceaseth, the noise of them that rejoice endeth, the joy of the harp ceaseth.
9 They shall not <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">drink wine with a song; strong drink shall be bitter to them that drink it.
10 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.931">city of confusion is broken down: every house is shut up, that no man may come in.
11 [There is] a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.994">crying for wine in the streets; all joy is darkened, the mirth of the land is gone.
12 In the city is left desolation, and the gate is smitten with destruction.
13 When thus it shall be in the midst of the land among the people, [there shall be] as the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.966">shaking of an olive tree, [and] as the gleaning grapes when the vintage is done.
14 They shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.802">lift up their voice, they shall sing for the majesty of the LORD, they shall cry aloud from the sea.
15 Wherefore <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.734">glorify ye the LORD in the fires, [even] the name of the LORD God of Israel in the isles of the sea.
16 From the uttermost part of the earth have we heard songs, [even] glory to the righteous. But I said, My leanness, my leanness, woe unto me! the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.253">treacherous dealers have dealt treacherously; yea, the treacherous dealers have dealt very treacherously.
17 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.548">Fear, and the pit, and the snare, [are] upon thee, O inhabitant of the earth.
18 And it shall come to pass, [that] he who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.982">fleeth from the noise of the fear shall fall into the pit; and he that cometh up out of the midst of the pit shall be taken in the snare: for the windows from on high are open, and the foundations of the earth do shake.
19 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.947">earth is utterly broken down, the earth is clean dissolved, the earth is moved exceedingly.
20 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.967">earth shall reel to and fro like a drunkard, and shall be removed like a cottage; and the transgression thereof shall be heavy upon it; and it shall fall, and not rise again.
21 And it shall come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.962">pass in that day, [that] the LORD shall punish the host of the high ones [that are] on high, and the kings of the earth upon the earth.
22 And they shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.960">gathered together, [as] prisoners are gathered in the pit, and shall be shut up in the prison, and after many days shall they be visited.
23 Then the moon shall be confounded, and the sun ashamed, when the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.952">LORD of hosts shall reign in mount Zion, and in Jerusalem, and before his ancients gloriously.
