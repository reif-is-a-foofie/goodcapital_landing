1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Behold, I say unto you, David, that you have feared man and have not relied on me for strength as you ought.
2 But your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">mind has been on the things of the earth more than on the things of me, your Maker, and the ministry whereunto you have been called; and you have not given heed unto my Spirit, and to those who were set over you, but have been persuaded by those whom I have not commanded.
3 Wherefore, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you are left to inquire for yourself at my hand, and ponder upon the things which you have received.
4 And your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">home shall be at your father's house, until I give unto you further commandments. And you shall attend to the ministry in the church, and before the world, and in the regions round about. Amen.
5 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Behold, I say unto you, Peter, that you shall take your journey with your brother Oliver; for the time has come that it is expedient in me that you shall open your mouth to declare my gospel; therefore, fear not, but give heed unto the words and advice of your brother, which he shall give you.
6 And be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you afflicted in all his afflictions, ever lifting up your heart unto me in prayer and faith, for his and your deliverance; for I have given unto him power to build up my church among the Lamanites;
7 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">none have I appointed to be his counselor over him in the church, concerning church matters, except it is his brother, Joseph Smith, Jun.
8 Wherefore, give heed unto these things and be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">diligent in keeping my commandments, and you shall be blessed unto eternal life. Amen.
9 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Behold, I say unto you, my servant John, that thou shalt commence from this time forth to proclaim my gospel, as with the voice of a trump.
10 And your labor shall be at your brother <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.946">Philip Burroughs', and in that region round about, yea, wherever you can be heard, until I command you to go from hence.
11 And your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.952">whole labor shall be in Zion, with all your soul, from henceforth; yea, you shall ever open your mouth in my cause, not fearing what man can do, for I am with you. Amen.
