1 CONCERNING the Ammonites, thus saith the LORD; Hath Israel no sons? hath he no heir? why [then] doth their king inherit Gad, and his people dwell in his cities?

7 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">Concerning Edom, thus saith the LORD of hosts; [Is] wisdom no more in Teman? is counsel perished from the prudent? is their wisdom vanished?

10 But I have made <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.826">Esau bare, I have uncovered his secret places, and he shall not be able to hide himself: his seed is spoiled, and his brethren, and his neighbours, and he [is] not.
11 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.950">Leave thy fatherless children, I will preserve [them] alive; and let thy widows trust in me.
12 For thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.940">LORD; Behold, they whose judgment [was] not to drink of the cup have assuredly drunken; and [art] thou he [that] shall altogether go unpunished? thou shalt not go unpunished, but thou shalt surely drink [of it].
13 For I have sworn by myself, saith the LORD, that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.996">Bozrah shall become a desolation, a reproach, a waste, and a curse; and all the cities thereof shall be perpetual wastes.
14 I have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.895">heard a rumour from the LORD, and an ambassador is sent unto the heathen, [saying], Gather ye together, and come against her, and rise up to the battle.

17 Also Edom shall be a desolation: every <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.952">one that goeth by it shall be astonished, and shall hiss at all the plagues thereof.

19 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.870">Behold, he shall come up like a lion from the swelling of Jordan against the habitation of the strong: but I will suddenly make him run away from her: and who [is] a chosen [man, that] I may appoint over her? for who [is] like me? and who will appoint me the time? and who [is] that shepherd that will stand before me?

23 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.771">Concerning Damascus. Hamath is confounded, and Arpad: for they have heard evil tidings:
24 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.996">Damascus is waxed feeble, [and] turneth herself to flee, and fear hath seized on [her]: anguish and sorrows have taken her, as a woman in travail.

26 Therefore her <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.936">young men shall fall in her streets, and all the men of war shall be cut off in that day, saith the LORD of hosts.

28 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.795">Concerning Kedar, and concerning the kingdoms of Hazor, which Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon shall smite, thus saith the LORD; Arise ye, go up to Kedar, and spoil the men of the east.
29 Their <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.956">tents and their flocks shall they take away: they shall take to themselves their curtains, and all their vessels, and their camels; and they shall cry unto them, Fear [is] on every side.
30 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.995">Flee, get you far off, dwell deep, O ye inhabitants of Hazor, saith the LORD; for Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon hath taken counsel against you, and hath conceived a purpose against you.
31 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.656">Arise, get you up unto the wealthy nation, that dwelleth without care, saith the LORD, which have neither gates nor bars, [which] dwell alone.
32 And their <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.686">camels shall be a booty, and the multitude of their cattle a spoil: and I will scatter into all winds them [that are] in the utmost corners; and I will bring their calamity from all sides thereof, saith the LORD.
33 And Hazor shall be a dwelling for <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.668">dragons, [and] a desolation for ever: there shall no man abide there, nor [any] son of man dwell in it.
34 The word of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.737">LORD that came to Jeremiah the prophet against Elam in the beginning of the reign of Zedekiah king of Judah, saying,

36 And upon <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.989">Elam will I bring the four winds from the four quarters of heaven, and will scatter them toward all those winds; and there shall be no nation whither the outcasts of Elam shall not come.
37 For I will <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.794">cause Elam to be dismayed before their enemies, and before them that seek their life: and I will bring evil upon them, [even] my fierce anger, saith the LORD; and I will send the sword after them, till I have consumed them:
38 And I will set my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.777">throne in Elam, and will destroy from thence the king and the princes, saith the LORD.
39 But it shall come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.926">pass in the latter days, [that] I will bring again the captivity of Elam, saith the LORD.
