12

1 AND at that time shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.341">Michael stand up, the great prince which standeth for the children of thy people: and there shall be a time of trouble, such as never was since there was a nation [even] to that same time: and at that time thy people shall be delivered, every one that shall be found written in the book.
2 And many of them that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_alma_40_250.427">sleep in the dust of the earth shall awake, some to everlasting life, and some to shame [and] everlasting contempt.

5 Then I Daniel looked, and, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.502">behold, there stood other two, the one on this side of the bank of the river, and the other on that side of the bank of the river.

8 And I heard, but I understood not: then said I, O my Lord, what [shall be] the end of these [things]?
9 And he said, Go thy way, Daniel: for the words [are] closed up and sealed till the time of the end.
10 Many shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_30_60.341">purified, and made white, and tried; but the wicked shall do wickedly: and none of the wicked shall understand; but the wise shall understand.
