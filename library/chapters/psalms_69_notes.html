Donaldson Commentary●●●○○Cross-ref · Moroni 10:34●●●○○
The original sense of magnify was "to praise highly, to glorify, to extol," specifically, "to praise or render honor to God." While the modern sense of magnify is connected to appearances, the older sense was to, in fact, "make greater in size, status, importance, to enlarge, increase, augment, or advance" (OED). To magnify is also "to cause to be held in greater esteem or respect" (Merriam-Webster Dictionary). While we most often think of the word "magnify" in terms of magnifying our callings, there are a number of different uses in the scripture.
