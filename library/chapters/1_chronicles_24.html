1 Chronicles

24

1 NOW [these are] the divisions of the sons of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.808">Aaron. The sons of Aaron; Nadab, and Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar.
2 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.963">Nadab and Abihu died before their father, and had no children: therefore Eleazar and Ithamar executed the priest's office.
3 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.911">David distributed them, both Zadok of the sons of Eleazar, and Ahimelech of the sons of Ithamar, according to their offices in their service.
4 And there were more chief men found of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.963">sons of Eleazar than of the sons of Ithamar; and [thus] were they divided. Among the sons of Eleazar [there were] sixteen chief men of the house of [their] fathers, and eight among the sons of Ithamar according to the house of their fathers.
5 Thus were they <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">divided by lot, one sort with another; for the governors of the sanctuary, and governors [of the house] of God, were of the sons of Eleazar, and of the sons of Ithamar.
6 And Shemaiah the son of Nethaneel the scribe, [one] of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.955">Levites, wrote them before the king, and the princes, and Zadok the priest, and Ahimelech the son of Abiathar, and [before] the chief of the fathers of the priests and Levites: one principal household being taken for Eleazar, and [one] taken for Ithamar.
7 Now the first <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.329">lot came forth to Jehoiarib, the second to Jedaiah,
8 The third to Harim, the fourth to Seorim,
9 The fifth to <span class="cw" data-tgt="papocryphalxx_apocrypha__charles_apot_vol1_apocrypha0.276">Malchijah, the sixth to Mijamin,
10 The seventh to Hakkoz, the eighth to Abijah,
11 The ninth to Jeshua, the tenth to Shecaniah,
12 The eleventh to Eliashib, the twelfth to Jakim,
13 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="ppseudepigraphapseudepigrapha__charles_apot_vol2_pseudepigrapha0.342">thirteenth to Huppah, the fourteenth to Jeshebeab,
14 The fifteenth to Bilgah, the sixteenth to Immer,
15 The seventeenth to Hezir, the eighteenth to Aphses,
16 The nineteenth to Pethahiah, the twentieth to Jehezekel,
17 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.261">one and twentieth to Jachin, the two and twentieth to Gamul,
18 The three and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.261">twentieth to Delaiah, the four and twentieth to Maaziah.
19 These [were] the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.994">orderings of them in their service to come into the house of the LORD, according to their manner, under Aaron their father, as the LORD God of Israel had commanded him.
20 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.958">rest of the sons of Levi [were these]: Of the sons of Amram; Shubael: of the sons of Shubael; Jehdeiah.
21 Concerning <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.509">Rehabiah: of the sons of Rehabiah, the first [was] Isshiah.
22 Of the Izharites; Shelomoth: of the sons of Shelomoth; Jahath.
23 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.961">sons [of Hebron]; Jeriah [the first], Amariah the second, Jahaziel the third, Jekameam the fourth.
24 [Of] the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.281">sons of Uzziel; Michah: of the sons of Michah; Shamir.
25 The brother of Michah [was] Isshiah: of the sons of Isshiah; Zechariah.
26 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.333">sons of Merari [were] Mahli and Mushi: the sons of Jaaziah; Beno.
27 The sons of Merari by Jaaziah; Beno, and Shoham, and Zaccur, and Ibri.
28 Of Mahli [came] Eleazar, who had no sons.
29 Concerning <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.259">Kish: the son of Kish [was] Jerahmeel.
30 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.958">sons also of Mushi; Mahli, and Eder, and Jerimoth. These [were] the sons of the Levites after the house of their fathers.
31 These likewise cast lots over against their brethren the sons of Aaron in the presence of David the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.920">king, and Zadok, and Ahimelech, and the chief of the fathers of the priests and Levites, even the principal fathers over against their younger brethren.
