Doctrine and Covenants

22

1 Behold, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">say unto you that all old covenants have I caused to be done away in this thing; and this is a new and an everlasting covenant, even that which was from the beginning.
2 Wherefore, although a man should be baptized an hundred times it availeth him nothing, for <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you cannot enter in at the strait gate by the law of Moses, neither by your dead works.
3 For it is <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">because of your dead works that I have caused this last covenant and this church to be built up unto me, even as in days of old.
4 Wherefore, enter ye in at the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">gate, as I have commanded, and seek not to counsel your God. Amen.
