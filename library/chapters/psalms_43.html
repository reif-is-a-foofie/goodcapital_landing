There are several clear examples of psalms exhibiting parallel arrangement schemes. A parallel arrangement may be employed to establish or underscore an important pattern in the psalmist's line of reasoning. The repetitions generated by this pattern may also serve to emphasize certain points. If the structure features an unmatched final unit, that unit presents the psalm's main point.
