Doctrine and Covenants

94

1 And again, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">verily I say unto you, my friends, a commandment I give unto you, that ye shall commence a work of laying out and preparing a beginning and foundation of the city of the stake of Zion, here in the land of Kirtland, beginning at my house.
2 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">behold, it must be done according to the pattern which I have given unto you.
3 And let the first <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">lot on the south be consecrated unto me for the building of a house for the presidency, for the work of the presidency, in obtaining revelations; and for the work of the ministry of the presidency, in all things pertaining to the church and kingdom.
4 Verily I say unto you, that it shall be built <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">fifty-five by sixty-five feet in the width thereof and in the length thereof, in the inner court.
5 And there shall be a lower court and a higher court, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">according to the pattern which shall be given unto you hereafter.
6 And it shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">dedicated unto the Lord from the foundation thereof, according to the order of the priesthood, according to the pattern which shall be given unto you hereafter.
7 And it shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">wholly dedicated unto the Lord for the work of the presidency.
8 And ye shall not suffer any <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">unclean thing to come in unto it; and my glory shall be there, and my presence shall be there.
9 But if there shall come into it any <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">unclean thing, my glory shall not be there; and my presence shall not come into it.
10 And again, verily I say unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.972">you, the second lot on the south shall be dedicated unto me for the building of a house unto me, for the work of the printing of the translation of my scriptures, and all things whatsoever I shall command you.
11 And it shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.971">fifty-five by sixty-five feet in the width thereof and the length thereof, in the inner court; and there shall be a lower and a higher court.
12 And this house shall be wholly <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.974">dedicated unto the Lord from the foundation thereof, for the work of the printing, in all things whatsoever I shall command you, to be holy, undefiled, according to the pattern in all things as it shall be given unto you.
13 And on the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.905">third lot shall my servant Hyrum Smith receive his inheritance.
14 And on the first and second lots on the north shall my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.960">servants Reynolds Cahoon and Jared Carter receive their inheritances—
15 That they may do the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.941">work which I have appointed unto them, to be a committee to build mine houses, according to the commandment, which I, the Lord God, have given unto you.
16 These <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.852">two houses are not to be built until I give unto you a commandment concerning them.
17 And now I give unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.433">you no more at this time. Amen.
