Doctrine and Covenants

99

1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Behold, thus saith the Lord unto my servant John Murdock—thou art called to go into the eastern countries from house to house, from village to village, and from city to city, to proclaim mine everlasting gospel unto the inhabitants thereof, in the midst of persecution and wickedness.
2 And who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">receiveth you receiveth me; and you shall have power to declare my word in the demonstration of my Holy Spirit.
3 And who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">receiveth you as a little child, receiveth my kingdom; and blessed are they, for they shall obtain mercy.
4 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">whoso rejecteth you shall be rejected of my Father and his house; and you shall cleanse your feet in the secret places by the way for a testimony against them.
5 And behold, and lo, I come quickly to judgment, to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">convince all of their ungodly deeds which they have committed against me, as it is written of me in the volume of the book.
6 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily I say unto you, that it is not expedient that you should go until your children are provided for, and sent up kindly unto the bishop of Zion.
7 And after a few <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">years, if thou desirest of me, thou mayest go up also unto the goodly land, to possess thine inheritance;
8 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Otherwise thou shalt continue proclaiming my gospel until thou be taken. Amen.
