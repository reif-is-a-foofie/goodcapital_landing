12 And this land, [which] we possessed at that time, from Aroer, which [is] by the river Arnon, and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.816">half mount Gilead, and the cities thereof, gave I unto the Reubenites and to the Gadites.

14 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.895">Jair the son of Manasseh took all the country of Argob unto the coasts of Geshuri and Maachathi; and called them after his own name, Bashanhavothjair, unto this day.
15 And I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.665">gave Gilead unto Machir.
16 And unto the Reubenites and unto the Gadites I gave from <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.977">Gilead even unto the river Arnon half the valley, and the border even unto the river Jabbok, [which is] the border of the children of Ammon;
17 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.892">plain also, and Jordan, and the coast [thereof], from Chinnereth even unto the sea of the plain, [even] the salt sea, under Ashdothpisgah eastward.

19 But your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.945">wives, and your little ones, and your cattle, ([for] I know that ye have much cattle,) shall abide in your cities which I have given you;

21 And I commanded Joshua at that time, saying, Thine eyes have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.995">seen all that the LORD your God hath done unto these two kings: so shall the LORD do unto all the kingdoms whither thou passest.

25 I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.929">pray thee, let me go over, and see the good land that [is] beyond Jordan, that goodly mountain, and Lebanon.

27 Get thee up into the top of Pisgah, and lift up thine <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.971">eyes westward, and northward, and southward, and eastward, and behold [it] with thine eyes: for thou shalt not go over this Jordan.
