Doctrine and Covenants

44

1 Behold, thus saith the Lord unto you my servants, it is expedient in me that the elders of my church should be called together, from the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">east and from the west, and from the north and from the south, by letter or some other way.
2 And it shall come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">pass, that inasmuch as they are faithful, and exercise faith in me, I will pour out my Spirit upon them in the day that they assemble themselves together.
3 And it shall come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">pass that they shall go forth into the regions round about, and preach repentance unto the people.
4 And many shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">converted, insomuch that ye shall obtain power to organize yourselves according to the laws of man;
5 That your enemies may not have power over <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you; that you may be preserved in all things; that you may be enabled to keep my laws; that every bond may be broken wherewith the enemy seeketh to destroy my people.
6 Behold, I say unto you, that ye must visit the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">poor and the needy and administer to their relief, that they may be kept until all things may be done according to my law which ye have received. Amen.
