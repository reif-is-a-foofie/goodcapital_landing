17 And I sent them with commandment unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.870">Iddo the chief at the place Casiphia, and I told them what they should say unto Iddo, [and] to his brethren the Nethinims, at the place Casiphia, that they should bring unto us ministers for the house of our God.
18 And by the good hand of our God upon us they brought us a man of understanding, of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="papocryphalxx_apocrypha__charles_apot_vol1_apocrypha0.770">sons of Mahli, the son of Levi, the son of Israel; and Sherebiah, with his sons and his brethren, eighteen;

23 So we <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_ezra_8_240.272">fasted and besought our God for this: and he was intreated of us.

26 I even <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.946">weighed unto their hand six hundred and fifty talents of silver, and silver vessels an hundred talents, [and] of gold an hundred talents;
