1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Verily thus saith the Lord unto you, my servant Lyman: Your sins are forgiven you, because you have obeyed my voice in coming up hither this morning to receive counsel of him whom I have appointed.
2 Therefore, let your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">soul be at rest concerning your spiritual standing, and resist no more my voice.
3 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">arise up and be more careful henceforth in observing your vows, which you have made and do make, and you shall be blessed with exceeding great blessings.
4 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Wait patiently until the solemn assembly shall be called of my servants, then you shall be remembered with the first of mine elders, and receive right by ordination with the rest of mine elders whom I have chosen.
5 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Behold, this is the promise of the Father unto you if you continue faithful.
6 And it shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">fulfilled upon you in that day that you shall have right to preach my gospel wheresoever I shall send you, from henceforth from that time.
7 Therefore, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">strengthen your brethren in all your conversation, in all your prayers, in all your exhortations, and in all your doings.
8 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">behold, and lo, I am with you to bless you and deliver you forever. Amen.
