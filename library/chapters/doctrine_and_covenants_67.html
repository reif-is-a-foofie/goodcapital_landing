Doctrine and Covenants

67

1 Behold and <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">hearken, O ye elders of my church, who have assembled yourselves together, whose prayers I have heard, and whose hearts I know, and whose desires have come up before me.
2 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Behold and lo, mine eyes are upon you, and the heavens and the earth are in mine hands, and the riches of eternity are mine to give.
3 Ye endeavored to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">believe that ye should receive the blessing which was offered unto you; but behold, verily I say unto you there were fears in your hearts, and verily this is the reason that ye did not receive.
4 And now I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Lord, give unto you a testimony of the truth of these commandments which are lying before you.
5 Your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">eyes have been upon my servant Joseph Smith, Jun., and his language you have known, and his imperfections you have known; and you have sought in your hearts knowledge that you might express beyond his language; this you also know.
6 Now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">seek ye out of the Book of Commandments, even the least that is among them, and appoint him that is the most wise among you;
7 Or, if there be any among <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you that shall make one like unto it, then ye are justified in saying that ye do not know that they are true;
8 But if ye cannot make <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">one like unto it, ye are under condemnation if ye do not bear record that they are true.
9 For ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">know that there is no unrighteousness in them, and that which is righteous cometh down from above, from the Father of lights.
10 And again, verily I say unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.982">you that it is your privilege, and a promise I give unto you that have been ordained unto this ministry, that inasmuch as you strip yourselves from jealousies and fears, and humble yourselves before me, for ye are not sufficiently humble, the veil shall be rent and you shall see me and know that I am—not with the carnal neither natural mind, but with the spiritual.
11 For no man has seen <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.925">God at any time in the flesh, except quickened by the Spirit of God.
12 Neither can any natural man abide the presence of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.897">God, neither after the carnal mind.
13 Ye are not <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.961">able to abide the presence of God now, neither the ministering of angels; wherefore, continue in patience until ye are perfected.
14 Let not your <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.969">minds turn back; and when ye are worthy, in mine own due time, ye shall see and know that which was conferred upon you by the hands of my servant Joseph Smith, Jun. Amen.
