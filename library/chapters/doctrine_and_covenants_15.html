Doctrine and Covenants

15

1 Hearken, my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">servant John, and listen to the words of Jesus Christ, your Lord and your Redeemer.
2 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">behold, I speak unto you with sharpness and with power, for mine arm is over all the earth.
3 And I will tell <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you that which no man knoweth save me and thee alone—
4 For many times <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you have desired of me to know that which would be of the most worth unto you.
5 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Behold, blessed are you for this thing, and for speaking my words which I have given you according to my commandments.
6 And now, behold, I say unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you, that the thing which will be of the most worth unto you will be to declare repentance unto this people, that you may bring souls unto me, that you may rest with them in the kingdom of my Father. Amen.
