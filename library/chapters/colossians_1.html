1PAUL, an apostle
2To the saints and faithful brethren in Christ
3We give thanks to God
4Since we heard
5For the hope which is laid up for you
6Which is come unto you
7As ye also learned
8Who also declared
9For this cause
10That ye might walk
11Strengthened
12Giving
13Who hath delivered
14In whom we have redemption through his blood
15Who is the image
16For by him were all things created
17 And he is before all things, and by him all things consist.
18And he is the head
19For it pleased
20And, having made peace
21And you
22In the body
23If ye continue
24Who now rejoice
25Whereof
26[Even] the mystery which hath been hid
27To whom God
28Whom we preach
29Whereunto
