Doctrine and Covenants

95

1 Verily, thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Lord unto you whom I love, and whom I love I also chasten that their sins may be forgiven, for with the chastisement I prepare a way for their deliverance in all things out of temptation, and I have loved you—
2 Wherefore, ye must <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">needs be chastened and stand rebuked before my face;
3 For ye have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">sinned against me a very grievous sin, in that ye have not considered the great commandment in all things, that I have given unto you concerning the building of mine house;
4 For the preparation wherewith I <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">design to prepare mine apostles to prune my vineyard for the last time, that I may bring to pass my strange act, that I may pour out my Spirit upon all flesh—
5 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">behold, verily I say unto you, that there are many who have been ordained among you, whom I have called but few of them are chosen.
6 They who are not chosen have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">sinned a very grievous sin, in that they are walking in darkness at noon-day.
7 And for this cause I gave unto you a commandment that you should call your solemn assembly, that your fastings and your mourning might come up into the ears of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Lord of Sabaoth, which is by interpretation, the creator of the first day, the beginning and the end.
8 Yea, verily I say unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you, I gave unto you a commandment that you should build a house, in the which house I design to endow those whom I have chosen with power from on high;
9 For this is the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">promise of the Father unto you; therefore I command you to tarry, even as mine apostles at Jerusalem.
10 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.964">Nevertheless, my servants sinned a very grievous sin; and contentions arose in the school of the prophets; which was very grievous unto me, saith your Lord; therefore I sent them forth to be chastened.
11 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.924">Verily I say unto you, it is my will that you should build a house. If you keep my commandments you shall have power to build it.
12 If <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.908">you keep not my commandments, the love of the Father shall not continue with you, therefore you shall walk in darkness.
13 Now here is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.949">wisdom, and the mind of the Lord—let the house be built, not after the manner of the world, for I give not unto you that ye shall live after the manner of the world;
14 Therefore, let it be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.950">built after the manner which I shall show unto three of you, whom ye shall appoint and ordain unto this power.
15 And the size thereof shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.949">fifty and five feet in width, and let it be sixty-five feet in length, in the inner court thereof.
16 And let the lower part of the inner <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.974">court be dedicated unto me for your sacrament offering, and for your preaching, and your fasting, and your praying, and the offering up of your most holy desires unto me, saith your Lord.
17 And let the higher part of the inner <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.979">court be dedicated unto me for the school of mine apostles, saith Son Ahman; or, in other words, Alphus; or, in other words, Omegus; even Jesus Christ your Lord. Amen.
