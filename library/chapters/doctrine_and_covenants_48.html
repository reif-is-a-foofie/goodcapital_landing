Doctrine and Covenants

48

1 It is necessary that ye should <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">remain for the present time in your places of abode, as it shall be suitable to your circumstances.
2 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">inasmuch as ye have lands, ye shall impart to the eastern brethren;
3 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">inasmuch as ye have not lands, let them buy for the present time in those regions round about, as seemeth them good, for it must needs be necessary that they have places to live for the present time.
4 It must <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">needs be necessary that ye save all the money that ye can, and that ye obtain all that ye can in righteousness, that in time ye may be enabled to purchase land for an inheritance, even the city.
5 The place is not yet to be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">revealed; but after your brethren come from the east there are to be certain men appointed, and to them it shall be given to know the place, or to them it shall be revealed.
6 And they shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">appointed to purchase the lands, and to make a commencement to lay the foundation of the city; and then shall ye begin to be gathered with your families, every man according to his family, according to his circumstances, and as is appointed to him by the presidency and the bishop of the church, according to the laws and commandments which ye have received, and which ye shall hereafter receive. Even so. Amen.
