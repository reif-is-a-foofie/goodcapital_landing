1 THEN Jonah prayed unto the LORD his God out of the fish's belly,
2 And said, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.994">cried by reason of mine affliction unto the LORD, and he heard me; out of the belly of hell cried I, [and] thou heardest my voice.

8 They that observe lying vanities forsake their own mercy.
9 But I will <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.843">sacrifice unto thee with the voice of thanksgiving; I will pay [that] that I have vowed. Salvation [is] of the LORD.
