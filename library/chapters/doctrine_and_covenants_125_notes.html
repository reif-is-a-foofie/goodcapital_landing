1 WHAT is the will of the Lord concerning the saints in the Territory of Iowa?
Donaldson Commentary●●○○○Cross-ref · Moroni 10:34●●○○○
In June 1839, shortly after land was purchased for the settlement of the Saints in Illinois, the Prophet Joseph Smith negotiated the purchase of land across the Mississippi River in Iowa Territory (Smith, History of the Church, 3:378). At the October 1839 general conference of the Church, a stake was appointed for the gathering of the Saints at Commerce (Nauvoo), Illinois. At that same time a stake was appointed on the "west side of the river, in Iowa Territory; over which Elder John Smith was appointed President" (Smith, History of the Church, 4:12).
