They just discussed the presentation at dinner and she knows how badly he needs his sleep. It occurs to him that his wife is being quite thoughtless as she lays there. He remembers several other instances of thoughtless-ness on her part in the last few months. Yes, indeed he thinks to himself, I am married to a thoughtless woman.
