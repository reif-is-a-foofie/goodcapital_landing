McConkie / Teachings of Joseph Smith
Men are commanded to repent, walk uprightly, and sin not. (D. & C. 5:21; 18:31; 46:7.) Parents are to teach their children "to walk uprightly before the Lord." (D. & C. 68:28.) Only the upright will be welcome in Zion (D. & C. 61:16), and all things shall work together for their good. (D. & C. 90:24; 100:15.) Mercy shall be their inheritance (D. & C. 109:1) and salvation their reward. (Ps. 15:1-2; Prov. 28:18; Isa. 33:14-16.)McConkie, Mormon Doctrine
