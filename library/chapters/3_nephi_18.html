3 Nephi

18

1 AND it came to pass that Jesus commanded his disciples that they should bring forth some bread and wine unto him.
2 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.324">while they were gone for bread and wine, he commanded the multitude that they should sit themselves down upon the earth.
3 And when the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.416">disciples had come with bread and wine, he took of the bread and brake and blessed it; and he gave unto the disciples and commanded that they should eat.
4 And when they had <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.295">eaten and were filled, he commanded that they should give unto the multitude.
5 And when the multitude had eaten and were filled, he said unto the disciples: Behold there shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_18_50.583">one be ordained among you, and to him will I give power that he shall break bread and bless it and give it unto the people of my church, unto all those who shall believe and be baptized in my name.
6 And this shall ye always <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_32_60.358">observe to do, even as I have done, even as I have broken bread and blessed it and given it unto you.
7 And this shall ye do in remembrance of my body, which I have shown unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pgeneral_conferencegc__general_conference_1989_04_the_beauty_and_importance_of_the_sacrament0.411">you. And it shall be a testimony unto the Father that ye do always remember me. And if ye do always remember me ye shall have my Spirit to be with you.
8 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.370">pass that when he said these words, he commanded his disciples that they should take of the wine of the cup and drink of it, and that they should also give unto the multitude that they might drink of it.
9 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.341">pass that they did so, and did drink of it and were filled; and they gave unto the multitude, and they did drink, and they were filled.
10 And when the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.454">disciples had done this, Jesus said unto them: Blessed are ye for this thing which ye have done, for this is fulfilling my commandments, and this doth witness unto the Father that ye are willing to do that which I have commanded you.
11 And this shall ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.443">always do to those who repent and are baptized in my name; and ye shall do it in remembrance of my blood, which I have shed for you, that ye may witness unto the Father that ye do always remember me. And if ye do always remember me ye shall have my Spirit to be with you.
12 And I give unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.346">you a commandment that ye shall do these things. And if ye shall always do these things blessed are ye, for ye are built upon my rock.
13 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.507">whoso among you shall do more or less than these are not built upon my rock, but are built upon a sandy foundation; and when the rain descends, and the floods come, and the winds blow, and beat upon them, they shall fall, and the gates of hell are ready open to receive them.
14 Therefore <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_12_10.281">blessed are ye if ye shall keep my commandments, which the Father hath commanded me that I should give unto you.
15 Verily, verily, I say unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_mosiah_4_300.536">you, ye must watch and pray always, lest ye be tempted by the devil, and ye be led away captive by him.
16 And as I have prayed among <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.251">you even so shall ye pray in my church, among my people who do repent and are baptized in my name. Behold I am the light; I have set an example for you.
17 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.277">pass that when Jesus had spoken these words unto his disciples, he turned again unto the multitude and said unto them:
18 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.382">Behold, verily, verily, I say unto you, ye must watch and pray always lest ye enter into temptation; for Satan desireth to have you, that he may sift you as wheat.
19 Therefore ye must <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.292">always pray unto the Father in my name;
20 And whatsoever ye shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_matthew_21_220.522">ask the Father in my name, which is right, believing that ye shall receive, behold it shall be given unto you.
21 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_18_210.453">Pray in your families unto the Father, always in my name, that your wives and your children may be blessed.
22 And behold, ye shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_alma_5_600.443">meet together oft; and ye shall not forbid any man from coming unto you when ye shall meet together, but suffer them that they may come unto you and forbid them not;
23 But ye shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_alma_5_600.425">pray for them, and shall not cast them out; and if it so be that they come unto you oft ye shall pray for them unto the Father, in my name.
24 Therefore, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_matthew_5_160.685">hold up your light that it may shine unto the world. Behold I am the light which ye shall hold up--that which ye have seen me do. Behold ye see that I have prayed unto the Father, and ye all have witnessed.
25 And ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_18_250.305">see that I have commanded that none of you should go away, but rather have commanded that ye should come unto me, that ye might feel and see; even so shall ye do unto the world; and whosoever breaketh this commandment suffereth himself to be led into temptation.
26 And now it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjournal_of_discoursesjd_vol_150.322">pass that when Jesus had spoken these words, he turned his eyes again upon the disciples whom he had chosen, and said unto them:
27 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.349">Behold verily, verily, I say unto you, I give unto you another commandment, and then I must go unto my Father that I may fulfil other commandments which he hath given me.
28 And now behold, this is the commandment which I give unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjournal_of_discoursesjd_vol_150.481">you, that ye shall not suffer any one knowingly to partake of my flesh and blood unworthily, when ye shall minister it;
29 For whoso <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjournal_of_discoursesjd_vol_150.589">eateth and drinketh my flesh and blood unworthily eateth and drinketh damnation to his soul; therefore if ye know that a man is unworthy to eat and drink of my flesh and blood ye shall forbid him.
30 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pgeneral_conferencegc__general_conference_1988_04_while_they_are_waiting0.392">Nevertheless, ye shall not cast him out from among you, but ye shall minister unto him and shall pray for him unto the Father, in my name; and if it so be that he repenteth and is baptized in my name, then shall ye receive him, and shall minister unto him of my flesh and blood.
31 But if he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_18_310.577">repent not he shall not be numbered among my people, that he may not destroy my people, for behold I know my sheep, and they are numbered.
32 Nevertheless, ye shall not cast him <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_18_320.742">out of your synagogues, or your places of worship, for unto such shall ye continue to minister; for ye know not but what they will return and repent, and come unto me with full purpose of heart, and I shall heal them; and ye shall be the means of bringing salvation unto them.
33 Therefore, keep these sayings which I have commanded you that ye come not under condemnation; for wo unto him whom the Father condemneth.
34 And I give you these commandments because of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_27_30.290">disputations which have been among you. And blessed are ye if ye have no disputations among you.
35 And now I go unto the Father, because it is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_18_350.372">expedient that I should go unto the Father for your sakes.
36 And it came to pass that when Jesus had made an end of these sayings, he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.316">touched with his hand the disciples whom he had chosen, one by one, even until he had touched them all, and spake unto them as he touched them.

38 And it came to pass that when Jesus had touched them all, there came a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.367">cloud and overshadowed the multitude that they could not see Jesus.
39 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.394">while they were overshadowed he departed from them, and ascended into heaven. And the disciples saw and did bear record that he ascended again into heaven.
