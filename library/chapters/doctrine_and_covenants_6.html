Doctrine and Covenants

6

1 A <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">great and marvelous work is about to come forth unto the children of men.
2 Behold, I am <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">God; give heed unto my word, which is quick and powerful, sharper than a two-edged sword, to the dividing asunder of both joints and marrow; therefore give heed unto my words.
3 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Behold, the field is white already to harvest; therefore, whoso desireth to reap, let him thrust in his sickle with his might, and reap while the day lasts, that he may treasure up for his soul everlasting salvation in the kingdom of God.
4 Yea, whosoever will <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">thrust in his sickle and reap, the same is called of God.
5 Therefore, if you will <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">ask of me you shall receive; if you will knock it shall be opened unto you.
6 Now, as you have asked, behold, I say unto you, keep my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">commandments, and seek to bring forth and establish the cause of Zion;
7 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Seek not for riches but for wisdom, and behold, the mysteries of God shall be unfolded unto you, and then shall you be made rich. Behold, he that hath eternal life is rich.
8 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Verily, verily, I say unto you, even as you desire of me so it shall be unto you; and if you desire, you shall be the means of doing much good in this generation.
9 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Say nothing but repentance unto this generation; keep my commandments, and assist to bring forth my work, according to my commandments, and you shall be blessed.
10 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.960">Behold thou hast a gift, and blessed art thou because of thy gift. Remember it is sacred and cometh from above—
11 And if thou wilt <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.984">inquire, thou shalt know mysteries which are great and marvelous; therefore thou shalt exercise thy gift, that thou mayest find out mysteries, that thou mayest bring many to the knowledge of the truth, yea, convince them of the error of their ways.
12 Make not thy <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.953">gift known unto any save it be those who are of thy faith. Trifle not with sacred things.
13 If thou wilt do good, yea, and hold out faithful to the end, thou shalt be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.974">saved in the kingdom of God, which is the greatest of all the gifts of God; for there is no gift greater than the gift of salvation.
14 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.979">Verily, verily, I say unto thee, blessed art thou for what thou hast done; for thou hast inquired of me, and behold, as often as thou hast inquired thou hast received instruction of my Spirit. If it had not been so, thou wouldst not have come to the place where thou art at this time.
15 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.976">Behold, thou knowest that thou hast inquired of me and I did enlighten thy mind; and now I tell thee these things that thou mayest know that thou hast been enlightened by the Spirit of truth;
16 Yea, I tell thee, that thou mayest <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.957">know that there is none else save God that knowest thy thoughts and the intents of thy heart.
17 I tell thee these things as a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.940">witness unto thee—that the words or the work which thou hast been writing are true.
18 Therefore be <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.944">diligent; stand by my servant Joseph, faithfully, in whatsoever difficult circumstances he may be for the word's sake.
19 Admonish him in his faults, and also receive admonition of him. Be patient; be sober; be temperate; have patience, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.954">faith, hope and charity.
20 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.978">Behold, thou art Oliver, and I have spoken unto thee because of thy desires; therefore treasure up these words in thy heart. Be faithful and diligent in keeping the commandments of God, and I will encircle thee in the arms of my love.
21 Behold, I am Jesus Christ, the Son of God. I am the same that came unto mine own, and mine own received me not. I am the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.950">light which shineth in darkness, and the darkness comprehendeth it not.
22 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.962">Verily, verily, I say unto you, if you desire a further witness, cast your mind upon the night that you cried unto me in your heart, that you might know concerning the truth of these things.
23 Did I not <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.933">speak peace to your mind concerning the matter? What greater witness can you have than from God?
24 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.948">behold, you have received a witness; for if I have told you things which no man knoweth have you not received a witness?
25 And, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.934">behold, I grant unto you a gift, if you desire of me, to translate, even as my servant Joseph.
26 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.931">Verily, verily, I say unto you, that there are records which contain much of my gospel, which have been kept back because of the wickedness of the people;
27 And now I <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.963">command you, that if you have good desires—a desire to lay up treasures for yourself in heaven—then shall you assist in bringing to light, with your gift, those parts of my scriptures which have been hidden because of iniquity.
28 And now, behold, I give unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.960">you, and also unto my servant Joseph, the keys of this gift, which shall bring to light this ministry; and in the mouth of two or three witnesses shall every word be established.
29 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.935">Verily, verily, I say unto you, if they reject my words, and this part of my gospel and ministry, blessed are ye, for they can do no more unto you than unto me.
30 And even if they do unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.882">you even as they have done unto me, blessed are ye, for you shall dwell with me in glory.
31 But if they reject not my words, which shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.942">established by the testimony which shall be given, blessed are they, and then shall ye have joy in the fruit of your labors.
32 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.944">Verily, verily, I say unto you, as I said unto my disciples, where two or three are gathered together in my name, as touching one thing, behold, there will I be in the midst of them—even so am I in the midst of you.
33 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.968">Fear not to do good, my sons, for whatsoever ye sow, that shall ye also reap; therefore, if ye sow good ye shall also reap good for your reward.
34 Therefore, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.947">fear not, little flock; do good; let earth and hell combine against you, for if ye are built upon my rock, they cannot prevail.
35 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.938">Behold, I do not condemn you; go your ways and sin no more; perform with soberness the work which I have commanded you.
36 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_hebrews_11_330.359">Look unto me in every thought; doubt not, fear not.
37 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.962">Behold the wounds which pierced my side, and also the prints of the nails in my hands and feet; be faithful, keep my commandments, and ye shall inherit the kingdom of heaven. Amen.
