Amos

2

1 THUS saith the LORD; For three <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.951">transgressions of Moab, and for four, I will not turn away [the punishment] thereof; because he burned the bones of the king of Edom into lime:
2 But I will send a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.932">fire upon Moab, and it shall devour the palaces of Kerioth: and Moab shall die with tumult, with shouting, [and] with the sound of the trumpet:
3 And I will <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">cut off the judge from the midst thereof, and will slay all the princes thereof with him, saith the LORD.
4 Thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">LORD; For three transgressions of Judah, and for four, I will not turn away [the punishment] thereof; because they have despised the law of the LORD, and have not kept his commandments, and their lies caused them to err, after the which their fathers have walked:
5 But I will send a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">fire upon Judah, and it shall devour the palaces of Jerusalem.
6 Thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.948">LORD; For three transgressions of Israel, and for four, I will not turn away [the punishment] thereof; because they sold the righteous for silver, and the poor for a pair of shoes;
7 That <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.943">pant after the dust of the earth on the head of the poor, and turn aside the way of the meek: and a man and his father will go in unto the [same] maid, to profane my holy name:
8 And they <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">lay [themselves] down upon clothes laid to pledge by every altar, and they drink the wine of the condemned [in] the house of their god.
9 Yet <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.840">destroyed I the Amorite before them, whose height [was] like the height of the cedars, and he [was] strong as the oaks; yet I destroyed his fruit from above, and his roots from beneath.
10 Also I brought you up from the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.649">land of Egypt, and led you forty years through the wilderness, to possess the land of the Amorite.
11 And I raised up of your sons for prophets, and of your young men for Nazarites. [Is it] not even thus, O ye children of Israel? saith the LORD.
12 But ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.958">gave the Nazarites wine to drink; and commanded the prophets, saying, Prophesy not.
13 Behold, I am pressed under you, as a cart is pressed [that is] full of sheaves.
14 Therefore the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.994">flight shall perish from the swift, and the strong shall not strengthen his force, neither shall the mighty deliver himself:
15 Neither shall he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.975">stand that handleth the bow; and [he that is] swift of foot shall not deliver [himself]: neither shall he that rideth the horse deliver himself.
16 And [he that is] courageous among the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.991">mighty shall flee away naked in that day, saith the LORD.
