14 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.927">fire is gone out of a rod of her branches, [which] hath devoured her fruit, so that she hath no strong rod [to be] a sceptre to rule. This [is] a lamentation, and shall be for a lamentation.
