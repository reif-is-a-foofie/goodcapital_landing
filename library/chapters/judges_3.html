21 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.994">Ehud put forth his left hand, and took the dagger from his right thigh, and thrust it into his belly:
22 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.996">haft also went in after the blade; and the fat closed upon the blade, so that he could not draw the dagger out of his belly; and the dirt came out.

26 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.993">Ehud escaped while they tarried, and passed beyond the quarries, and escaped unto Seirath.
27 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.954">pass, when he was come, that he blew a trumpet in the mountain of Ephraim, and the children of Israel went down with him from the mount, and he before them.

29 And they <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.992">slew of Moab at that time about ten thousand men, all lusty, and all men of valour; and there escaped not a man.

31 And after him was <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.846">Shamgar the son of Anath, which slew of the Philistines six hundred men with an ox goad: and he also delivered Israel.
