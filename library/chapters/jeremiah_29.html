12 Then shall ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.807">call upon me, and ye shall go and pray unto me, and I will hearken unto you.

15 Because ye have said, The LORD hath raised us up prophets in Babylon;
16 [<span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.753">Know] that thus saith the LORD of the king that sitteth upon the throne of David, and of all the people that dwelleth in this city, [and] of your brethren that are not gone forth with you into captivity;
