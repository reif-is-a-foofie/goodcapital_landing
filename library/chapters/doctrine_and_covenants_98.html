Doctrine and Covenants

1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Verily I say unto you my friends, fear not, let your hearts be comforted; yea, rejoice evermore, and in everything give thanks;
2 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Waiting patiently on the Lord, for your prayers have entered into the ears of the Lord of Sabaoth, and are recorded with this seal and testament—the Lord hath sworn and decreed that they shall be granted.
3 Therefore, he giveth this promise unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you, with an immutable covenant that they shall be fulfilled; and all things wherewith you have been afflicted shall work together for your good, and to my name's glory, saith the Lord.
4 And now, verily I say unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you concerning the laws of the land, it is my will that my people should observe to do all things whatsoever I command them.
5 And that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">law of the land which is constitutional, supporting that principle of freedom in maintaining rights and privileges, belongs to all mankind, and is justifiable before me.
6 Therefore, I, the Lord, justify you, and your brethren of my church, in <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">befriending that law which is the constitutional law of the land;
7 And as <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">pertaining to law of man, whatsoever is more or less than this, cometh of evil.
8 I, the Lord <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">God, make you free, therefore ye are free indeed; and the law also maketh you free.
9 Nevertheless, when the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.540">wicked rule the people mourn.
10 Wherefore, honest men and wise men should be sought for diligently, and good men and wise men ye should observe to uphold; otherwise whatsoever is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.970">less than these cometh of evil.
11 And I give unto you a commandment, that ye shall forsake all evil and cleave unto all good, that ye shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.961">live by every word which proceedeth forth out of the mouth of God.
12 For he will give unto the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.946">faithful line upon line, precept upon precept; and I will try you and prove you herewith.
13 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.919">whoso layeth down his life in my cause, for my name's sake, shall find it again, even life eternal.
14 Therefore, be not afraid of your enemies, for I have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.949">decreed in my heart, saith the Lord, that I will prove you in all things, whether you will abide in my covenant, even unto death, that you may be found worthy.
15 For if ye will not <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.902">abide in my covenant ye are not worthy of me.
16 Therefore, renounce war and proclaim peace, and seek diligently to <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.949">turn the hearts of the children to their fathers, and the hearts of the fathers to the children;
17 And again, the hearts of the Jews unto the prophets, and the prophets unto the Jews; <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.956">lest I come and smite the whole earth with a curse, and all flesh be consumed before me.
18 Let not your hearts be troubled; for in my Father's <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.950">house are many mansions, and I have prepared a place for you; and where my Father and I am, there ye shall be also.
19 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.886">Behold, I, the Lord, am not well pleased with many who are in the church at Kirtland;
20 For they do not <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.958">forsake their sins, and their wicked ways, the pride of their hearts, and their covetousness, and all their detestable things, and observe the words of wisdom and eternal life which I have given unto them.
21 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.953">Verily I say unto you, that I, the Lord, will chasten them and will do whatsoever I list, if they do not repent and observe all things whatsoever I have said unto them.
22 And again I say unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.959">you, if ye observe to do whatsoever I command you, I, the Lord, will turn away all wrath and indignation from you, and the gates of hell shall not prevail against you.
23 Now, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.965">speak unto you concerning your families—if men will smite you, or your families, once, and ye bear it patiently and revile not against them, neither seek revenge, ye shall be rewarded;
24 But if ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.956">bear it not patiently, it shall be accounted unto you as being meted out as a just measure unto you.
25 And again, if your enemy shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.843">smite you the second time, and you revile not against your enemy, and bear it patiently, your reward shall be an hundred-fold.
26 And again, if he shall smite <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.960">you the third time, and ye bear it patiently, your reward shall be doubled unto you four-fold;
27 And these <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.908">three testimonies shall stand against your enemy if he repent not, and shall not be blotted out.
28 And now, verily I say unto you, if that enemy shall escape my vengeance, that he be not brought into judgment before me, then ye shall see to it that ye warn him in my name, that he come no more upon you, neither upon your family, even your <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.967">children's children unto the third and fourth generation.
29 And then, if he shall come upon you or your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.958">children, or your children's children unto the third and fourth generation, I have delivered thine enemy into thine hands;
30 And then if thou wilt spare him, thou shalt be rewarded for thy righteousness; and also thy <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.965">children and thy children's children unto the third and fourth generation.
31 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.978">Nevertheless, thine enemy is in thine hands; and if thou rewardest him according to his works thou art justified; if he has sought thy life, and thy life is endangered by him, thine enemy is in thine hands and thou art justified.
32 Behold, this is the law I gave unto my servant Nephi, and thy fathers, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.959">Joseph, and Jacob, and Isaac, and Abraham, and all mine ancient prophets and apostles.
33 And again, this is the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.946">law that I gave unto mine ancients, that they should not go out unto battle against any nation, kindred, tongue, or people, save I, the Lord, commanded them.
34 And if any <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.944">nation, tongue, or people should proclaim war against them, they should first lift a standard of peace unto that people, nation, or tongue;
35 And if that people did not <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.931">accept the offering of peace, neither the second nor the third time, they should bring these testimonies before the Lord;
36 Then I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.938">Lord, would give unto them a commandment, and justify them in going out to battle against that nation, tongue, or people.
37 And I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.957">Lord, would fight their battles, and their children's battles, and their children's children's, until they had avenged themselves on all their enemies, to the third and fourth generation.
38 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.911">Behold, this is an ensample unto all people, saith the Lord your God, for justification before me.
39 And again, verily I say unto you, if after <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.971">thine enemy has come upon thee the first time, he repent and come unto thee praying thy forgiveness, thou shalt forgive him, and shalt hold it no more as a testimony against thine enemy—
40 And so on unto the second and third time; and as oft as thine enemy repenteth of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.964">trespass wherewith he has trespassed against thee, thou shalt forgive him, until seventy times seven.
41 And if he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.898">trespass against thee and repent not the first time, nevertheless thou shalt forgive him.
42 And if he <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.903">trespass against thee the second time, and repent not, nevertheless thou shalt forgive him.
43 And if he <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.889">trespass against thee the third time, and repent not, thou shalt also forgive him.
44 But if he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.968">trespass against thee the fourth time thou shalt not forgive him, but shalt bring these testimonies before the Lord; and they shall not be blotted out until he repent and reward thee four-fold in all things wherewith he has trespassed against thee.
45 And if he do this, thou shalt forgive him with all <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.946">thine heart; and if he do not this, I, the Lord, will avenge thee of thine enemy an hundred-fold;
46 And upon his <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.898">children, and upon his children's children of all them that hate me, unto the third and fourth generation.
47 But if the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.978">children shall repent, or the children's children, and turn to the Lord their God, with all their hearts and with all their might, mind, and strength, and restore four-fold for all their trespasses wherewith they have trespassed, or wherewith their fathers have trespassed, or their fathers' fathers, then thine indignation shall be turned away;
48 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.932">vengeance shall no more come upon them, saith the Lord thy God, and their trespasses shall never be brought any more as a testimony before the Lord against them. Amen.
