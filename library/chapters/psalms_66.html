Donaldson●●○○○

The structure of Psalm 67, a hymn of praise, serves to emphasize the prayer, positioned at the center, that all peoples might praise God. The psalmist's wishes that God might bless Israel and that the nations might praise God are underscored by matched repetition (David A. Dorsey, The Literary Structure of the Old Testament)
