A righteous woman is a student of the scriptures. Many apply uniquely to her life (see Genesis 27:46; Psalm 113:9; Proverbs 31:10–31; Ephesians 5:22–33; Colossians 3:18; Titus 2:3–5; Jacob 3:7; D&C 25). In the scriptures she finds “great treasures of knowledge, even hidden treasures” (D&C 89:19). — Russell M. Nelson, in Conference Report, Oct. 1989, 27; or Ensign, Nov. 1989, 22
