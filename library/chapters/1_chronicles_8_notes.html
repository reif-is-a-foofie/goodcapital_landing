Benjamin begot Bela his first-born, Ashbel the second. The text says “Ashbel the second” when he was really the third; for the sons of Benjamin were Bela, Becher, and Ashbel (Gen 46:21). Indeed, above it does say “Bela, Becher” (7:6). But because it does not mention Becher [here], it says “Ashbel the second.” It does not mention Becher here much as it does not mention him in parashat Pineḥas—just: “Of Bela, the clan of the Belaites; of Ashbel, the clan of the Ashbelites” (Num 26:38).
