6 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">Whereupon the princes of Israel and the king humbled themselves; and they said, The LORD [is] righteous.

8 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.844">Nevertheless they shall be his servants; that they may know my service, and the service of the kingdoms of the countries.

16 And Rehoboam <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.942">slept with his fathers, and was buried in the city of David: and Abijah his son reigned in his stead.
