This psalm was composed when the Ziphites, men of the same tribe with David, instigated Saul to destroy him, and directed him how to accomplish it, 1 Sam. 23-24.

A prayer for help against enemies, Psalm 54 opens with an introductory plea for deliverance and closes with a matching report of deliver ante- The description of the threat of enemy aggression is balanced by the prayer that God will destroy the enemies.
