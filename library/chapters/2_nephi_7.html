2 Nephi

7

1 Yea, for thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_7_10.744">Lord: Have I put thee away, or have I cast thee off forever? For thus saith the Lord: Where is the bill of your mother's divorcement? To whom have I put thee away, or to which of my creditors have I sold you? Yea, to whom have I sold you? Behold, for your iniquities have ye sold yourselves, and for your transgressions is your mother put away.
2 Wherefore, when I came, there was no man; when I called, yea, there was none to answer. O house of Israel, is my hand shortened at all that it cannot redeem, or have I no power to deliver? <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_7_20.640">Behold, at my rebuke I dry up the sea, I make their rivers a wilderness and their fish to stink because the waters are dried up, and they die because of thirst.
3 I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.975">clothe the heavens with blackness, and I make sackcloth their covering.
4 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_7_40.533">Lord God hath given me the tongue of the learned, that I should know how to speak a word in season unto thee, O house of Israel. When ye are weary he waketh morning by morning. He waketh mine ear to hear as the learned.
5 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_7_50.906">Lord God hath opened mine ear, and I was not rebellious, neither turned away back.
6 I gave my back to the smiter, and my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_isaiah_50_60.851">cheeks to them that plucked off the hair. I hid not my face from shame and spitting.
7 For the Lord God will help me, therefore shall I not be confounded. Therefore have I set my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.575">face like a flint, and I know that I shall not be ashamed.
8 And the Lord is near, and he justifieth me. Who will <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_7_80.794">contend with me? Let us stand together. Who is mine adversary? Let him come near me, and I will smite him with the strength of my mouth.
9 For the Lord God will help me. And all they who shall condemn me, behold, all they shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_7_90.769">wax old as a garment, and the moth shall eat them up.
10 Who is among <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.835">you that feareth the Lord, that obeyeth the voice of his servant, that walketh in darkness and hath no light?
11 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.898">Behold all ye that kindle fire, that compass yourselves about with sparks, walk in the light of your fire and in the sparks which ye have kindled. This shall ye have of mine hand--ye shall lie down in sorrow.
