2 Nephi

24

1 FOR the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.695">Lord will have mercy on Jacob, and will yet choose Israel, and set them in their own land; and the strangers shall be joined with them, and they shall cleave to the house of Jacob.
2 And the people shall take them and bring them to their place; yea, from far unto the ends of the earth; and they shall return to their lands of promise. And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.658">house of Israel shall possess them, and the land of the Lord shall be for servants and handmaids; and they shall take them captives unto whom they were captives; and they shall rule over their oppressors.
3 And it shall come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_24_30.954">pass in that day that the Lord shall give thee rest, from thy sorrow, and from thy fear, and from the hard bondage wherein thou wast made to serve.
4 And it shall come to pass in that day, that thou shalt take up this <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">proverb against the king of Babylon, and say: How hath the oppressor ceased, the golden city ceased!
5 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.787">Lord hath broken the staff of the wicked, the scepters of the rulers.
6 He who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">smote the people in wrath with a continual stroke, he that ruled the nations in anger, is persecuted, and none hindereth.
7 The whole earth is at rest, and is quiet; they break forth into singing.
8 Yea, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_24_80.985">fir-trees rejoice at thee, and also the cedars of Lebanon, saying: Since thou art laid down no feller is come up against us.
9 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.703">Hell from beneath is moved for thee to meet thee at thy coming; it stirreth up the dead for thee, even all the chief ones of the earth; it hath raised up from their thrones all the kings of the nations.
10 All they shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.959">speak and say unto thee: Art thou also become weak as we? Art thou become like unto us?
11 Thy <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.933">pomp is brought down to the grave; the noise of thy viols is not heard; the worm is spread under thee, and the worms cover thee.
12 How art thou fallen from heaven, O Lucifer, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.670">son of the morning! Art thou cut down to the ground, which did weaken the nations!
13 For thou hast said in thy <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.841">heart: I will ascend into heaven, I will exalt my throne above the stars of God; I will sit also upon the mount of the congregation, in the sides of the north;
14 I will ascend above the heights of the clouds; I will be like the Most High.
15 Yet thou shalt be brought down to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.257">hell, to the sides of the pit.
16 They that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_24_160.826">see thee shall narrowly look upon thee, and shall consider thee, and shall say: Is this the man that made the earth to tremble, that did shake kingdoms?
17 And made the world as a wilderness, and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_24_170.974">destroyed the cities thereof, and opened not the house of his prisoners?
18 All the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.913">kings of the nations, yea, all of them, lie in glory, every one of them in his own house.
19 But thou art <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.979">cast out of thy grave like an abominable branch, and the remnant of those that are slain, thrust through with a sword, that go down to the stones of the pit; as a carcass trodden under feet.
20 Thou shalt not be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.702">joined with them in burial, because thou hast destroyed thy land and slain thy people; the seed of evil-doers shall never be renowned.
21 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.928">Prepare slaughter for his children for the iniquities of their fathers, that they do not rise, nor possess the land, nor fill the face of the world with cities.
22 For I will <span class="cw" data-tgt="ptimes_and_seasonstimes___seasons__times_and_seasons_abbyy0.289">rise up against them, saith the Lord of Hosts, and cut off from Babylon the name, and remnant, and son, and nephew, saith the Lord.
23 I will also make it a possession for the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.819">bittern, and pools of water; and I will sweep it with the besom of destruction, saith the Lord of Hosts.
24 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.963">Lord of Hosts hath sworn, saying: Surely as I have thought, so shall it come to pass; and as I have purposed, so shall it stand--
25 That I will bring the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_24_250.893">Assyrian in my land, and upon my mountains tread him under foot; then shall his yoke depart from off them, and his burden depart from off their shoulders.
26 This is the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.636">purpose that is purposed upon the whole earth; and this is the hand that is stretched out upon all nations.
27 For the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.870">Lord of Hosts hath purposed, and who shall disannul? And his hand is stretched out,
28 In the year that king Ahaz died was this burden.
29 Rejoice not thou, whole Palestina, because the rod of him that smote thee is broken; for out of the serpent's <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.970">root shall come forth a cockatrice, and his fruit shall be a fiery flying serpent.
30 And the first-<span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.829">born of the poor shall feed, and the needy shall lie down in safety; and I will kill thy root with famine, and he shall slay thy remnant.
31 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.888">Howl, O gate; cry, O city; thou, whole Palestina, art dissolved; for there shall come from the north a smoke, and none shall be alone in his appointed times.
32 What shall then answer the messengers of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_nephi_24_320.771">nations? That the Lord hath founded Zion, and the poor of his people shall trust in it.
