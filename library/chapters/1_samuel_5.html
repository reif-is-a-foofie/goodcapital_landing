9 And it was [so], that, after they had <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.983">carried it about, the hand of the LORD was against the city with a very great destruction: and he smote the men of the city, both small and great, and they had emerods in their secret parts.

11 So they sent and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.906">gathered together all the lords of the Philistines, and said, Send away the ark of the God of Israel, and let it go again to his own place, that it slay us not, and our people: for there was a deadly destruction throughout all the city; the hand of God was very heavy there.
12 And the men that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.838">died not were smitten with the emerods: and the cry of the city went up to heaven.
