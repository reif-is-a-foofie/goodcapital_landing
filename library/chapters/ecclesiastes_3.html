1 TO every [thing there is] a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.413">season, and a time to every purpose under the heaven:
2 A time to be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.843">born, and a time to die; a time to plant, and a time to pluck up [that which is] planted;
3 A time to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.937">kill, and a time to heal; a time to break down, and a time to build up;
4 A time to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjournal_of_discoursesjd_vol_150.252">weep, and a time to laugh; a time to mourn, and a time to dance;
5 A time to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.916">cast away stones, and a time to gather stones together; a time to embrace, and a time to refrain from embracing;
6 A time to get, and a time to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pancient_textsancient_texts__book_of_jubilees0.289">lose; a time to keep, and a time to cast away;
7 A time to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">rend, and a time to sew; a time to keep silence, and a time to speak;
8 A time to love, and a time to hate; a time of war, and a time of peace.
9 What profit hath he that worketh in that wherein he laboureth?
10 I have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.933">seen the travail, which God hath given to the sons of men to be exercised in it.
11 He hath made every [thing] <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.846">beautiful in his time: also he hath set the world in their heart, so that no man can find out the work that God maketh from the beginning to the end.
12 I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.478">know that [there is] no good in them, but for [a man] to rejoice, and to do good in his life.
13 And also that every man should <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.937">eat and drink, and enjoy the good of all his labour, it [is] the gift of God.
14 I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.882">know that, whatsoever God doeth, it shall be for ever: nothing can be put to it, nor any thing taken from it: and God doeth [it], that [men] should fear before him.
15 That which hath been is now; and that which is to be hath <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.866">already been; and God requireth that which is past.
16 And moreover I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.933">saw under the sun the place of judgment, [that] wickedness [was] there; and the place of righteousness, [that] iniquity [was] there.
17 I said in <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.943">mine heart, God shall judge the righteous and the wicked: for [there is] a time there for every purpose and for every work.
18 I said in <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.778">mine heart concerning the estate of the sons of men, that God might manifest them, and that they might see that they themselves are beasts.
19 For that which <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.906">befalleth the sons of men befalleth beasts; even one thing befalleth them: as the one dieth, so dieth the other; yea, they have all one breath; so that a man hath no preeminence above a beast: for all [is] vanity.
20 All go unto one place; all are of the dust, and all turn to dust again.
21 Who knoweth the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.994">spirit of man that goeth upward, and the spirit of the beast that goeth downward to the earth?
22 Wherefore I perceive that [there is] <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.947">nothing better, than that a man should rejoice in his own works; for that [is] his portion: for who shall bring him to see what shall be after him?
