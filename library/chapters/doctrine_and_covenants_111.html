Doctrine and Covenants

111

1 I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Lord your God, am not displeased with your coming this journey, notwithstanding your follies.
2 I have much <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">treasure in this city for you, for the benefit of Zion, and many people in this city, whom I will gather out in due time for the benefit of Zion, through your instrumentality.
3 Therefore, it is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">expedient that you should form acquaintance with men in this city, as you shall be led, and as it shall be given you.
4 And it shall come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">pass in due time that I will give this city into your hands, that you shall have power over it, insomuch that they shall not discover your secret parts; and its wealth pertaining to gold and silver shall be yours.
5 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Concern not yourselves about your debts, for I will give you power to pay them.
6 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Concern not yourselves about Zion, for I will deal mercifully with her.
7 Tarry in this place, and in the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.417">regions round about;
8 And the place where it is my will that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you should tarry, for the main, shall be signalized unto you by the peace and power of my Spirit, that shall flow unto you.
9 This place you may obtain by hire. And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">inquire diligently concerning the more ancient inhabitants and founders of this city;
10 For there are more <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.965">treasures than one for you in this city.
11 Therefore, be ye as <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.962">wise as serpents and yet without sin; and I will order all things for your good, as fast as ye are able to receive them. Amen.
