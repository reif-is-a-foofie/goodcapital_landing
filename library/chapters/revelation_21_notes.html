first heaven and the first earth were passed away. Heaven and earth as we know them will cease to exist, having "passed away," as we pass away in death. no more sea "The abode of chaos, the home of the satanic beast, the old seat over which the whore held sway, has vanished. But the sea and the abyss are one and the same. Therefore, the destruction of the sea represents the annihilation of the primeval deep, the home of malevolence and evil.
