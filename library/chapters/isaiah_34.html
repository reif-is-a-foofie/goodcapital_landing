Donaldson Commentary

Now, some Bible commentators, because the name of Idumea, a little country east of the Jordan, is mentioned, have an idea that this had reference to that little country; but the term Idumea is one that the Lord uses to mean the world. You will find it so recorded in Section 1 of the Doctrine and Covenants (See D&C 1:36.). He is speaking of the world.. . . This prophecy had nothing to do with that little country called Idumea but to the nations of the earth. (Joseph Fielding Smith, Signs of the Times, pp. 150-51.)
