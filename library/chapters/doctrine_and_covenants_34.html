Doctrine and Covenants

34

1 My son Orson, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">hearken and hear and behold what I, the Lord God, shall say unto you, even Jesus Christ your Redeemer;
2 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">light and the life of the world, a light which shineth in darkness and the darkness comprehendeth it not;
3 Who so loved the world that he gave his own life, that as many as would <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">believe might become the sons of God. Wherefore you are my son;
4 And blessed are you because you have believed;
5 And more blessed are <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you because you are called of me to preach my gospel—
6 To <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">lift up your voice as with the sound of a trump, both long and loud, and cry repentance unto a crooked and perverse generation, preparing the way of the Lord for his second coming.
7 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">behold, verily, verily, I say unto you, the time is soon at hand that I shall come in a cloud with power and great glory.
8 And it shall be a <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">great day at the time of my coming, for all nations shall tremble.
9 But before that great day shall come, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">sun shall be darkened, and the moon be turned into blood; and the stars shall refuse their shining, and some shall fall, and great destructions await the wicked.
10 Wherefore, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.954">lift up your voice and spare not, for the Lord God hath spoken; therefore prophesy, and it shall be given by the power of the Holy Ghost.
11 And if <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.782">you are faithful, behold, I am with you until I come—
12 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.931">verily, verily, I say unto you, I come quickly. I am your Lord and your Redeemer. Even so. Amen.
