Hell shall rage against thee. Every truth of salvation is attested to by two witnesses: (1) the sweet, quiet whisperings of the Spirit and (2) the loud, ugly, and rancorous hollering of the adversary. If the prince of darkness and his legions do not oppose a doctrine, fighting and warring against it, we can have every assurance it is not a principle of salvation. The importance of a doctrine can always be measured in Satan's opposition to it. Standing opposite the greatest doctrines may always be found the greatest heresies.
