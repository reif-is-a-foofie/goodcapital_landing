Donaldson Commentary●●●○○Cross-ref · Moroni 10:34●●●○○
"The words or the work which thou hast been writing are true" (D&C 6:17). To the Three Witnesses, prior to their viewing the plates from which the record was translated, the Lord declared, "And he has translated the book, even that part which I have commanded him, and as your Lord and your God liveth it is true" (D&C 17:6). This declaration, "as your Lord and your God liveth," was the most binding and solemn of all oaths, in ancient times when an oath was still considered sacred.
