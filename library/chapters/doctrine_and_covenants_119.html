Here we see that the law of tithing is not a remote Old Testament practice but a commandment directly from the Savior to the people of our day. The Lord reaffirmed that law in modern revelation, commanding his people to pay "one-tenth of all their interest annually" and declaring that "this shall be a standing law unto them forever" (D&C 119:4). (Dallin H. Oaks, in Conference Report, Apr. 1994, 43; or Ensign, May 1994, 33)
