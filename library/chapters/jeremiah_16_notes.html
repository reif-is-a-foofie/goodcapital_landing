and eating together a symbol of fellowship. In addition, Jeremiah was commanded to explain very clearly to the people the reasons for his actions as well as the reasons for their coming punishment. (Old Testament Student Manual: 1 Kings-Malachi, p. 241)

In the evening I again met with a large assembly of the Saints and strangers, and while singing the first hymn the spirit of the Lord rested upon me and the voice of God said to me, "This is the last meeting that you will hold with this people for many days."
