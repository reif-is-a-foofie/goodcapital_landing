Doctrine and Covenants

63

1 Hearken, O ye people, and open your hearts and give <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">ear from afar; and listen, you that call yourselves the people of the Lord, and hear the word of the Lord and his will concerning you.
2 Yea, verily, I say, hear the word of him whose anger is <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">kindled against the wicked and rebellious;
3 Who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">willeth to take even them whom he will take, and preserveth in life them whom he will preserve;
4 Who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">buildeth up at his own will and pleasure; and destroyeth when he pleases, and is able to cast the soul down to hell.
5 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Behold, I, the Lord, utter my voice, and it shall be obeyed.
6 Wherefore, verily I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">say, let the wicked take heed, and let the rebellious fear and tremble; and let the unbelieving hold their lips, for the day of wrath shall come upon them as a whirlwind, and all flesh shall know that I am God.
7 And he that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">seeketh signs shall see signs, but not unto salvation.
8 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Verily, I say unto you, there are those among you who seek signs, and there have been such even from the beginning;
9 But, behold, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">faith cometh not by signs, but signs follow those that believe.
10 Yea, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.898">signs come by faith, not by the will of men, nor as they please, but by the will of God.
11 Yea, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.975">signs come by faith, unto mighty works, for without faith no man pleaseth God; and with whom God is angry he is not well pleased; wherefore, unto such he showeth no signs, only in wrath unto their condemnation.
12 Wherefore, I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.946">Lord, am not pleased with those among you who have sought after signs and wonders for faith, and not for the good of men unto my glory.
13 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.883">Nevertheless, I give commandments, and many have turned away from my commandments and have not kept them.
14 There were among <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.913">you adulterers and adulteresses; some of whom have turned away from you, and others remain with you that hereafter shall be revealed.
15 Let such beware and repent <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.973">speedily, lest judgment shall come upon them as a snare, and their folly shall be made manifest, and their works shall follow them in the eyes of the people.
16 And verily I say unto you, as I have said before, he that looketh on a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.965">woman to lust after her, or if any shall commit adultery in their hearts, they shall not have the Spirit, but shall deny the faith and shall fear.
17 Wherefore, I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.975">Lord, have said that the fearful, and the unbelieving, and all liars, and whosoever loveth and maketh a lie, and the whoremonger, and the sorcerer, shall have their part in that lake which burneth with fire and brimstone, which is the second death.
18 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.818">Verily I say, that they shall not have part in the first resurrection.
19 And now behold, I, the Lord, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.899">say unto you that ye are not justified, because these things are among you.
20 Nevertheless, he that endureth in <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.944">faith and doeth my will, the same shall overcome, and shall receive an inheritance upon the earth when the day of transfiguration shall come;
21 When the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.955">earth shall be transfigured, even according to the pattern which was shown unto mine apostles upon the mount; of which account the fulness ye have not yet received.
22 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.953">verily I say unto you, that as I said that I would make known my will unto you, behold I will make it known unto you, not by the way of commandment, for there are many who observe not to keep my commandments.
23 But unto him that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.945">keepeth my commandments I will give the mysteries of my kingdom, and the same shall be in him a well of living water, springing up unto everlasting life.
24 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.964">behold, this is the will of the Lord your God concerning his saints, that they should assemble themselves together unto the land of Zion, not in haste, lest there should be confusion, which bringeth pestilence.
25 Behold, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.886">land of Zion—I, the Lord, hold it in mine own hands;
26 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.302">Nevertheless, I, the Lord, render unto Cæsar the things which are Cæsar's.
27 Wherefore, I the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.953">Lord will that you should purchase the lands, that you may have advantage of the world, that you may have claim on the world, that they may not be stirred up unto anger.
28 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.910">Satan putteth it into their hearts to anger against you, and to the shedding of blood.
29 Wherefore, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.929">land of Zion shall not be obtained but by purchase or by blood, otherwise there is none inheritance for you.
30 And if by purchase, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.265">behold you are blessed;
31 And if by <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.961">blood, as you are forbidden to shed blood, lo, your enemies are upon you, and ye shall be scourged from city to city, and from synagogue to synagogue, and but few shall stand to receive an inheritance.
32 I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.923">Lord, am angry with the wicked; I am holding my Spirit from the inhabitants of the earth.
33 I have sworn in my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.945">wrath, and decreed wars upon the face of the earth, and the wicked shall slay the wicked, and fear shall come upon every man;
34 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.940">saints also shall hardly escape; nevertheless, I, the Lord, am with them, and will come down in heaven from the presence of my Father and consume the wicked with unquenchable fire.
35 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.271">behold, this is not yet, but by and by.
36 Wherefore, seeing that I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.937">Lord, have decreed all these things upon the face of the earth, I will that my saints should be assembled upon the land of Zion;
37 And that every man should take righteousness in his <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.965">hands and faithfulness upon his loins, and lift a warning voice unto the inhabitants of the earth; and declare both by word and by flight that desolation shall come upon the wicked.
38 Wherefore, let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.941">disciples in Kirtland arrange their temporal concerns, who dwell upon this farm.
39 Let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.978">servant Titus Billings, who has the care thereof, dispose of the land, that he may be prepared in the coming spring to take his journey up unto the land of Zion, with those that dwell upon the face thereof, excepting those whom I shall reserve unto myself, that shall not go until I shall command them.
40 And let all the moneys which can be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.954">spared, it mattereth not unto me whether it be little or much, be sent up unto the land of Zion, unto them whom I have appointed to receive.
41 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.961">Behold, I, the Lord, will give unto my servant Joseph Smith, Jun., power that he shall be enabled to discern by the Spirit those who shall go up unto the land of Zion, and those of my disciples who shall tarry.
42 Let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.943">servant Newel K. Whitney retain his store, or in other words, the store, yet for a little season.
43 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.918">Nevertheless, let him impart all the money which he can impart, to be sent up unto the land of Zion.
44 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.891">Behold, these things are in his own hands, let him do according to wisdom.
45 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.948">Verily I say, let him be ordained as an agent unto the disciples that shall tarry, and let him be ordained unto this power;
46 And now <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.951">speedily visit the churches, expounding these things unto them, with my servant Oliver Cowdery. Behold, this is my will, obtaining moneys even as I have directed.
47 He that is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.861">faithful and endureth shall overcome the world.
48 He that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.944">sendeth up treasures unto the land of Zion shall receive an inheritance in this world, and his works shall follow him, and also a reward in the world to come.
49 Yea, and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.965">blessed are the dead that die in the Lord, from henceforth, when the Lord shall come, and old things shall pass away, and all things become new, they shall rise from the dead and shall not die after, and shall receive an inheritance before the Lord, in the holy city.
50 And he that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.933">liveth when the Lord shall come, and hath kept the faith, blessed is he; nevertheless, it is appointed to him to die at the age of man.
51 Wherefore, children shall grow up until they become old; old men shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.946">die; but they shall not sleep in the dust, but they shall be changed in the twinkling of an eye.
52 Wherefore, for this <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.908">cause preached the apostles unto the world the resurrection of the dead.
53 These things are the things that ye must <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.936">look for; and, speaking after the manner of the Lord, they are now nigh at hand, and in a time to come, even in the day of the coming of the Son of Man.
54 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.962">until that hour there will be foolish virgins among the wise; and at that hour cometh an entire separation of the righteous and the wicked; and in that day will I send mine angels to pluck out the wicked and cast them into unquenchable fire.
55 And now <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.948">behold, verily I say unto you, I, the Lord, am not pleased with my servant Sidney Rigdon; he exalted himself in his heart, and received not counsel, but grieved the Spirit;
56 Wherefore his <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.946">writing is not acceptable unto the Lord, and he shall make another; and if the Lord receive it not, behold he standeth no longer in the office to which I have appointed him.
57 And again, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.941">verily I say unto you, those who desire in their hearts, in meekness, to warn sinners to repentance, let them be ordained unto this power.
58 For this is a day of <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.901">warning, and not a day of many words. For I, the Lord, am not to be mocked in the last days.
59 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.938">Behold, I am from above, and my power lieth beneath. I am over all, and in all, and through all, and search all things, and the day cometh that all things shall be subject unto me.
60 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.596">Behold, I am Alpha and Omega, even Jesus Christ.
61 Wherefore, let all men <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.871">beware how they take my name in their lips—
62 For behold, verily I say, that many there be who are under this condemnation, who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.925">use the name of the Lord, and use it in vain, having not authority.
63 Wherefore, let the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.888">church repent of their sins, and I, the Lord, will own them; otherwise they shall be cut off.
64 Remember that that which <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.958">cometh from above is sacred, and must be spoken with care, and by constraint of the Spirit; and in this there is no condemnation, and ye receive the Spirit through prayer; wherefore, without this there remaineth condemnation.
65 Let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.929">servants, Joseph Smith, Jun., and Sidney Rigdon, seek them a home, as they are taught through prayer by the Spirit.
66 These things <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.944">remain to overcome through patience, that such may receive a more exceeding and eternal weight of glory, otherwise, a greater condemnation. Amen.
