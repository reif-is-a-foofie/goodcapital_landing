Isaiah

35

1 THE <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.523">wilderness and the solitary place shall be glad for them; and the desert shall rejoice, and blossom as the rose.
2 It shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.705">blossom abundantly, and rejoice even with joy and singing: the glory of Lebanon shall be given unto it, the excellency of Carmel and Sharon, they shall see the glory of the LORD, [and] the excellency of our God.
3 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.745">Strengthen ye the weak hands, and confirm the feeble knees.
4 Say to them [that are] of a fearful heart, Be strong, fear not: behold, your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjournal_of_discoursesjd_vol_150.409">God will come [with] vengeance, [even] God [with] a recompence; he will come and save you.
5 Then the eyes of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">blind shall be opened, and the ears of the deaf shall be unstopped.
6 Then shall the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">lame [man] leap as an hart, and the tongue of the dumb sing: for in the wilderness shall waters break out, and streams in the desert.
7 And the parched ground shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">become a pool, and the thirsty land springs of water: in the habitation of dragons, where each lay, [shall be] grass with reeds and rushes.
8 And an <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.686">highway shall be there, and a way, and it shall be called The way of holiness; the unclean shall not pass over it; but it [shall be] for those: the wayfaring men, though fools, shall not err [therein].
9 No <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">lion shall be there, nor [any] ravenous beast shall go up thereon, it shall not be found there; but the redeemed shall walk [there]:
10 And the ransomed of the LORD shall return, and come to Zion with songs and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.997">everlasting joy upon their heads: they shall obtain joy and gladness, and sorrow and sighing shall flee away.
