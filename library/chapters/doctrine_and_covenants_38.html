Doctrine and Covenants

38

1 Thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Lord your God, even Jesus Christ, the Great I AM, Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end, the same which looked upon the wide expanse of eternity, and all the seraphic hosts of heaven, before the world was made;
2 The same which <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">knoweth all things, for all things are present before mine eyes;
3 I am the same which <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">spake, and the world was made, and all things came by me.
4 I am the same which have taken the Zion of Enoch into mine own bosom; and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily, I say, even as many as have believed in my name, for I am Christ, and in mine own name, by the virtue of the blood which I have spilt, have I pleaded before the Father for them.
5 But behold, the residue of the wicked have I kept in <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">chains of darkness until the judgment of the great day, which shall come at the end of the earth;
6 And even so will I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">cause the wicked to be kept, that will not hear my voice but harden their hearts, and wo, wo, wo, is their doom.
7 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">behold, verily, verily, I say unto you that mine eyes are upon you. I am in your midst and ye cannot see me;
8 But the day <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">soon cometh that ye shall see me, and know that I am; for the veil of darkness shall soon be rent, and he that is not purified shall not abide the day.
9 Wherefore, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">gird up your loins and be prepared. Behold, the kingdom is yours, and the enemy shall not overcome.
10 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.885">Verily I say unto you, ye are clean, but not all; and there is none else with whom I am well pleased;
11 For all <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.942">flesh is corrupted before me; and the powers of darkness prevail upon the earth, among the children of men, in the presence of all the hosts of heaven—
12 Which causeth silence to reign, and all eternity is pained, and the angels are <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.969">waiting the great command to reap down the earth, to gather the tares that they may be burned; and, behold, the enemy is combined.
13 And now I show unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.957">you a mystery, a thing which is had in secret chambers, to bring to pass even your destruction in process of time, and ye knew it not;
14 But now I tell it unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.951">you, and ye are blessed, not because of your iniquity, neither your hearts of unbelief; for verily some of you are guilty before me, but I will be merciful unto your weakness.
15 Therefore, be ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.915">strong from henceforth; fear not, for the kingdom is yours.
16 And for your salvation I give unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.945">you a commandment, for I have heard your prayers, and the poor have complained before me, and the rich have I made, and all flesh is mine, and I am no respecter of persons.
17 And I have made the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.915">earth rich, and behold it is my footstool, wherefore, again I will stand upon it.
18 And I hold forth and deign to give unto you greater riches, even a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.959">land of promise, a land flowing with milk and honey, upon which there shall be no curse when the Lord cometh;
19 And I will give it unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.872">you for the land of your inheritance, if you seek it with all your hearts.
20 And this shall be my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.970">covenant with you, ye shall have it for the land of your inheritance, and for the inheritance of your children forever, while the earth shall stand, and ye shall possess it again in eternity, no more to pass away.
21 But, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.929">verily I say unto you that in time ye shall have no king nor ruler, for I will be your king and watch over you.
22 Wherefore, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.960">hear my voice and follow me, and you shall be a free people, and ye shall have no laws but my laws when I come, for I am your lawgiver, and what can stay my hand?
23 But, verily I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.919">say unto you, teach one another according to the office wherewith I have appointed you;
24 And let every man <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.926">esteem his brother as himself, and practice virtue and holiness before me.
25 And again I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.870">say unto you, let every man esteem his brother as himself.
26 For what man among <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.981">you having twelve sons, and is no respecter of them, and they serve him obediently, and he saith unto the one: Be thou clothed in robes and sit thou here; and to the other: Be thou clothed in rags and sit thou there—and looketh upon his sons and saith I am just?
27 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.911">Behold, this I have given unto you as a parable, and it is even as I am. I say unto you, be one; and if ye are not one ye are not mine.
28 And again, I say unto you that the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.923">enemy in the secret chambers seeketh your lives.
29 Ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.971">hear of wars in far countries, and you say that there will soon be great wars in far countries, but ye know not the hearts of men in your own land.
30 I tell <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.977">you these things because of your prayers; wherefore, treasure up wisdom in your bosoms, lest the wickedness of men reveal these things unto you by their wickedness, in a manner which shall speak in your ears with a voice louder than that which shall shake the earth; but if ye are prepared ye shall not fear.
31 And that ye might <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.945">escape the power of the enemy, and be gathered unto me a righteous people, without spot and blameless—
32 Wherefore, for this <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.943">cause I gave unto you the commandment that ye should go to the Ohio; and there I will give unto you my law; and there you shall be endowed with power from on high;
33 And from thence, whosoever I will shall go forth among all <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.961">nations, and it shall be told them what they shall do; for I have a great work laid up in store, for Israel shall be saved, and I will lead them whithersoever I will, and no power shall stay my hand.
34 And now, I give unto the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.941">church in these parts a commandment, that certain men among them shall be appointed, and they shall be appointed by the voice of the church;
35 And they shall look to the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.955">poor and the needy, and administer to their relief that they shall not suffer; and send them forth to the place which I have commanded them;
36 And this shall be their work, to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.909">govern the affairs of the property of this church.
37 And they that have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.921">farms that cannot be sold, let them be left or rented as seemeth them good.
38 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.943">See that all things are preserved; and when men are endowed with power from on high and sent forth, all these things shall be gathered unto the bosom of the church.
39 And if ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.971">seek the riches which it is the will of the Father to give unto you, ye shall be the richest of all people, for ye shall have the riches of eternity; and it must needs be that the riches of the earth are mine to give; but beware of pride, lest ye become as the Nephites of old.
40 And again, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.950">say unto you, I give unto you a commandment, that every man, both elder, priest, teacher, and also member, go to with his might, with the labor of his hands, to prepare and accomplish the things which I have commanded.
41 And let your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.935">preaching be the warning voice, every man to his neighbor, in mildness and in meekness.
42 And go ye out from among the wicked. Save yourselves. Be ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.930">clean that bear the vessels of the Lord. Even so. Amen.
