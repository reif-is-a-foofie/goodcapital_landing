3 Nephi

11

1 AND now it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.276">pass that there were a great multitude gathered together, of the people of Nephi, round about the temple which was in the land Bountiful; and they were marveling and wondering one with another, and were showing one to another the great and marvelous change which had taken place.
2 And they were also conversing about this Jesus Christ, of whom the sign had been given concerning his death.
3 And it came to pass that while they were thus conversing one with another, they <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_11_30.616">heard a voice as if it came out of heaven; and they cast their eyes round about, for they understood not the voice which they heard; and it was not a harsh voice, neither was it a loud voice; nevertheless, and notwithstanding it being a small voice it did pierce them that did hear to the center, insomuch that there was no part of their frame that it did not cause to quake; yea, it did pierce them to the very soul, and did cause their hearts to burn.
4 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.318">pass that again they heard the voice, and they understood it not.
5 And again the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_11_30.502">third time they did hear the voice, and did open their ears to hear it; and their eyes were towards the sound thereof; and they did look steadfastly towards heaven, from whence the sound came.
6 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pgeneral_conferencegc__general_conference_1981_10_my_sheep_hear_my_voice0.268">behold, the third time they did understand the voice which they heard; and it said unto them:
7 Behold my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_mark_1_110.498">Beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased, in whom I have glorified my name--hear ye him.
8 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_kings_21_260.498">pass, as they understood they cast their eyes up again towards heaven; and behold, they saw a Man descending out of heaven; and he was clothed in a white robe; and he came down and stood in the midst of them; and the eyes of the whole multitude were turned upon him, and they durst not open their mouths, even one to another, and wist not what it meant, for they thought it was an angel that had appeared unto them.
9 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_11_90.716">pass that he stretched forth his hand and spake unto the people, saying:
10 Behold, I am <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_11_90.642">Jesus Christ, whom the prophets testified shall come into the world.
11 And behold, I am the light and the life of the world; and I have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_11_90.567">drunk out of that bitter cup which the Father hath given me, and have glorified the Father in taking upon me the sins of the world, in the which I have suffered the will of the Father in all things from the beginning.
12 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_2_kings_21_260.349">pass that when Jesus had spoken these words the whole multitude fell to the earth; for they remembered that it had been prophesied among them that Christ should show himself unto them after his ascension into heaven.
13 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.415">pass that the Lord spake unto them saying:
14 Arise and come forth unto me, that ye may <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moses_1_60.518">thrust your hands into my side, and also that ye may feel the prints of the nails in my hands and in my feet, that ye may know that I am the God of Israel, and the God of the whole earth, and have been slain for the sins of the world.
15 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.442">pass that the multitude went forth, and thrust their hands into his side, and did feel the prints of the nails in his hands and in his feet; and this they did do, going forth one by one until they had all gone forth, and did see with their eyes and did feel with their hands, and did know of a surety and did bear record, that it was he, of whom it was written by the prophets, that should come.
16 And when they had all gone forth and had witnessed for themselves, they did cry out with one accord, saying:
17 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.368">Hosanna! Blessed be the name of the Most High God! And they did fall down at the feet of Jesus, and did worship him.
18 And it came to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.277">pass that he spake unto Nephi (for Nephi was among the multitude) and he commanded him that he should come forth.
19 And Nephi arose and went forth, and bowed himself before the Lord and did kiss his feet.

20 And the Lord commanded him that he should arise. And he arose and stood before him.
21 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.320">Lord said unto him: I give unto you power that ye shall baptize this people when I am again ascended into heaven.
22 And again the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.425">Lord called others, and said unto them likewise; and he gave unto them power to baptize. And he said unto them: On this wise shall ye baptize; and there shall be no disputations among you.
23 Verily I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.409">say unto you, that whoso repenteth of his sins through your words, and desireth to be baptized in my name, on this wise shall ye baptize them--Behold, ye shall go down and stand in the water, and in my name shall ye baptize them.
24 And now behold, these are the words which ye shall say, calling them by name, saying:
25 Having authority given me of Jesus Christ, I baptize you in the name of the Father, and of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.481">Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Amen.
26 And then shall ye <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.668">immerse them in the water, and come forth again out of the water.
27 And after this manner shall ye baptize in my name; for behold, verily I say unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.360">you, that the Father, and the Son, and the Holy Ghost are one; and I am in the Father, and the Father in me, and the Father and I are one.
28 And according as I have commanded <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_acts_15_20.477">you thus shall ye baptize. And there shall be no disputations among you, as there have hitherto been; neither shall there be disputations among you concerning the points of my doctrine, as there have hitherto been.
29 For verily, verily I say unto you, he that hath the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_acts_15_20.613">spirit of contention is not of me, but is of the devil, who is the father of contention, and he stirreth up the hearts of men to contend with anger, one with another.
30 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_acts_15_20.494">Behold, this is not my doctrine, to stir up the hearts of men with anger, one against another; but this is my doctrine, that such things should be done away.
31 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.456">Behold, verily, verily, I say unto you, I will declare unto you my doctrine.
32 And this is my doctrine, and it is the doctrine which the Father hath given unto me; and I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.392">bear record of the Father, and the Father beareth record of me, and the Holy Ghost beareth record of the Father and me; and I bear record that the Father commandeth all men, everywhere, to repent and believe in me.
33 And whoso <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.553">believeth in me, and is baptized, the same shall be saved; and they are they who shall inherit the kingdom of God.
34 And whoso <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.724">believeth not in me, and is not baptized, shall be damned.
35 Verily, verily, I say unto you, that this is my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.321">doctrine, and I bear record of it from the Father; and whoso believeth in me believeth in the Father also; and unto him will the Father bear record of me, for he will visit him with fire and with the Holy Ghost.
36 And thus will the Father <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_11_360.438">bear record of me, and the Holy Ghost will bear record unto him of the Father and me; for the Father, and I, and the Holy Ghost are one.
37 And again I say unto you, ye must repent, and <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_10_190.645">become as a little child, and be baptized in my name, or ye can in nowise receive these things.
38 And again I say unto you, ye must repent, and be baptized in my name, and become as a little child, or ye can in <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_10_190.637">nowise inherit the kingdom of God.
39 Verily, verily, I say unto you, that this is my doctrine, and whoso buildeth upon this buildeth upon my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_3_nephi_10_190.585">rock, and the gates of hell shall not prevail against them.
40 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pmillennial_starmillennial_star__millennial_star_abbyy0.407">whoso shall declare more or less than this, and establish it for my doctrine, the same cometh of evil, and is not built upon my rock; but he buildeth upon a sandy foundation, and the gates of hell stand open to receive such when the floods come and the winds beat upon them.
41 Therefore, go forth unto this people, and declare the words which I have <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.251">spoken, unto the ends of the earth.
