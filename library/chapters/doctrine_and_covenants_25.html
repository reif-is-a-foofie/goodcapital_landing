Doctrine and Covenants

25

1 Hearken unto the voice of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Lord your God, while I speak unto you, Emma Smith, my daughter; for verily I say unto you, all those who receive my gospel are sons and daughters in my kingdom.
2 A revelation I give unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you concerning my will; and if thou art faithful and walk in the paths of virtue before me, I will preserve thy life, and thou shalt receive an inheritance in Zion.
3 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Behold, thy sins are forgiven thee, and thou art an elect lady, whom I have called.
4 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Murmur not because of the things which thou hast not seen, for they are withheld from thee and from the world, which is wisdom in me in a time to come.
5 And the office of thy <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">calling shall be for a comfort unto my servant, Joseph Smith, Jun., thy husband, in his afflictions, with consoling words, in the spirit of meekness.
6 And thou shalt go with him at the time of his going, and be unto him for a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">scribe, while there is no one to be a scribe for him, that I may send my servant, Oliver Cowdery, whithersoever I will.
7 And thou shalt be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">ordained under his hand to expound scriptures, and to exhort the church, according as it shall be given thee by my Spirit.
8 For he shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">lay his hands upon thee, and thou shalt receive the Holy Ghost, and thy time shall be given to writing, and to learning much.
9 And thou needest not fear, for thy <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">husband shall support thee in the church; for unto them is his calling, that all things might be revealed unto them, whatsoever I will, according to their faith.
10 And verily I say unto thee that thou shalt <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.943">lay aside the things of this world, and seek for the things of a better.
11 And it shall be given thee, also, to make a selection of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.968">sacred hymns, as it shall be given thee, which is pleasing unto me, to be had in my church.
12 For my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.956">soul delighteth in the song of the heart; yea, the song of the righteous is a prayer unto me, and it shall be answered with a blessing upon their heads.
13 Wherefore, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.942">lift up thy heart and rejoice, and cleave unto the covenants which thou hast made.
14 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.954">Continue in the spirit of meekness, and beware of pride. Let thy soul delight in thy husband, and the glory which shall come upon him.
15 Keep my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.929">commandments continually, and a crown of righteousness thou shalt receive. And except thou do this, where I am you cannot come.
16 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.899">verily, verily, I say unto you, that this is my voice unto all. Amen.
