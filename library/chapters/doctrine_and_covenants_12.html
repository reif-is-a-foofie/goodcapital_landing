Being Temperate in All Things By Elder Kent D. Watson Of the Seventy Learning to be temperate in all things is a spiritual gift available through the Holy Ghost. In response to the Prophet Joseph Smith’s inquiry, the Lord instructed, “And no one can assist in this work except he shall be humble and full of love, having faith, hope, and charity, being temperate in all things, whatsoever shall be entrusted to his care.” The instruction on being temperate in all things applies to each of us.
