"His Meat Was Locusts and Wild Honey." (Matt. 3:4.) The locust is the most frequently mentioned insect in scripture. Locusts were one of the ten plagues of Egypt, and they have been the periodically recurring plague of the modern Near East and Africa. Major invasions of the desert locust, Schistocerca gregaria, have occurred in recent decades, desolating lands of all their vegetation. A swarm of locusts one square mile can devour four hundred tons a day.
