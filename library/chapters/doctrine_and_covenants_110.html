Doctrine and Covenants

1 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">veil was taken from our minds, and the eyes of our understanding were opened.
2 We <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">saw the Lord standing upon the breastwork of the pulpit, before us; and under his feet was a paved work of pure gold, in color like amber.
3 His <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">eyes were as a flame of fire; the hair of his head was white like the pure snow; his countenance shone above the brightness of the sun; and his voice was as the sound of the rushing of great waters, even the voice of Jehovah, saying:
4 I am the first and the last; I am he who <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">liveth, I am he who was slain; I am your advocate with the Father.
5 Behold, your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">sins are forgiven you; you are clean before me; therefore, lift up your heads and rejoice.
6 Let the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.881">hearts of your brethren rejoice, and let the hearts of all my people rejoice, who have, with their might, built this house to my name.
7 For behold, I have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">accepted this house, and my name shall be here; and I will manifest myself to my people in mercy in this house.
8 Yea, I will <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">appear unto my servants, and speak unto them with mine own voice, if my people will keep my commandments, and do not pollute this holy house.
9 Yea the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">hearts of thousands and tens of thousands shall greatly rejoice in consequence of the blessings which shall be poured out, and the endowment with which my servants have been endowed in this house.
10 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.995">fame of this house shall spread to foreign lands; and this is the beginning of the blessing which shall be poured out upon the heads of my people. Even so. Amen.
11 After this vision closed, the heavens were again opened unto us; and Moses appeared before us, and committed unto us the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.963">keys of the gathering of Israel from the four parts of the earth, and the leading of the ten tribes from the land of the north.
12 After this, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.936">Elias appeared, and committed the dispensation of the gospel of Abraham, saying that in us and our seed all generations after us should be blessed.
13 After this <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.961">vision had closed, another great and glorious vision burst upon us; for Elijah the prophet, who was taken to heaven without tasting death, stood before us, and said:
14 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.968">Behold, the time has fully come, which was spoken of by the mouth of Malachi—testifying that he [Elijah] should be sent, before the great and dreadful day of the Lord come—
15 To <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.699">turn the hearts of the fathers to the children, and the children to the fathers, lest the whole earth be smitten with a curse—
16 Therefore, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.939">keys of this dispensation are committed into your hands; and by this ye may know that the great and dreadful day of the Lord is near, even at the doors.
