1 Who is the Stem of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Jesse spoken of in the 1st, 2d, 3d, 4th, and 5th verses of the 11th chapter of Isaiah?
2 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.418">Verily thus saith the Lord: It is Christ.
3 What is the rod <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">spoken of in the first verse of the 11th chapter of Isaiah, that should come of the Stem of Jesse?
4 Behold, thus saith the Lord: It is a servant in the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">hands of Christ, who is partly a descendant of Jesse as well as of Ephraim, or of the house of Joseph, on whom there is laid much power.
5 What is the root of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Jesse spoken of in the 10th verse of the 11th chapter?
6 Behold, thus saith the Lord, it is a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">descendant of Jesse, as well as of Joseph, unto whom rightly belongs the priesthood, and the keys of the kingdom, for an ensign, and for the gathering of my people in the last days.
7 Questions by Elias Higbee: What is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">meant by the command in Isaiah, 52d chapter, 1st verse, which saith: Put on thy strength, O Zion—and what people had Isaiah reference to?
8 He had reference to those whom <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">God should call in the last days, who should hold the power of priesthood to bring again Zion, and the redemption of Israel; and to put on her strength is to put on the authority of the priesthood, which she, Zion, has a right to by lineage; also to return to that power which she had lost.
9 What are we to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">understand by Zion loosing herself from the bands of her neck; 2d verse?
10 We are to understand that the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.980">scattered remnants are exhorted to return to the Lord from whence they have fallen; which if they do, the promise of the Lord is that he will speak to them, or give them revelation. See the 6th, 7th, and 8th verses. The bands of her neck are the curses of God upon her, or the remnants of Israel in their scattered condition among the Gentiles.
