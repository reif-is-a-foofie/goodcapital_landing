The Prophet Isaiah foresaw the time in which we are now living, with the development of the priesthood administrative system through the present correlation program. I feel that he had reference to us and that the question and answer exchange referred to by the Prophet Joseph Smith in Section 113 of the Doctrine and Covenants has reference to us now.
