Doctrine and Covenants

78

1 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Lord spake unto Joseph Smith, Jun., saying: Hearken unto me, saith the Lord your God, who are ordained unto the high priesthood of my church, who have assembled yourselves together;
2 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">listen to the counsel of him who has ordained you from on high, who shall speak in your ears the words of wisdom, that salvation may be unto you in that thing which you have presented before me, saith the Lord God.
3 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily I say unto you, the time has come, and is now at hand; and behold, and lo, it must needs be that there be an organization of my people, in regulating and establishing the affairs of the storehouse for the poor of my people, both in this place and in the land of Zion—
4 For a permanent and everlasting establishment and order unto my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">church, to advance the cause, which ye have espoused, to the salvation of man, and to the glory of your Father who is in heaven;
5 That you may be equal in the bonds of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">heavenly things, yea, and earthly things also, for the obtaining of heavenly things.
6 For if ye are not equal in <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">earthly things ye cannot be equal in obtaining heavenly things;
7 For if <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you will that I give unto you a place in the celestial world, you must prepare yourselves by doing the things which I have commanded you and required of you.
8 And now, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily thus saith the Lord, it is expedient that all things be done unto my glory, by you who are joined together in this order;
9 Or, in other words, let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">servant Newel K. Whitney and my servant Joseph Smith, Jun., and my servant Sidney Rigdon sit in council with the saints which are in Zion;
10 Otherwise <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.946">Satan seeketh to turn their hearts away from the truth, that they become blinded and understand not the things which are prepared for them.
11 Wherefore, a commandment I give unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.948">you, to prepare and organize yourselves by a bond or everlasting covenant that cannot be broken.
12 And he who breaketh it shall lose his office and standing in the church, and shall be delivered over to the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.946">buffetings of Satan until the day of redemption.
13 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.962">Behold, this is the preparation wherewith I prepare you, and the foundation, and the ensample which I give unto you, whereby you may accomplish the commandments which are given you;
14 That through my providence, notwithstanding the tribulation which shall descend upon you, that the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.949">church may stand independent above all other creatures beneath the celestial world;
15 That <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.966">you may come up unto the crown prepared for you, and be made rulers over many kingdoms, saith the Lord God, the Holy One of Zion, who hath established the foundations of Adam-ondi-Ahman;
16 Who hath appointed Michael your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.970">prince, and established his feet, and set him upon high, and given unto him the keys of salvation under the counsel and direction of the Holy One, who is without beginning of days or end of life.
17 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.952">Verily, verily, I say unto you, ye are little children, and ye have not as yet understood how great blessings the Father hath in his own hands and prepared for you;
18 And ye cannot bear all things now; nevertheless, be of good cheer, for I will <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.955">lead you along. The kingdom is yours and the blessings thereof are yours, and the riches of eternity are yours.
19 And he who <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.948">receiveth all things with thankfulness shall be made glorious; and the things of this earth shall be added unto him, even an hundred fold, yea, more.
20 Wherefore, do the things which I have <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.956">commanded you, saith your Redeemer, even the Son Ahman, who prepareth all things before he taketh you;
21 For ye are the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.934">church of the Firstborn, and he will take you up in a cloud, and appoint every man his portion.
22 And he that is a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.908">faithful and wise steward shall inherit all things. Amen.
