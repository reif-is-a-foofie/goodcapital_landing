1 CONCERNING the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.445">divisions of the porters: Of the Korhites [was] Meshelemiah the son of Kore, of the sons of Asaph.
2 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.978">sons of Meshelemiah [were], Zechariah the firstborn, Jediael the second, Zebadiah the third, Jathniel the fourth,
3 Elam the fifth, Jehohanan the sixth, Elioenai the seventh.
4 Moreover the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.847">sons of Obededom [were], Shemaiah the firstborn, Jehozabad the second, Joah the third, and Sacar the fourth, and Nethaneel the fifth,
5 Ammiel the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.863">sixth, Issachar the seventh, Peulthai the eighth: for God blessed him.
6 Also unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">Shemaiah his son were sons born, that ruled throughout the house of their father: for they [were] mighty men of valour.
7 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">sons of Shemaiah; Othni, and Rephael, and Obed, Elzabad, whose brethren [were] strong men, Elihu, and Semachiah.
8 All these of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.800">sons of Obededom: they and their sons and their brethren, able men for strength for the service, [were] threescore and two of Obededom.
9 And Meshelemiah had sons and brethren, strong men, eighteen.
10 Also <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.900">Hosah, of the children of Merari, had sons; Simri the chief, (for [though] he was not the firstborn, yet his father made him the chief;)
11 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.970">Hilkiah the second, Tebaliah the third, Zechariah the fourth: all the sons and brethren of Hosah [were] thirteen.
12 Among these [were] the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.993">divisions of the porters, [even] among the chief men, [having] wards one against another, to minister in the house of the LORD.
13 And they <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.931">cast lots, as well the small as the great, according to the house of their fathers, for every gate.
14 And the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.967">lot eastward fell to Shelemiah. Then for Zechariah his son, a wise counsellor, they cast lots; and his lot came out northward.
15 To Obededom southward; and to his sons the house of Asuppim.
16 To <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.964">Shuppim and Hosah [the lot came forth] westward, with the gate Shallecheth, by the causeway of the going up, ward against ward.
17 Eastward [were] six Levites, northward four a day, southward four a day, and toward Asuppim two [and] two.
18 At Parbar <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.310">westward, four at the causeway, [and] two at Parbar.
19 These [are] the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.928">divisions of the porters among the sons of Kore, and among the sons of Merari.
20 And of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.992">Levites, Ahijah [was] over the treasures of the house of God, and over the treasures of the dedicated things.
21 [As <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.974">concerning] the sons of Laadan; the sons of the Gershonite Laadan, chief fathers, [even] of Laadan the Gershonite, [were] Jehieli.
22 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.933">sons of Jehieli; Zetham, and Joel his brother, [which were] over the treasures of the house of the LORD.
23 Of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.360">Amramites, [and] the Izharites, the Hebronites, [and] the Uzzielites:
24 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.991">Shebuel the son of Gershom, the son of Moses, [was] ruler of the treasures.
25 And his <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.967">brethren by Eliezer; Rehabiah his son, and Jeshaiah his son, and Joram his son, and Zichri his son, and Shelomith his son.
26 Which <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.995">Shelomith and his brethren [were] over all the treasures of the dedicated things, which David the king, and the chief fathers, the captains over thousands and hundreds, and the captains of the host, had dedicated.
27 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.991">Out of the spoils won in battles did they dedicate to maintain the house of the LORD.
28 And all that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.813">Samuel the seer, and Saul the son of Kish, and Abner the son of Ner, and Joab the son of Zeruiah, had dedicated; [and] whosoever had dedicated [any thing, it was] under the hand of Shelomith, and of his brethren.
29 Of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.529">Izharites, Chenaniah and his sons [were] for the outward business over Israel, for officers and judges.
30 [And] of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.870">Hebronites, Hashabiah and his brethren, men of valour, a thousand and seven hundred, [were] officers among them of Israel on this side Jordan westward in all the business of the LORD, and in the service of the king.
31 Among the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.992">Hebronites [was] Jerijah the chief, [even] among the Hebronites, according to the generations of his fathers. In the fortieth year of the reign of David they were sought for, and there were found among them mighty men of valour at Jazer of Gilead.
32 And his brethren, men of valour, [were] two thousand and seven hundred chief fathers, whom king David made rulers over the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.891">Reubenites, the Gadites, and the half tribe of Manasseh, for every matter pertaining to God, and affairs of the king.
