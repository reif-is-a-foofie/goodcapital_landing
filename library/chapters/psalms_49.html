Donaldson●●●○○

Paul said that God “will have all men to be saved” (1 Timothy 2:4). To our Father in Heaven, “the worth of souls is great” (D&C 18:10), and “the redemption of their soul is precious” (Psalm 49:8). Therefore, God sent his Son, the Savior and Redeemer, to loose the bands of death and atone for the sins of carnal, fallen men. The Lord suffered the pain of all men that all men might come unto him on condition of repentance. (See D&C 18:11–12.) (Jack H. Goaslind, in Conference Report, Oct. 1983, 46; or Ensign, Nov. 1983, 32)
