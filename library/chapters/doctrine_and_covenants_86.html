Doctrine and Covenants

86

1 Verily, thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Lord unto you my servants, concerning the parable of the wheat and of the tares:
2 Behold, verily I say, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">field was the world, and the apostles were the sowers of the seed;
3 And after they have <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">fallen asleep the great persecutor of the church, the apostate, the whore, even Babylon, that maketh all nations to drink of her cup, in whose hearts the enemy, even Satan, sitteth to reign—behold he soweth the tares; wherefore, the tares choke the wheat and drive the church into the wilderness.
4 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">behold, in the last days, even now while the Lord is beginning to bring forth the word, and the blade is springing up and is yet tender—
5 Behold, verily I say unto you, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">angels are crying unto the Lord day and night, who are ready and waiting to be sent forth to reap down the fields;
6 But the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Lord saith unto them, pluck not up the tares while the blade is yet tender (for verily your faith is weak), lest you destroy the wheat also.
7 Therefore, let the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">wheat and the tares grow together until the harvest is fully ripe; then ye shall first gather out the wheat from among the tares, and after the gathering of the wheat, behold and lo, the tares are bound in bundles, and the field remaineth to be burned.
8 Therefore, thus saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Lord unto you, with whom the priesthood hath continued through the lineage of your fathers—
9 For ye are <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">lawful heirs, according to the flesh, and have been hid from the world with Christ in God—
10 Therefore your life and the priesthood have remained, and must needs remain through you and your lineage until the restoration of all things <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.960">spoken by the mouths of all the holy prophets since the world began.
11 Therefore, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.962">blessed are ye if ye continue in my goodness, a light unto the Gentiles, and through this priesthood, a savior unto my people Israel. The Lord hath said it. Amen.
