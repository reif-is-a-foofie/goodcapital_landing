1 AND it came to pass at the end of twenty years, wherein <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.676">Solomon had built the house of the LORD, and his own house,
2 That the cities which <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">Huram had restored to Solomon, Solomon built them, and caused the children of Israel to dwell there.
3 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.329">Solomon went to Hamathzobah, and prevailed against it.
4 And he <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">built Tadmor in the wilderness, and all the store cities, which he built in Hamath.
5 Also he built Bethhoron the upper, and Bethhoron the nether, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.294">fenced cities, with walls, gates, and bars;
6 And Baalath, and all the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">store cities that Solomon had, and all the chariot cities, and the cities of the horsemen, and all that Solomon desired to build in Jerusalem, and in Lebanon, and throughout all the land of his dominion.
7 [As for] all the people [that were] left of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.968">Hittites, and the Amorites, and the Perizzites, and the Hivites, and the Jebusites, which [were] not of Israel,
8 [But] of their <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.928">children, who were left after them in the land, whom the children of Israel consumed not, them did Solomon make to pay tribute until this day.
9 But of the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.606">children of Israel did Solomon make no servants for his work; but they [were] men of war, and chief of his captains, and captains of his chariots and horsemen.
10 And these [were] the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.917">chief of king Solomon's officers, [even] two hundred and fifty, that bare rule over the people.
11 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.890">Solomon brought up the daughter of Pharaoh out of the city of David unto the house that he had built for her: for he said, My wife shall not dwell in the house of David king of Israel, because [the places are] holy, whereunto the ark of the LORD hath come.
12 Then Solomon offered <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.834">burnt offerings unto the LORD on the altar of the LORD, which he had built before the porch,
13 Even after a certain rate every day, offering according to the commandment of Moses, on the sabbaths, and on the new moons, and on the solemn feasts, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.977">three times in the year, [even] in the feast of unleavened bread, and in the feast of weeks, and in the feast of tabernacles.
14 And he appointed, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.956">according to the order of David his father, the courses of the priests to their service, and the Levites to their charges, to praise and minister before the priests, as the duty of every day required: the porters also by their courses at every gate: for so had David the man of God commanded.
15 And they <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.943">departed not from the commandment of the king unto the priests and Levites concerning any matter, or concerning the treasures.
16 Now all the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.951">work of Solomon was prepared unto the day of the foundation of the house of the LORD, and until it was finished. [So] the house of the LORD was perfected.
17 Then went <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.711">Solomon to Ezion-geber, and to Eloth, at the sea side in the land of Edom.
18 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.997">Huram sent him by the hands of his servants ships, and servants that had knowledge of the sea; and they went with the servants of Solomon to Ophir, and took thence four hundred and fifty talents of gold, and brought [them] to king Solomon.
