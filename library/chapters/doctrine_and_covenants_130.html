Doctrine and Covenants

130

1 When the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Savior shall appear we shall see him as he is. We shall see that he is a man like ourselves.
2 And that same <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">sociality which exists among us here will exist among us there, only it will be coupled with eternal glory, which glory we do not now enjoy.
3 John 14:23—The appearing of the Father and the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Son, in that verse, is a personal appearance; and the idea that the Father and the Son dwell in a man's heart is an old sectarian notion, and is false.
4 In answer to the question—Is not the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">reckoning of God's time, angel's time, prophet's time, and man's time, according to the planet on which they reside?
5 I answer, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Yes. But there are no angels who minister to this earth but those who do belong or have belonged to it.
6 The <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_1_nephi_10_190.372">angels do not reside on a planet like this earth;
7 But they <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">reside in the presence of God, on a globe like a sea of glass and fire, where all things for their glory are manifest, past, present, and future, and are continually before the Lord.
8 The place where <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_1_nephi_10_190.365">God resides is a great Urim and Thummim.
9 This <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">earth, in its sanctified and immortal state, will be made like unto crystal and will be a Urim and Thummim to the inhabitants who dwell thereon, whereby all things pertaining to an inferior kingdom, or all kingdoms of a lower order, will be manifest to those who dwell on it; and this earth will be Christ's.
10 Then the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.996">white stone mentioned in Revelation 2:17, will become a Urim and Thummim to each individual who receives one, whereby things pertaining to a higher order of kingdoms will be made known;
11 And a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.964">white stone is given to each of those who come into the celestial kingdom, whereon is a new name written, which no man knoweth save he that receiveth it. The new name is the key word.
12 I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.952">prophesy, in the name of the Lord God, that the commencement of the difficulties which will cause much bloodshed previous to the coming of the Son of Man will be in South Carolina.
13 It may <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.963">probably arise through the slave question. This a voice declared to me, while I was praying earnestly on the subject, December 25th, 1832.
14 I was once <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.993">praying very earnestly to know the time of the coming of the Son of Man, when I heard a voice repeat the following:
15 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.975">Joseph, my son, if thou livest until thou art eighty-five years old, thou shalt see the face of the Son of Man; therefore let this suffice, and trouble me no more on this matter.
16 I was <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.959">left thus, without being able to decide whether this coming referred to the beginning of the millennium or to some previous appearing, or whether I should die and thus see his face.
17 I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.889">believe the coming of the Son of Man will not be any sooner than that time.
18 Whatever <span class="cw" data-tgt="pbh_robertsb_h__roberts__comprehensive_history_of_church0.975">principle of intelligence we attain unto in this life, it will rise with us in the resurrection.
19 And if a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.938">person gains more knowledge and intelligence in this life through his diligence and obedience than another, he will have so much the advantage in the world to come.
20 There is a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.939">law, irrevocably decreed in heaven before the foundations of this world, upon which all blessings are predicated—
21 And when we <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.983">obtain any blessing from God, it is by obedience to that law upon which it is predicated.
22 The Father has a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.963">body of flesh and bones as tangible as man's; the Son also; but the Holy Ghost has not a body of flesh and bones, but is a personage of Spirit. Were it not so, the Holy Ghost could not dwell in us.
23 A man may receive the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.900">Holy Ghost, and it may descend upon him and not tarry with him.
