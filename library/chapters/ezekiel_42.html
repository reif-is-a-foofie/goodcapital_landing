43:1-27. JEHOVAH'S RETURN TO THE TEMPLE.

The high point of the final revelations to Ezekiel is described in this chapter and the next. He saw the return of the Glory of the Lord to the future temple in some of the same imagery he had seen in his call and in the departure of the Spirit of the Lord from the temple (Ezek. 43:1-6; 1:4-28; 3:22-23; 10; 11:23). (Ellis T. Rasmussen, A Latter-day Saint Commentary on the Old Testament, p.612)
