Doctrine and Covenants

50

1 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Hearken, O ye elders of my church, and give ear to the voice of the living God; and attend to the words of wisdom which shall be given unto you, according as ye have asked and are agreed as touching the church, and the spirits which have gone abroad in the earth.
2 Behold, verily I say unto you, that there are many <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">spirits which are false spirits, which have gone forth in the earth, deceiving the world.
3 And also <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Satan hath sought to deceive you, that he might overthrow you.
4 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Behold, I, the Lord, have looked upon you, and have seen abominations in the church that profess my name.
5 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">blessed are they who are faithful and endure, whether in life or in death, for they shall inherit eternal life.
6 But wo unto them that are <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">deceivers and hypocrites, for, thus saith the Lord, I will bring them to judgment.
7 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Behold, verily I say unto you, there are hypocrites among you, who have deceived some, which has given the adversary power; but behold such shall be reclaimed;
8 But the hypocrites shall be detected and shall be <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">cut off, either in life or in death, even as I will; and wo unto them who are cut off from my church, for the same are overcome of the world.
9 Wherefore, let every man <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">beware lest he do that which is not in truth and righteousness before me.
10 And now come, saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.935">Lord, by the Spirit, unto the elders of his church, and let us reason together, that ye may understand;
11 Let us <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.930">reason even as a man reasoneth one with another face to face.
12 Now, when a man <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.963">reasoneth he is understood of man, because he reasoneth as a man; even so will I, the Lord, reason with you that you may understand.
13 Wherefore, I the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.911">Lord ask you this question—unto what were ye ordained?
14 To <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.907">preach my gospel by the Spirit, even the Comforter which was sent forth to teach the truth.
15 And then <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.927">received ye spirits which ye could not understand, and received them to be of God; and in this are ye justified?
16 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.946">Behold ye shall answer this question yourselves; nevertheless, I will be merciful unto you; he that is weak among you hereafter shall be made strong.
17 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.963">Verily I say unto you, he that is ordained of me and sent forth to preach the word of truth by the Comforter, in the Spirit of truth, doth he preach it by the Spirit of truth or some other way?
18 And if it be by some other way it is not of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_daniel_7_180.384">God.
19 And again, he that <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.917">receiveth the word of truth, doth he receive it by the Spirit of truth or some other way?
20 If it be some other way it is not of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_daniel_7_180.384">God.
21 Therefore, why is it that ye cannot <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.956">understand and know, that he that receiveth the word by the Spirit of truth receiveth it as it is preached by the Spirit of truth?
22 Wherefore, he that preacheth and he that receiveth, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.931">understand one another, and both are edified and rejoice together.
23 And that which doth not <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.858">edify is not of God, and is darkness.
24 That which is of <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.973">God is light; and he that receiveth light, and continueth in God, receiveth more light; and that light groweth brighter and brighter until the perfect day.
25 And again, <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.911">verily I say unto you, and I say it that you may know the truth, that you may chase darkness from among you;
26 He that is <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.911">ordained of God and sent forth, the same is appointed to be the greatest, notwithstanding he is the least and the servant of all.
27 Wherefore, he is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.957">possessor of all things; for all things are subject unto him, both in heaven and on the earth, the life and the light, the Spirit and the power, sent forth by the will of the Father through Jesus Christ, his Son.
28 But no man is <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.918">possessor of all things except he be purified and cleansed from all sin.
29 And if ye are <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.943">purified and cleansed from all sin, ye shall ask whatsoever you will in the name of Jesus and it shall be done.
30 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.944">know this, it shall be given you what you shall ask; and as ye are appointed to the head, the spirits shall be subject unto you.
31 Wherefore, it shall come to <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.952">pass, that if you behold a spirit manifested that you cannot understand, and you receive not that spirit, ye shall ask of the Father in the name of Jesus; and if he give not unto you that spirit, then you may know that it is not of God.
32 And it shall be given unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.940">you, power over that spirit; and you shall proclaim against that spirit with a loud voice that it is not of God—
33 Not with <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.942">railing accusation, that ye be not overcome, neither with boasting nor rejoicing, lest you be seized therewith.
34 He that <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.937">receiveth of God, let him account it of God; and let him rejoice that he is accounted of God worthy to receive.
35 And by <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.946">giving heed and doing these things which ye have received, and which ye shall hereafter receive—and the kingdom is given you of the Father, and power to overcome all things which are not ordained of him—
36 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.935">behold, verily I say unto you, blessed are you who are now hearing these words of mine from the mouth of my servant, for your sins are forgiven you.
37 Let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.957">servant Joseph Wakefield, in whom I am well pleased, and my servant Parley P. Pratt go forth among the churches and strengthen them by the word of exhortation;
38 And also my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.957">servant John Corrill, or as many of my servants as are ordained unto this office, and let them labor in the vineyard; and let no man hinder them doing that which I have appointed unto them—
39 Wherefore, in this thing my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.933">servant Edward Partridge is not justified; nevertheless let him repent and he shall be forgiven.
40 Behold, ye are little <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.932">children and ye cannot bear all things now; ye must grow in grace and in the knowledge of the truth.
41 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.892">Fear not, little children, for you are mine, and I have overcome the world, and you are of them that my Father hath given me;
42 And <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.823">none of them that my Father hath given me shall be lost.
43 And the Father and I are <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.891">one. I am in the Father and the Father in me; and inasmuch as ye have received me, ye are in me and I in you.
44 Wherefore, I am in your midst, and I am the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.940">good shepherd, and the stone of Israel. He that buildeth upon this rock shall never fall.
45 And the day <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.860">cometh that you shall hear my voice and see me, and know that I am.
46 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.307">Watch, therefore, that ye may be ready. Even so. Amen.
