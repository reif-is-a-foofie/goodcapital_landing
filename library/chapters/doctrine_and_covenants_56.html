Doctrine and Covenants

56

1 Hearken, O ye people who profess my name, saith the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Lord your God; for behold, mine anger is kindled against the rebellious, and they shall know mine arm and mine indignation, in the day of visitation and of wrath upon the nations.
2 And he that will not take up his <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">cross and follow me, and keep my commandments, the same shall not be saved.
3 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Behold, I, the Lord, command; and he that will not obey shall be cut off in mine own due time, after I have commanded and the commandment is broken.
4 Wherefore I, the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">Lord, command and revoke, as it seemeth me good; and all this to be answered upon the heads of the rebellious, saith the Lord.
5 Wherefore, I revoke the commandment which was given unto my servants <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Thomas B. Marsh and Ezra Thayre, and give a new commandment unto my servant Thomas, that he shall take up his journey speedily to the land of Missouri, and my servant Selah J. Griffin shall also go with him.
6 For behold, I revoke the commandment which was given unto my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">servants Selah J. Griffin and Newel Knight, in consequence of the stiffneckedness of my people which are in Thompson, and their rebellions.
7 Wherefore, let my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">servant Newel Knight remain with them; and as many as will go may go, that are contrite before me, and be led by him to the land which I have appointed.
8 And again, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">verily I say unto you, that my servant Ezra Thayre must repent of his pride, and of his selfishness, and obey the former commandment which I have given him concerning the place upon which he lives.
9 And if he will do this, as there shall be no <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">divisions made upon the land, he shall be appointed still to go to the land of Missouri;
10 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.960">Otherwise he shall receive the money which he has paid, and shall leave the place, and shall be cut off out of my church, saith the Lord God of hosts;
11 And though the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.914">heaven and the earth pass away, these words shall not pass away, but shall be fulfilled.
12 And if my <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.963">servant Joseph Smith, Jun., must needs pay the money, behold, I, the Lord, will pay it unto him again in the land of Missouri, that those of whom he shall receive may be rewarded again according to that which they do;
13 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.876">according to that which they do they shall receive, even in lands for their inheritance.
14 <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.967">Behold, thus saith the Lord unto my people—you have many things to do and to repent of; for behold, your sins have come up unto me, and are not pardoned, because you seek to counsel in your own ways.
15 And your <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.913">hearts are not satisfied. And ye obey not the truth, but have pleasure in unrighteousness.
16 Wo unto you rich men, that will not give your substance to the poor, for your riches will canker your souls; and this shall be your lamentation in the day of visitation, and of judgment, and of indignation: The harvest is <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.973">past, the summer is ended, and my soul is not saved!
17 Wo unto <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.971">you poor men, whose hearts are not broken, whose spirits are not contrite, and whose bellies are not satisfied, and whose hands are not stayed from laying hold upon other men's goods, whose eyes are full of greediness, and who will not labor with your own hands!
18 But <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.969">blessed are the poor who are pure in heart, whose hearts are broken, and whose spirits are contrite, for they shall see the kingdom of God coming in power and great glory unto their deliverance; for the fatness of the earth shall be theirs.
19 For <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.947">behold, the Lord shall come, and his recompense shall be with him, and he shall reward every man, and the poor shall rejoice;
20 And their generations shall <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.941">inherit the earth from generation to generation, forever and ever. And now I make an end of speaking unto you. Even so. Amen.
