"And the day cometh that you shall hear my voice and see me, and know that I am." There are four Greek words which are translated as 'know': o ginosko - a knowledge grounded on personal experience o eido - to see with the mind's eye, signifies a clear and purely mental perception o epistamai- a knowledge obtained by proximity to the thing known, cf. our understanding o suniemi- implies a native insight, knowledge gained through the five senses
