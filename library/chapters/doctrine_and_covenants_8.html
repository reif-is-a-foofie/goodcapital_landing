Doctrine and Covenants

8

1 Oliver Cowdery, verily, verily, I <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">say unto you, that assuredly as the Lord liveth, who is your God and your Redeemer, even so surely shall you receive a knowledge of whatsoever things you shall ask in faith, with an honest heart, believing that you shall receive a knowledge concerning the engravings of old records, which are ancient, which contain those parts of my scripture of which has been spoken by the manifestation of my Spirit.
2 Yea, behold, I will tell <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">you in your mind and in your heart, by the Holy Ghost, which shall come upon you and which shall dwell in your heart.
3 Now, behold, this is the spirit of revelation; behold, this is the spirit by which Moses brought the <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">children of Israel through the Red Sea on dry ground.
4 Therefore this is thy <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">gift; apply unto it, and blessed art thou, for it shall deliver you out of the hands of your enemies, when, if it were not so, they would slay you and bring your soul to destruction.
5 Oh, remember these words, and keep my <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">commandments. Remember, this is your gift.
6 Now this is not all thy <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_11.000">gift; for you have another gift, which is the gift of Aaron; behold, it has told you many things;
7 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">Behold, there is no other power, save the power of God, that can cause this gift of Aaron to be with you.
8 Therefore, <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">doubt not, for it is the gift of God; and you shall hold it in your hands, and do marvelous works; and no power shall be able to take it away out of your hands, for it is the work of God.
9 And, therefore, whatsoever <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_341.000">you shall ask me to tell you by that means, that will I grant unto you, and you shall have knowledge concerning it.
10 Remember that without <span class="cw" data-tgt="phistory_of_churchhoc__vol_10.936">faith you can do nothing; therefore ask in faith. Trifle not with these things; do not ask for that which you ought not.
11 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.956">Ask that you may know the mysteries of God, and that you may translate and receive knowledge from all those ancient records which have been hid up, that are sacred; and according to your faith shall it be done unto you.
12 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pdonaldsondonaldson_on_moroni_10_340.925">Behold, it is I that have spoken it; and I am the same that spake unto you from the beginning. Amen.
