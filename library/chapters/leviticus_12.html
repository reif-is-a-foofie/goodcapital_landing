Leviticus

12

1 AND the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.594">LORD spake unto Moses, saying,
2 <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.867">Speak unto the children of Israel, saying, If a woman have conceived seed, and born a man child: then she shall be unclean seven days; according to the days of the separation for her infirmity shall she be unclean.
3 And in the <span class="cw" data-tgt="psefariaquoting_commentary__steinsaltz_on_leviticus_12_30.528">eighth day the flesh of his foreskin shall be circumcised.
4 And she shall then <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.834">continue in the blood of her purifying three and thirty days; she shall touch no hallowed thing, nor come into the sanctuary, until the days of her purifying be fulfilled.
5 But if she <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.809">bear a maid child, then she shall be unclean two weeks, as in her separation: and she shall continue in the blood of her purifying threescore and six days.
6 And when the days of her purifying are fulfilled, for a son, or for a daughter, she shall bring a <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.903">lamb of the first year for a burnt offering, and a young pigeon, or a turtledove, for a sin offering, unto the door of the tabernacle of the congregation, unto the priest:
7 Who shall offer it before the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18670.867">LORD, and make an atonement for her; and she shall be cleansed from the issue of her blood. This [is] the law for her that hath born a male or a female.
8 And if she be not able to bring a lamb, then she shall bring two turtles, or two young pigeons; the <span class="cw" data-tgt="pjstjst__inspired_version_18671.000">one for the burnt offering, and the other for a sin offering: and the priest shall make an atonement for her, and she shall be clean.
