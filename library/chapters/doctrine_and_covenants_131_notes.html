[May, 1S43.] Wednesday, 17th. Partook of break- fast at brother Perkins’; after which, we took a .pleasure-ride through Fountain Green. At ten, a.m., preached from 2nd Peter, 1st chapter, and showed that knowledge is powers and the man who has the most knowledge has the greatest power. Salvation means a man’s being placed beyond the power of all his enemies. The more sure word of prophecy means a man’s knowing that he is sealed up unto eternal life by revelation and the spirit of prophecy, through the power of the holy Priesthood. It is impossible for a man to be saved in ignorance.
