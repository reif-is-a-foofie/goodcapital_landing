Tim Ludwig

Managing Partner

Tim Ludwig is a Co-Founder and Managing Partner of Portrait Capital and the host of Transitions, a podcast that explores the pivotal moments in the lives of business leaders.

Tim is involved in all investing, portfolio management, and investor relations activities for Portrait.

Tim is also the founder of Ohana Capital, a private holding company through which he has invested in over 80 search funds since 2007 and which owns companies in the business services, healthcare, and transportation industries. Before Tim began investing in search funds, he was an executive at a real estate company, consulted with Arthur Andersen and a manufacturing company, founded two online publishing companies, and held positions with Dell and a venture-backed startup.

Tim is from Ann Arbor, graduated from The College of Wooster, received his MBA from the University of Michigan, and lives in San Diego.

Gaurav Shah

Partner and Head of Portfolio Operations

Gaurav Shah is a Partner and Head of Portfolio Operations at Portrait Capital.

Gaurav leads the firm's portfolio value creation efforts, drawing on his extensive experience in operations and management. Prior to joining Portrait, Gaurav led portfolio value creation programs at Partners Group, a leading global private equity firm, and was an Associate Partner at McKinsey, where he advised clients on performance improvement and growth strategies. Earlier in his career, Gaurav was an Area Project Manager at Fluor, overseeing large-scale construction projects.

Originally from India, Gaurav earned a Master's in Construction Management from Texas A&M University and an MBA from UC Berkeley's Haas School of Business. He currently lives in the Bay Area with his wife and two children.

Bruce Eskesen

Associate

Bruce Eskesen is an Associate at Portrait Capital involved in all aspects of investment origination and execution.

Bruce is originally from upstate New York, graduated summa cum laude from The Wharton School, and lives in New York City.
