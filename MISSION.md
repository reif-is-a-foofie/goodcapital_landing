## What We Are Building

A library where a serious reader can start at any verse, any name, any place —
and reach the best connected material in the world on that subject without losing
their thread or their place.

The library gathers:

- **Scripture** — the full LDS canon: Old Testament, New Testament, Book of Mormon,
  Doctrine and Covenants, Pearl of Great Price
- **Commentary** — General Conference talks, apostolic writings, doctrinal expositions
- **Scholarship** — biblical scholarship, ancient texts, early Christian sources,
  Near Eastern studies, Restoration-era documents
- **The noun world** — every person, place, and thing that appears across these
  sources, each with its own profile, connections, and documentary trail

---

## The Standard

Every quote stays attached to the talk or book it came from.
Every cross-reference shows which text made the connection.
Every figure links to their appearances across the whole canon.

The reader should always know:
- What kind of text they are reading
- Where it came from
- What connects to it and why

---

## The Scope

The collection grows outward from the LDS core canon and has no hard boundary.
Any text that illuminates scripture, doctrine, or the history of the Restoration belongs.

The long-term scope is what Joseph Smith described: every true principle, gathered.
